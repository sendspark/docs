title: "Mac app blocked by Apple security check"
description: "Learn how to fix the Sendspark.app malicious software error by adjusting your Mac's Privacy & Security settings for safe app launch."
---

Have you had this message when trying to open our desktop app?

No worries. It's possible your level of security has been set to a high standard. Apple usually shows this message when an App was not downloaded from the App Store

Simply go to your settings, search for "Privacy & Security" and then to the right, scroll down until you find the Security section. Be sure to switch to the "App Store and Identified developers"

![Mac Privacy and Security settings showing App Store and Identified developers option](/images/9460326/e5a0ad5f-image.png)

You can now try again!

Happy recordings!
