To embed a Sendspark video on any web page or blog, open the share modal from Your Library or the video page editor, click into the Embed video section, copy the Website Embed code, and paste it into your site's HTML. It works with builders like WordPress, Wix, and Webflow.

## Key takeaways

- You can embed Sendspark videos into web pages or blog posts on custom HTML sites or builders like WordPress, Wix, and Webflow.
- Open the share modal from Your Library or from the purple "Share Video" button in the video page editor.
- Copy the code under "Website Embed" in the Embed video section of the share modal.
- On a website builder, you may need to create an HTML section first, then paste the embed code there.

Whether you're creating your own HTML website, or using a website builder like Wordpress, Wix, or Webflow, you can easily embed Sendspark videos into your web pages or blog posts.

## How to Embed Sendspark Videos on Web Pages

## 1. Launch the Sendspark share modal

You can access your share options in two places:

- From [Your Library](https://sendspark.com/my-library), find the video you want to embed and click on **"Share"**

![Library page](/images/4984944/22165676-image.png)

- Also, from the video page editor, you can click on the purple **"Share Video"** button

![Share Video](/images/4984944/2267b44e-image.png)

## 2. Copy the Website Embed Code to your clipboard

- Click into the **Embed video**section of the share modal
- Under **Website Embed,** click **"Copy Embed Code"**

![Embed tab on share modal](/images/4984944/93527d39-image.png)

## 3. Paste into the HTML of your website

- If you are building a custom website, you can paste this code snippet right into your HTML.
- If you are using a website builder (i.e. Wordpress, Webflow, Hubspot, Shopify, etc.) then you might need to create an HTML section first. Then, you can paste your video in there.
  - This will look differently on different website builders, but here is a screenshot of how it looks in Hubspot:

![Insert Video Embed Code into Webpage](/images/4984944/c7f4f6b3-image.png)
