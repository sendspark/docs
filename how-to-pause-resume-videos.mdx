## How to Pause Recording

To pause your video while recording, simply click the **pause** button on the recording toolbar:

![Pause Recording](/images/6891756/c381caf7-image.png)

You'll know that your video is paused when you see this paused when you see this modal pop up on your screen.

![Recording is Paused](/images/6891756/68366b33-image.png)

## How to Resume Recording

To resume recording, simply click the same pause button,  anywhere on the screen, or any key. This will let you pick up where you left off!

Happy recording!

## Use Hotkeys

Want to pause and resume the recording without the viewer seeing what you're doing?

Use these shortcuts:  **Alt (ALT) + Shift (⇧) + R**
