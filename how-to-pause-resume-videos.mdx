To pause a recording, click the **pause** button on the recording toolbar, and a "paused" modal confirms it. To resume, click the pause button again, click anywhere on screen, or press any key. You can also pause and resume with the hotkey **Alt + Shift + R** so viewers never see you doing it.

## Key takeaways

- Pause by clicking the **pause** button on the recording toolbar; a paused modal appears to confirm the recording is stopped.
- Resume by clicking the same pause button, clicking anywhere on the screen, or pressing any key.
- Use the hotkey **Alt (ALT) + Shift (⇧) + R** to pause and resume without the viewer seeing what you're doing.

## How to Pause Recording

To pause your video while recording, simply click the **pause** button on the recording toolbar:

## How to Resume Recording

To resume recording, simply click the same pause button,  anywhere on the screen, or any key. This will let you pick up where you left off!

Happy recording!

## Use Hotkeys

Want to pause and resume the recording without the viewer seeing what you're doing?

Use these shortcuts:  **Alt (ALT) + Shift (⇧) + R**
