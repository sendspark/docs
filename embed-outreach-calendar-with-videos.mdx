Sending videos in your Outreach prospecting sequences? See how to embed your calendar right on the video page, so prospects can easily book meetings!

![Sendspark video page with meeting-booking calendar embedded beside the video](/images/9052044/bf143e3d-image.png)

## How to Embed Outreach Calendar Link Next To Videos

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/SfmuHgjodLg?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Under your Sendspark video, toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**
2. Select **"Outreach"** from the Provider Dropdown List
3. **Add the link**to the Outreach calendar you want to use

![Select Outreach Calendar Option](/images/9052044/93429680-image.png)

You should be able to see your embedded Outreach calendar in the preview area!

![Preview Outreach Embedded Calendar Next to Video](/images/9052044/7247c15f-image.png)
