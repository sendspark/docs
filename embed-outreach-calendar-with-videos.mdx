To embed an Outreach calendar next to your Sendspark video, toggle **Calendar** to **On** under the video, select **Outreach** from the provider dropdown, and paste the link to your Outreach calendar. Your embedded calendar then appears in the preview area, so prospects can book a meeting without leaving the video page.

## Key takeaways

- Turn on the **Calendar** toggle under your Sendspark video and pick **Outreach** as the provider.
- Add the **link** to your Outreach calendar (use the calendar link, not the embed code, Sendspark embeds it for you).
- The embedded calendar shows in the preview area and lets prospects book meetings directly on the video page.
- Embedded calendars work everywhere you customize video pages: video messages, dynamic video campaigns, and templates. See [How to Embed Calendars with Videos](/how-to-embed-calendars-with-videos) for the full list of supported platforms.

Sending videos in your Outreach prospecting sequences? See how to embed your calendar right on the video page, so prospects can easily book meetings!

## How to Embed Outreach Calendar Link Next To Videos

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/SfmuHgjodLg?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Under your Sendspark video, toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**
2. Select **"Outreach"** from the Provider Dropdown List
3. **Add the link**to the Outreach calendar you want to use

![Select Outreach Calendar Option](/images/9052044/93429680-image.png)

You should be able to see your embedded Outreach calendar in the preview area!

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I paste the Outreach embed code or the calendar link?">
    Paste the calendar link, not the embed code. Sendspark automatically embeds your calendar using the information from the link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where does the embedded calendar appear for my prospect?">
    Right on the video page, next to the video. Prospects can watch your video and book a meeting on your Outreach calendar without ever leaving the page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use an embedded calendar on dynamic video campaigns too?">
    Yes. Embedded calendars are supported everywhere you can customize video pages in Sendspark, including individual video messages, dynamic video campaigns, and templates. The process is the same in each place.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I save my Outreach calendar so I don't add it every time?">
    Yes. Save the embedded calendar in a Template, then apply that template to new videos, so you don't have to paste your calendar link for every recording. See [How to Embed Calendars with Videos](/how-to-embed-calendars-with-videos) for details.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
