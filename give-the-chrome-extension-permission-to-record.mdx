---
title: "Give the Chrome Extension Permission to Record"
description: "Learn how to give Chrome extension permission to record by enabling camera and microphone access in your browser settings."
---

## 1. Paste this link into your browser to access your Chrome settings:

chrome://settings/content/siteDetails?site=chrome-extension%3A%2F%2Fblimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph%2F&search=camera

👆 Make sure you're pasting, not clicking!

## 2. Set both (A) **Camera** and (B) **Microphone** to "Allow"

![Allow Screen Recording](/images/4651449/128c9adc-image.png)

## 3. Click on the Sendspark icon in your browser to start recording

![Sendspark Logo](/images/4651449/6ec3588c-image.png)
