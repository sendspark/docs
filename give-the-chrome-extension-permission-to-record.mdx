To let the Sendspark Chrome extension record, open its camera/microphone settings by pasting the extension link into your browser (don't click it), set both **Camera** and **Microphone** to "Allow," then click the Sendspark icon to start recording.

## Key takeaways

- Paste the settings link into your address bar rather than clicking it, then set **Camera** and **Microphone** to "Allow."
- After granting permission, click the Sendspark icon in your browser to start recording.
- If your camera or microphone still isn't detected, check that no other app is controlling it and that the correct device is selected.

## 1. Paste this link into your browser to access your Chrome settings:

chrome://settings/content/siteDetails?site=chrome-extension%3A%2F%2Fblimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph%2F&search=camera

👆 Make sure you're pasting, not clicking!

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why do I need to paste the link instead of clicking it?">
    Chrome's camera and microphone settings page opens correctly when the link is pasted into the address bar. The same instruction applies when granting permissions to sendspark.com itself.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The extension still can't detect my camera or microphone. What should I do?">
    Make sure no other application (such as OBS or Zoom) is controlling your camera or mic, connect the device directly to your computer rather than through a hub, and select the correct device in the extension's Settings. See [Camera or Microphone not detected / No Inputs](/camera-or-microphone-not-detected-no-inputs).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I also need to give permission to the Sendspark website?">
    Yes. If you record from sendspark.com as well as the extension, set Camera and Microphone to "Allow" for the site too, then refresh and try again. See [Why Isn't My Camera Working?](/why-isn-t-my-camera-working).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
