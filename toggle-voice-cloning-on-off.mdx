---
title: "Toggle Voice Cloning On/Off"
description: "Learn how to enable or disable Sendspark voice cloning to personalize AI-generated video narration for your sales and marketing outreach."
---

In this tutorial, you'll learn how to toggle voice cloning on or off.

Go to the Dynamic Videos section on the left-hand side nav bar and then click on the Create Dynamic Video button

![Dynamic Videos page with the Create Dynamic Video button highlighted](/images/14199753/692531e3-image.png)

Give your Campaign a name:

![Create Campaign dialog with a field to name your campaign](/images/14199753/a94e6822-image.png)

And now you'll notice that "name personalization", which is the same thing as voice cloning, is ON by default. So nothing else is needed. But you can always turn it off if you decide to only generate videos with a dynamic background, but no AI voice cloning.

![Dynamic Video Guidelines with Name Personalization toggle set to ON](/images/14199753/1d2acb26-image.png)

That's all!
