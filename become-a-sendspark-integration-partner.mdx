Building a Sendspark integration lets your customers add scaled video outreach without building it themselves, and you can earn recurring revenue through the affiliate program. To get started, review the API docs, create a partner account, join the affiliate program, and let the team know so they can enable API access.

## Key takeaways

- Any email sequencing, LinkedIn automation, or other GTM platform can build a Sendspark integration.
- Earn recurring revenue by joining the [affiliate program](https://sendspark.firstpromoter.com).
- To start: review the API docs, create a partner account (tell the team so they can grant API access), and join the affiliate program; a shared Slack channel is available for technical support.
- Co-marketing scales across three tiers by mutual customers: Tier 1 (built + 3+), Tier 2 (15+), and Tier 3 (25+).

## Why Build a Sendspark Integration?

Whether you have an email sequencing platform, LinkedIn automation platform, or other type of GTM platform, Sendspark can help your customers get better results.

You can build a Sendspark integration to give your customers the great results of scaled video outreach, without having to build everything yourself.

And, you can make recurring revenue! Join our[affiliate program](https://sendspark.firstpromoter.com) to make money from customers you refer to Sendspark, recurring indefinitely. 💰

## Co-Marketing Opportunities

This is generally how we think about co-marketing with integration partners. However, each relationship is different. Let us know if you have questions!

### Note: Tiers Are Reference Points Only.

We will not be able to co-market with you if there are conflicts with existing partners, or if we don't believe your integration will help our customers.

Please contact us before building if you have any doubts.

## Tier 1:

**Qualification Criteria:**

- You’ve built an integration!
- 3+ mutual customers use it

**What you get:**

- Guide in Sendspark Help Center
- Social posts on LinkedIn & X announcing integration
- Small feature in our next newsletter

## Tier 2

**Qualification Criteria:**

- 15+ mutual customers use integration

**What you get:**

- Integration Page on Sendspark Website
- Dedicated blog post highlighting use cases of the integration
- YouTube video highlighting the integration + use cases

## Tier 3

**Qualification Criteria:**

- 25+ mutual customers use integration

**What you get:**

- Dedicated newsletter to Sendspark customers highlighting integrations
- Live webinar for Sendspark + your customers
