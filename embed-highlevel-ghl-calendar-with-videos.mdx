Sendspark makes it easy to embed HighLevel calendars next to videos, so your viewers can schedule a meeting with you without ever leaving your video page.

To embed a HighLevel (GoHighLevel) calendar, create a calendar in HighLevel, open **Share**, and copy its **permanent link** — not the embed code. In Sendspark, open the video, toggle **Calendar** to **On**, set the provider to **HighLevel**, and paste the permanent link. The calendar appears in the preview and viewers can book right from your video page.

## Key takeaways

- Use the HighLevel **permanent link** (from the calendar's **Share** menu), not the embed code, when connecting it to Sendspark.
- In Sendspark, toggle **Calendar** to **On**, choose **HighLevel** as the provider, and paste the link.
- You can attach a HighLevel **form** to the calendar to collect extra details (first name, last name, email, phone) when someone books.
- If you rebuild or edit the calendar, grab the permanent link again in case it changed, then re-paste it in Sendspark. You can save the video setup to reuse it later.

## How to embed a HighLevel calendar with your video

1. In Sendspark, click a video to open its edit/share page, where you set up everything around the video.
2. In your HighLevel account, go to **Calendars → Calendar settings** and click **Create calendar**. A **Simple calendar** is the easiest option — name it and save.
3. Open the calendar's **Share** menu and copy the **Permanent link** (not the embed code and not the other link options).
4. Back in Sendspark, toggle **Calendar** to **On**, set the provider to **HighLevel**, and paste the permanent link. Give it a moment to load in the preview.
5. (Optional) Save the video setup so you can reuse this configuration later.

## How to collect extra details with a form (optional)

1. In HighLevel, go to **Sites → Forms** and create a new form (**Add form → start from scratch**).
2. In the form builder, edit the fields you want to collect (for example first name, last name, email, phone), name the form, and save.
3. Return to **Calendars → Calendar settings**, open your calendar, and click **Edit**.
4. Open the **Forms and payment** tab and use the dropdown to select the form you just created, then **Save**.
5. Grab the calendar's **permanent link** again (in case it changed) and re-paste it in Sendspark with the provider set to **HighLevel**. Now the booking flow includes the extra step that collects your prospect's information.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which HighLevel link do I use — the embed code or the permanent link?">
    Use the permanent link. In the calendar's Share menu you'll see several options; the permanent link is the one that works in Sendspark. Do not use the embed code.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I collect information when someone books a meeting?">
    Yes. Build a form in HighLevel under Sites → Forms with the fields you want (first name, last name, email, phone, or anything else), then attach it to your calendar under Calendar settings → Forms and payment. The booking flow will then include an extra step to gather that information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I edited my calendar and it stopped loading. What should I do?">
    Editing or rebuilding the calendar can change its permanent link. Open the calendar's Share menu, copy the permanent link again, and re-paste it into Sendspark with the provider set to HighLevel.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to set this up every time?">
    No. You can save the video setup in Sendspark and reuse the same calendar configuration whenever you want.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
