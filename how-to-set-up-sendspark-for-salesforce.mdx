To connect Sendspark with Salesforce, open your [Sendspark integrations tab](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations), click **Connect** next to Salesforce, enter your Salesforce **Organization ID**, and save. This sends Sendspark video-page visits to Salesforce so you can track engagement across leads, contacts, and user records and visualize it in reports and dashboards. The integration is available on every plan.

## Key takeaways

- The integration sends Sendspark video-page visits to Salesforce so you can track engagement with leads, contacts, and user records and visualize it in reports and dashboards.
- It's available on **every Sendspark plan**; you also need an active Salesforce Experience account with access to your business unit ID.
- You connect the tools from the [Sendspark integrations tab](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations) by clicking **Connect** next to Salesforce and entering your Salesforce Organization ID.
- Once saved, Salesforce shows as enabled with a checkmark. The video below shows where to find your Salesforce Organization ID.

The Sendspark for Salesforce integration allows you to measure the impact video is having on your business.

This integration will allow you to send Sendspark page visits to Salesforce to track engagement with individual leads, contacts, and user records, and visualize video engagement data in reports and dashboards.

## Requirements

- An active Sendspark account (the Sendspark for Salesforce integration is available on every plan)
- An active Salesforce Experience account with access to your [business unit ID](#h_197a0c7066)

## Integrate Sendspark with Salesforce

1. Go to your [Sendspark integrations tab](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations)
2. Next to Salesforce, click **Connect** ​![Sendspark integrations tab with Connect button next to Salesforce](/images/5970577/bebac7f5-image.png)
3. Enter your Salesforce**Organization ID:**![Sendspark dialog box prompting user to enter Salesforce Organization ID](/images/5970577/141a1237-image.png)
4. **Save** changes
5. You should see that Salesforce is **enabled** now 🎉 ​![Sendspark integrations tab showing Salesforce integration enabled with checkmark](/images/5970577/4279024e-image.png)
