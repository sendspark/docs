---
title: "Set Global Styles"
description: "Configure custom brand styles and logos for your Sendspark video messages to create a polished, professional experience for viewers."
---

Sendspark lets you add your own brand styles on video messages and requests to deliver a beautiful, polished experience.

---

1. Click here to access your: **[workspace settings](https://sendspark.com/settings/workspace)**. Or navigate through your dashboard by clicking on the bottom left-hand side logo, then click **My Account**:

![My account menu section](/images/5548680/05960a8f-image.png)

2. Click **Manage Workspace** and then on **Set Global Styles**

![Manage workspace modal](/images/5548680/33c64232-image.png)

3. Use the side bar on the left to add your own logo and brand styles. You can preview how your video pages will look on the right.

When you click a field on the left, you'll see the element you're editing highlighted on the right.

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/4WLFbbzE5Jg?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

4. Save changes, and you're all set!

![Save Global Styles modal](/images/5548680/da216fd1-image.png)
