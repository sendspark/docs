Set Global Styles lets you apply your own logo and brand styles across your Sendspark video messages and requests. Open your workspace settings (or go to My Account from the dashboard), click Manage Workspace, then Set Global Styles. Use the left sidebar to add your logo and styles, preview on the right, and save.

## Key takeaways

- Global Styles apply your logo and brand styles to Sendspark video messages and requests for a polished, professional look.
- Access them from your workspace settings, or via the dashboard by clicking the bottom-left logo, then My Account.
- Click **Manage Workspace**, then **Set Global Styles** to open the editor.
- Edit fields in the left sidebar and preview changes on the right; clicking a field highlights the element you're editing. Save to apply.

---

1. Click here to access your: **[workspace settings](https://sendspark.com/settings/workspace)**. Or navigate through your dashboard by clicking on the bottom left-hand side logo, then click **My Account**:

![My account menu section](/images/5548680/05960a8f-image.png)

2. Click **Manage Workspace** and then on **Set Global Styles**

![Manage workspace modal](/images/5548680/33c64232-image.png)

3. Use the side bar on the left to add your own logo and brand styles. You can preview how your video pages will look on the right.

When you click a field on the left, you'll see the element you're editing highlighted on the right.

4. Save changes, and you're all set!
