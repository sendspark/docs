To start a free Sendspark trial, visit [auth.sendspark.com/sign-up](https://auth.sendspark.com/sign-up) and create your account. No credit card is required. Your trial gives you 7 days of full access to every Sendspark feature, with the only limit being 5 Dynamic Video Minutes.

## Key takeaways

- Create your account at [auth.sendspark.com/sign-up](https://auth.sendspark.com/sign-up); no credit card or payment method is required to start.
- The trial lasts **7 days** and unlocks **every Sendspark feature** with no feature restrictions. It's not a trial of one specific plan.
- The only limitation during the trial is **5 Dynamic Video Minutes**. Recording ~10-second videos helps you personalize for more recipients.
- You'll only be asked for payment if your 7-day trial expires and you sign back in, or if you choose to upgrade early from the Billing page.

## ✨ Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit the signup page below and complete the registration process:

🔗 [https://auth.sendspark.com/sign-up](https://auth.sendspark.com/sign-up)

Once your account is created, you'll be ready to start exploring Sendspark immediately.

## 🎁 What's Included?

Your free trial includes:

- ✅ **7 days** of full access
- ✅ **Every Sendspark feature** unlocked
- ✅ No feature restrictions

**Important:** This isn't a trial of a specific plan. During your 7-day trial, you'll have access to **all of Sendspark's features**.

The only limitation is:

🎥 **5 Dynamic Video Minutes**

💡 **Pro Tip:** We recommend recording a video that's **around 10 seconds long** for your first email campaign. This helps you personalize videos for more recipients while making the most of your available Dynamic Video Minutes.

---

## 💳 Do I Need a Credit Card?

🚫 **No credit card or payment method is required** to start your free trial.

You'll only be asked to add a payment method in either of these situations:

## 1️⃣ Your Trial Has Ended

If your **7-day trial expires** and you try to sign back in, you'll be prompted to choose a paid plan before continuing.

## 2️⃣ You Upgrade Early

If you decide to upgrade before your trial ends, simply visit the **Billing** page and choose the plan you'd like to purchase. You'll be asked to enter your payment information at that time.

---

## 🎉 You're Ready to Go!

That's it! Create your account, explore every Sendspark feature, and start creating personalized videos—all without entering a payment method upfront.
