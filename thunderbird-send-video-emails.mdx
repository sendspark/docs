To send a video email in Thunderbird, record your video in Sendspark, click Share Video, then under Share click Copy Linked GIF and paste it into your Thunderbird email draft. Recipients see a clickable preview in their inbox and can click it to watch the full video in their browser.

## Key takeaways

- Send videos in Thunderbird with Copy Linked GIF: record in Sendspark, click Share Video, click Copy Linked GIF, and paste into your Thunderbird draft.
- Recipients see a preview in their inbox and can click to watch the full video in their browser.
- Thunderbird can't embed videos that play in-email because that requires editing the email's HTML; email marketing platforms like Mailchimp, HubSpot, and ActiveCampaign support that instead.
- Installing the Sendspark Chrome Extension is suggested for a better recording experience.

## How to Send Videos in Thunderbird

1. Record your video in Sendspark. (We suggest installing our [Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph?hl=en-US) for a better recording experience). ​
2. Click **Share Video** ​![Share Videos in Thunderbird](/images/5777490/56a7d70e-image.png)
3. Under **Share**, click **Copy Linked GIF** ​![Share modal](/images/5777490/1bf039d0-image.png)
4. Paste in your email draft in Thunderbird ​![Paste Video in Thunderbird](/images/5777490/e5128b23-image.png)

## Commonly Asked Questions about Sending Videos in Thunderbird

## How do videos sent through Thunderbird render for recipients?

Your recipients will see a preview in their inbox, and they will be able to click to watch the full video in their browser.

## Can I embed videos in Thunderbird so they play in-email?

No. To embed videos so they play in-email, you need to be able to edit the HTML of your email. Many email marketing platforms (like [Mailchimp](/mailchimp-send-video-emails), [Hubspot](/hubspot-static-videos-marketing-hub), and [ActiveCampaign](/activecampaign-send-personalized-videos-at-scale)) let you do this.

Check out our resources for [embedding videos in email using an email marketing platform](https://help.sendspark.com/en/collections/1957874-share-your-video#email-automation-platforms) if you'd like to do this!
