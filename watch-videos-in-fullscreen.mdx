---
title: "Watch Videos in Fullscreen"
description: "Learn how to watch Sendspark videos in fullscreen mode by clicking the fullscreen icon in the video player for better viewing."
---

To watch Sendspark videos in fullscreen, follow these steps:

- Open your video on a video share page by either clicking on the **preview icon** or pasting the **video** **share URL** into your browser ![Preview Share Page button](/images/5566677/1d633f53-image.png)
- Start playing your video
- Click the **fullscreen icon** on the bottom right of the video player
