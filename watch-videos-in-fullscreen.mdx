To watch a Sendspark video in fullscreen, open it on its video share page, start playing it, then click the **fullscreen icon** in the bottom right of the video player.

## Key takeaways

- Open the video on its share page, either from the **preview icon** or by pasting the video **share URL** into your browser.
- Start playing the video, then click the **fullscreen icon** in the bottom right of the player.

To watch Sendspark videos in fullscreen, follow these steps:

- Open your video on a video share page by either clicking on the **preview icon** or pasting the **video** **share URL** into your browser ![Preview Share Page button](/images/5566677/1d633f53-image.png)
- Start playing your video
- Click the **fullscreen icon** on the bottom right of the video player
