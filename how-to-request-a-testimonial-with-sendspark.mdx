Sendspark's Request Video makes it easy to request testimonials from your customers.

## How to Request a Testimonial with Sendspark

1. Navigate to the Sendspark [Video Requests](https://sendspark.com/requested-videos) folder ​
2. Click **Copy Request Link** ​![Copy Request Link](/images/5951428/508fb5af-image.png)
3. Now, simply share the link with your customers. Here are [some common ways to get video testimonials](https://blog.sendspark.com/get-video-testimonials).
4. Once your recipients open the link, this is what they'll see: ![Video request modal for recipient](/images/5951428/9ec17a02-image.png)
5. Regardless of whether they have a Sendspark account or not, they will be able to share a recording through any of these three options: ​![Record options for video request ](/images/5951428/5d4df953-image.png)
6. Recipients will have a chance to **review and approve** the video before submitting it. (Try it out by [recording a video for us here](https://sendspark.com/request/Sendspark/ywpsoec6egdg2yvg8r9sd9payzm0svhg)!) ​![Video approval](/images/5951428/2eed39d1-image.png)
7. When viewers submit the video, it will **automatically upload** to your [Video Requests folder](https://sendspark.com/requested-videos) for you to view, download, or share. ​![Requested video sent](/images/5951428/1ee54512-image.png)

## 💡 Bonus tip: Help your customers feel more comfortable recording videos

Use Sendspark to [record yourself](/record-yourself) asking customers to make you a testimonial to help them feel more comfortable (and prompt them with [customer testimonial questions](https://blog.sendspark.com/video-testimonial-questions)).

If you'd like to do this, just add your video request link to the [call-to-action below your video](/use-a-call-to-action-url-link).
