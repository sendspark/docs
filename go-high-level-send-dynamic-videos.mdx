📹 How to Send Dynamic Videos Through GoHighLevel

Hey there 👋 Dan here from Tech Support!  
In this guide, I’ll walk you through how to send **dynamic videos** using Sendspark + GoHighLevel (via Lead Connector). 🚀

---

## 🔑 Step 1: Log in and Select Your Account

1. Log in to your **GoHighLevel** account.
2. If you manage multiple accounts, pick the one you’re working on ✅.

---

## 👥 Step 2: Upload Your Contacts

1. Head over to **Contacts** in the left menu.
2. You can add contacts manually ✍️, but the best option is to **upload a CSV file**.
3. Map your fields (First Name, Last Name, Job Title, Website 🌐, etc.).
  - Missing a field? Add a note or skip it.
4. Select **Create a list from the contacts** and give it a name (e.g., *First List*).
5. Click **Submit** ✔️.

⚡ For small lists, the upload finishes almost instantly!

---

## 📝 Step 3: Create a Template (Optional but Recommended)

1. Go to **Marketing → Templates**.
2. Select **Blank Template** 📄.
3. Name it something simple (e.g., *New List Template*).
4. Open your **Dynamic Video Campaign in Sendspark**.
5. Click **Share Campaign → Lead Connector (GoHighLevel)**.
6. Copy the code snippet 📋.
7. Back in GoHighLevel, add a **Code element** to your template.
8. Paste the snippet and hit **Save** 💾.

🎉 You’ll see the video **thumbnail/cover** show up.

ℹ️ Note: GoHighLevel won’t yet link dynamic contact data at this stage—this is just the template setup.

---

## 📤 Step 4: Create and Send Your Campaign

1. Go to **Marketing → Campaigns**.
2. Click **Create Campaign** ➕.
3. Choose your saved template (e.g., *New List Template*).
4. Fill out the email details:
  - **From email**: `dan@sendspark.com`
  - **Subject**: `Dynamic Videos 101`
5. Under recipients, choose **Smart List** 📋 → select the list you uploaded earlier.
6. Click **Send** ✉️.

---

## ⏳ Step 5: Wait for Delivery

- GoHighLevel may take a few moments to process ⏱️, depending on list size.
- Once complete, your **dynamic video emails are live**! 🎬

---

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Tips

- ❌ Don’t see the thumbnail? → Make sure you pasted the Sendspark snippet into a **Code element**.
- 📂 List missing? → Double-check your CSV import under **Contacts → Lists**.
- 🕒 Sending slow? → Larger campaigns take longer as GoHighLevel runs background checks.

---

## ✅ Summary

That’s it! 🎉 You’re now ready to send dynamic video campaigns from GoHighLevel using Sendspark.

If you have questions, don’t hesitate to reach out—we’re here to help 💬.
