## How to Send Dynamic Videos Without a Partner Integration

## Why This Is Needed

Usually, if you want to send personalized (dynamic) videos in bulk, you need an email automation tool or CRM integration. These tools allow you to:

- Send emails to large contact lists.
- Use **HTML formatting** (so the video preview and link display correctly).
- Track engagement at scale.

That’s why many Sendspark customers connect to platforms like HubSpot, Salesforce, or Mailchimp.

But if you don’t use one of our partner integrations, we’ve built a solution for you: bulk sending directly from **Gmail** or **Outlook**.
