To send dynamic Sendspark videos through Salesforce, embed them in your email campaigns using two links from your Sendspark campaign modal: a thumbnail link you insert as a web image, and a Salesforce Video link you attach to that thumbnail. Each recipient automatically sees their personalized version when they click.

## Key takeaways

- You need Salesforce email access, an active Sendspark account with Dynamic video campaigns, and at least one Salesforce contact.
- The process uses two links from the Sendspark campaign modal: the thumbnail link and the Salesforce Video link.
- When you preview or test the email you'll see the original background video, but each recipient sees their personalized version on click.
- The system automatically selects the right personalized background for each contact based on their Salesforce record data.

This guide will walk you through the process of sharing your Sendspark Dynamic videos through Salesforce by embedding them in your email campaigns. Dynamic videos automatically personalize content for each recipient while maintaining a professional appearance.

## Prerequisites

- Access to Salesforce with email permissions
- An active Sendspark account with Dynamic video campaigns
- At least one contact in your Salesforce database

## Step 1: Set Up Your Salesforce Email

1. Navigate to your **Contacts** section in Salesforce
2. Click on **Send List Email**
3. Select your desired recipients for the campaign

## Step 2: Add Your Dynamic Video Thumbnail

1. Go to your **Dynamic Videos** campaign in Sendspark
2. Click **Share Campaign**
3. In the modal that appears, type "Salesforce" to access the integration options
4. Copy the provided thumbnail link
5. Return to your Salesforce email composer
6. Click the **Image** icon in the email editor
7. Select **Web Image**
8. Paste the thumbnail link and click **Insert**

## Step 3: Link Your Dynamic Video

1. Go back to your Sendspark campaign modal
2. Click on **Salesforce Video** (this is the second link you'll need)
3. Copy this video link
4. In your Salesforce email, single-click on the video thumbnail you just inserted
5. When the thumbnail shows a selection outline, click the **Link** option
6. Paste the video link URL in the link field
7. Click **Save**

## Step 4: Send Your Email

1. Add a subject line to your email
2. Review your email content
3. Click **Send** to deliver your Dynamic video campaign

**Note:** When you preview or test the email, you'll see the original background video used in your campaign setup. However, each recipient will automatically see their personalized version when they click the video thumbnail.

**Tip:** The system automatically selects the appropriate personalized background for each contact based on the data associated with their Salesforce record, ensuring each recipient gets a tailored video experience.
