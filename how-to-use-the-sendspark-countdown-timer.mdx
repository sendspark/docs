Sendspark offers countdown timers for both the Chrome extension and the web application. In the Chrome extension, open the Settings icon and toggle the Countdown option for camera and screen recordings. In the web app, click the countdown button in the bottom-left of the camera recorder to toggle it, and you can skip the countdown on screen recordings.

## Key takeaways

- Countdown timers are available in both the Chrome extension and the web app.
- In the Chrome extension, open the **Settings** icon and toggle **Countdown** for camera and screen recording.
- In the web app camera recorder, click the countdown timer button in the bottom left to toggle it on/off.
- Web app screen recording has no on/off toggle, but you can **skip** the countdown to start recording sooner.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I turn the countdown on or off in the Chrome extension?">
    Launch the Sendspark Chrome extension, click the Settings icon, and toggle the Countdown option on or off. This applies to both camera and screen recording.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I toggle the countdown in the web app?">
    Select Camera recording to launch the recorder, then click the countdown timer button on the bottom left of the video player to toggle it on or off.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I toggle the countdown for screen recording in the web app?">
    No. There's no on/off toggle for the countdown timer on web app screen recording, but you can skip the countdown to start recording sooner.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does the countdown work for both camera and screen recording?">
    In the Chrome extension, the Countdown toggle covers both camera and screen recording. In the web app, you can toggle it for camera recording and skip it for screen recording.
  </Accordion>
