Sendspark has **countdown timers** for both the Chrome extension and web application. Here is how to use both:

## Countdown in Chrome Extension

## Camera & Screen Recording

1. **Launch** the Sendspark Chrome Extension
2. Click on the **Settings** icon
3. Toggle the Countdown option on/off

![Sendspark extension with cursor on the Settings icon to toggle countdown](/images/5963256/e72c9bf9-count.gif)

## Web Application

## Camera Recording

1. **Select Camera recording** to launch the recorder ​![Web App Recording](/images/5963256/a81d0efc-image.png) ​
2. **Click the countdown timer**on the bottom left of the video player to toggle it on/off ​![Countdown button](/images/5963256/30cd57d9-image.png)

## Screen Recording

1. There is no way to toggle on/off the countdown timer for screen recording, but you can **skip countdown**to start recording sooner — so you don't have to wait! ​![Cancel Recording button](/images/5963256/2986e595-image.png)
