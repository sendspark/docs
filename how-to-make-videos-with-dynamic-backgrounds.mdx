A dynamic background lets one recorded video show a different background for each viewer, such as their website or LinkedIn page, without re-recording. In Sendspark, create a Dynamic Video campaign, add your camera-bubble video, optionally customize the share page, then add each prospect's name and URL and click **Generate Dynamic Video**.

## Key takeaways

- A dynamic background is a video background that personalizes for each viewer (for example, their website), so you record once instead of re-recording per prospect.
- To build one, create a Dynamic Video campaign, add the Sendspark share URL for your camera-bubble video, then generate videos per prospect using their name and URL.
- Customizing the share page (header, message, call-to-action) is optional but can lift conversion, and supports dynamic variables and your own branding.
- Share videos 1-on-1 with **Copy Video Link** or **Copy GIF + Video Link**, or at scale via **Share Campaign** and an email automation platform.

## What is a dynamic background in a video?

A dynamic element in a video is something that can be changed for each viewer. A dynamic background means that the background can be personalized for each viewer. For example, you can show your viewer's website or LinkedIn page without re-recording a video specifically for them.

![Example of a video with personalized background showing viewers website behind speaker](/images/7192095/9cc853c4-sendspark-sneak_peek-resized.gif)

## Why use dynamic video backgrounds?

Using dynamic video backgrounds can save you A TON of time if you were planning to record the same video over multiple website pages. Instead of recording multiple videos, you can record just one video, and then easily swap out the background for different viewers.

This is particularly useful for...

- **Prospecting**— add a video of yourself over your prospect's website
- **Demos** — explain how your solution can help, over the right URL
- **Walking through proposals**— explain the general agreement, and show the specific terms of your viewer

And more! There are infinite ways to use dynamic videos in your sales communication to scale yourself and connect with prospects.

## How to make videos with dynamic backgrounds

Follow the steps below, or watch this video:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/JzGHXIJLgCk?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. From your [Dynamic Video library](https://sendspark.com/dynamic-videos) in Sendspark, click "Create Dynamic Video".
2. Give your campaign a title: ​![Campaign title input field in Sendspark dynamic video creation form](/images/7192095/bd855747-image.png)
3. **Add the Sendspark share URL for your camera bubble video.** This is the part of your video that will stay consistent across all of your dynamic videos. You can [record with Sendspark](/record-yourself), or [upload an existing video](/upload-a-video-to-sendspark). ​![Sendspark share URL input field for uploading camera bubble video](/images/7192095/f5a71ff3-image.png) You'll know that you've added your video successfully when you see it pop up in the "Share Page Preview" in the section below. ![Share page preview section displaying uploaded video with customization options](/images/7192095/2b6f45e2-image.png)
4. **Customize the video share page.**This section is optional, but can help you increase conversion rates when sending out your video for prospecting or sales campaigns. You can add...![Customize video share page panel with header message and call-to-action fields](/images/7192095/71e52304-image.png) A couple of notes here:
  1. **Header** — shows up above the video on the share page
  2. **Message** — shows up below the video on the share page
  3. **Call-to-action**— is a button on the video share page that leads the viewer to schedule a meeting with you, or take the next step in the sales process ​
  1. For any of these sections, you can use [dynamic variables as placeholders](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale) for the viewer's first name, company name, and job title. When you generate your dynamic videos, this will populate for each of your contacts.
  2. You can use your own branding (logo, colors, [domain](/use-a-custom-domain-for-videos), etc.) instead of the Sendspark default styles by [setting your global styles](/set-global-styles) in your workspace settings.
5. **To generate a dynamic video, simply add your prospects' name and URL, and click "Generate Dynamic Video."** It will take a few seconds, but soon you will see their custom video popular on the right of the page. ​

![Generate dynamic video interface with prospect name and URL input fields](/images/7192095/27971774-image.png)

## How to share dynamic videos

You have a few options for how you can share dynamic videos:

## Share videos 1-on-1 with prospects:

To share a specific video with a specific person, simply click on the "..." next to their name, and select a share option.

![Dropdown menu showing copy video link copy GIF and download options](/images/7192095/2e1ebd26-image.png)

1. **Copy Video Link** -- click on this to copy the link to your video share page to your clipboard. Then, you can paste the link anywhere on the web to share your videos. This is the best way to share your video in LinkedIn messages or any messaging platform.
2. **Copy GIF + Video Link** -- click on this to copy a preview of your video to your clipboard. Then, you can paste it into any email draft (Gmail, Outlook, Superhuman, SalesLoft, Outreach, Hubspot, or anywhere)! Your viewer will see an animated GIF thumbnail and be able to click on it to watch the video on the web.
3. **Download video** -- this will download the MP4 file of your video to your computer.

## Share at scale:

To share videos at scale, in email campaigns or sales sequences, click "Share Campaign"

![Share campaign button to distribute videos across email automation platforms](/images/7192095/1161eeaf-image.png)

You'll now see options to select different email automation platforms. Just select the one you want to use, and follow the specific share steps for that platform. Here are [tutorials for all of our email platform integrations](https://help.sendspark.com/en/collections/6113494-email-dynamic-videos).

![Email platform integration selection menu including Gmail Outlook and Hubspot](/images/7192095/a5d9f9c4-image.png)

## Who has access to dynamic backgrounds?

At this time, the dynamic video background feature is generally available to all users. Create highly personalized video campaigns by leveraging Sendspark's dynamic background functions.
