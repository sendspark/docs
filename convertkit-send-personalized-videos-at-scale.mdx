To send personalized videos through ConvertKit: set your brand styles, add personalization tokens to the video caption and landing page, enable the ConvertKit integration from Share Video to copy the dynamic code, then paste it into your ConvertKit email. Each subscriber sees their own name and contact details on the video.

## Key takeaways

- Enable the integration from **Share Video → ConvertKit** — if you don't see it, click **Edit → Add**, select ConvertKit, and confirm a blue checkmark; the dynamic code then copies to your clipboard.
- Paste the code into your ConvertKit email editor after deleting any existing content in that area.
- Until subscriber data is added, you'll see a fallback message like "Hey there," which you can customize in Sendspark.
- Personalize both the video caption (which appears in your email) and the landing page content, then preview as different contacts before sending.

This guide will show you how to send dynamically personalized videos through ConvertKit using Sendspark. Your subscribers will see their own name and contact information on each video, creating a personalized experience at scale.

## Prerequisites

- A Sendspark account with a video uploaded or recorded
- A ConvertKit account with subscriber data
- Email subscribers with contact properties (name, company, etc.)

## Step 1: Set Up Your Brand Styling

Before creating personalized videos, customize your video landing pages to match your brand:

1. Click **Set Brand Styles** when you first sign up
2. Add your logo and brand colors
3. This ensures a professional appearance for your company

## Step 2: Add Dynamic Personalization to Your Video

You can personalize both the video caption and landing page content.

## Customize Video Caption

1. From your video's landing page, click **Share Video**
2. Click **Custom** over the caption or **Customize** over the video thumbnail
3. Edit the text overlay by clicking the personalization tokens you want to add
4. This caption text will appear in your email

## Customize Landing Page Content

1. Add personalized text above or below the video (e.g., "Hi [First Name]")
2. Include company-specific messaging (e.g., "Let's get [Company Name] rolling with Sendspark")
3. Add a call-to-action button with a custom link for next steps

**Tip:** Your call-to-action can link to a calendar booking page, signup form, or any other step in your buyer journey.

## Step 3: Enable ConvertKit Integration

1. Click **Share Video**
2. Look for the **ConvertKit** option and click it
3. If you don't see ConvertKit, click **Edit** then **Add**
4. Scroll through the integrations list and select ConvertKit
5. Ensure there's a blue checkmark next to ConvertKit
6. The dynamic code will be copied to your clipboard

## Step 4: Add Video to Your ConvertKit Email

1. Go to your ConvertKit email editor
2. Delete any existing content in the area where you want the video
3. Paste the copied code (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V) into the content area
4. You'll see a fallback message (like "Hey there") until subscriber information is added

**Note:** You can customize the fallback message in Sendspark if needed.

## Step 5: Test Your Personalized Video

1. In ConvertKit, click **Continue** then **Preview**
2. Preview the email as different contacts in your system
3. Verify that each contact sees their personalized information on the video
4. Send your email when ready

**Tip:** Always test with multiple subscriber profiles to ensure personalization is working correctly before sending to your entire list.

## Results

Once set up, each subscriber will automatically see their own name and contact properties on the video thumbnail and content, creating a personalized experience that helps you connect with your audience at scale.
