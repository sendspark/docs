The Sendspark for Hubspot integration allows you to measure the impact video is having on your business.

This integration will allow you to send Sendspark page visits to Hubspot to track engagement with contacts and visualize video engagement data in dashboards.

---

## Requirements

- An active Sendspark account *(the Sendspark for Hubspot integration is available on every plan)*
- An active Hubspot account

---

## Integrate Sendspark with Hubspot

1. Go to your [Sendspark integrations tab](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations)
2. Next to Hubspot, click **Connect** ​![Sendspark integrations tab with Connect button next to Hubspot](/images/5970758/45ff5af8-image.png)
3. Enter your **Hub id** ![Hub ID input field in Sendspark Hubspot integration setup form](/images/5970758/c57dcdb3-image.png)
4. **Save** changes
5. You should see that Hubspot is **enabled** now 🎉

## Where to find your Hub ID

You can head to [https://app.hubspot.com/myaccounts](https://app.hubspot.com/myaccounts) and see the number right below your account name:

![Hubspot account settings showing Hub ID number below account name](/images/5970758/c8aadd24-image.png)

Or simply by clicking on the top right-hand side menu:

![Hubspot top right menu with account options and Hub ID location](/images/5970758/ecfc0184-image.png)
