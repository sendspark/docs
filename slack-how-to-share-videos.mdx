Sendspark makes it easy to share video messages in Slack. Follow the steps below to send videos to your team members.

## How to Send Videos in Slack

1. Click **Share Video** from your video page

![Share Video in Slack](/images/5776349/dbc32c09-image.png)

2. Under Share Link, click **Copy Link**

![Copy Video Share LInk](/images/5776349/db5c50b1-image.png)

**3. Paste** in Slack

![Paste Video Message in Slack](/images/5776349/b878046c-loopinguin.png)

You'll see the video link automatically unfurl into a video preview in Slack. Your team members can click here to watch the video.
