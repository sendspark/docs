To share a Sendspark video in Slack, open your video page and click **Share Video**, then under Share Link click **Copy Link**, and paste the link into Slack. The link automatically unfurls into a video preview that your team members can click to watch.

## Key takeaways

- Share a video in Slack in three steps: click **Share Video**, click **Copy Link** under Share Link, then paste into Slack.
- The pasted link automatically unfurls into a video preview inside Slack.
- Team members can click the preview to watch the video.

Sendspark makes it easy to share video messages in Slack. Follow the steps below to send videos to your team members.
