---
title: "Use a Call-to-Action (URL Link)"
description: "Learn how to add clickable call-to-action buttons to your videos with custom text and URLs to drive viewer engagement and conversions."
---

**Note**: you must be on the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or a higher plan to use video CTAs (Call-To-Action)

---

Follow these steps to add a button to your video:

- **Toggle** on the Call-to-Action field

![Call to Action](/images/5566693/6f97cc32-image.png)

- Write your desired **button text**(ex: Get Started Free)
- Add the **URL** you want to send viewers to (ex: [https://sendspark.com](https://sendspark.com))

![Call to Action display](/images/5566704/85268517-image.png)

You'll be able to check that everything looks good in the preview area on the right of your page 👍
