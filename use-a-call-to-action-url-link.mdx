To add a URL call-to-action button to your Sendspark video, toggle on the Call-to-Action field, write your button text (for example, Get Started Free), and add the URL you want viewers to visit. The button appears below your video, and you can confirm it in the preview area on the right of the page. Video CTAs require the Starter plan or higher.

## Key takeaways

- Toggle on the Call-to-Action field to add a button below your video.
- Enter your button text and the destination URL you want viewers sent to.
- Check that everything looks right in the live preview area on the right of the page.
- Video CTAs require at least the Starter plan.

**Note**: you must be on the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or a higher plan to use video CTAs (Call-To-Action)

Follow these steps to add a button to your video:

- **Toggle** on the Call-to-Action field

- Add the **URL** you want to send viewers to (ex: [https://sendspark.com](https://sendspark.com))
