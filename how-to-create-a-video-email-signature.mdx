## Want to add a personal touch to every email you send?

A video email signature helps you stand out in crowded inboxes, build trust instantly, and drive more replies — without recording a new video every time.

## ✨ Why Use a Video Email Signature?

If you send a lot of external emails — to customers, prospects, partners, candidates, or investors — a video signature can:

- Help people get to know you before a meeting
- Showcase your personality and communication style
- Increase replies (video can increase responses by up to 400%)
- Drive bookings with a built-in call-to-action

Even when you’re not sending a full personalized video, your signature keeps working for you.

---

## 🎬 Example: What Makes It Powerful?

With a video signature:

- People can see and hear you
- They feel more comfortable replying
- They can click directly to book time with you

It turns a normal email into a warmer introduction.

## 🛠 How to Create a Video Email Signature

Follow these simple steps:

---

## 1️⃣ Record Your Video

Use Sendspark (free plan available) to record:

- Webcam video
- Screen recording
- Webcam + floating bubble

Keep it short: **20–40 seconds**.

What to include:

- Quick introduction
- Who you help
- How you can help
- A simple next step

---

## 2️⃣ Customize Your Video Page

After recording:

- Add a clear call-to-action (CTA)
- Include your calendar link, LinkedIn, website, or “Send Me a Video” link
- Make sure the page reflects your brand

---

## 3️⃣ Create Your Video Thumbnail (Headshot with Play Button)

Your email signature will use an image that links to your video.

Recommended steps:

- Duplicate our Figma template (password: `sendmorevideos`)
- Upload your headshot
- Change the color to your brand color
- Export as a PNG

Prefer Photoshop? Use our PSD template instead.

---

## 4️⃣ Use the Signature Template

- Duplicate the Google Doc template
- Replace the placeholder content with:
  - Your name
  - Title
  - Links
  - Video thumbnail (hyperlinked to your Sendspark video)

---

## 5️⃣ Add It to Your Email Client (Example: Gmail)

1. Go to **Settings → See All Settings → Signature**
2. Click **Create New**
3. Name your signature
4. Paste the content from the Google Doc
5. Set signature defaults
6. Click **Save Changes**

## 💡 Tips for an Awesome Video Signature

## ✅ Keep it short

20–40 seconds is ideal.

## 👕 Dress season-neutral

Avoid clothing that makes the video feel outdated in a few months.

## 🔁 Update occasionally

You can re-record inside Sendspark without changing the link.

## 🎯 Always include a CTA

Examples:

- Book a meeting
- Connect on LinkedIn
- Visit your website
- Send you a video reply

## 🎉 Let your personality show

This is about being human — not polished or corporate.
