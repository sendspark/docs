---
title: "Edit Thumbnail Caption colors"
description: "Learn how to customize thumbnail caption colors by editing text and banner background colors in just a few simple steps."
---

If you are looking for the right color, you can completely customize the text color for your thumbnail and the banner very easily, check it out!

![Thumbnail modal](/images/5113587/ebcd8448-image.png)

To change the color of the font and the banner click on **Edit Styles** at the bottom:

![Edit Styles button](/images/5113587/fb4dc666-image.png)

Below **Text Overlay**, you will find the**Text Color**and the **Banner Background Color** options.

In this example, you can see a green banner with white text on it.

![Thumbnail modal](/images/5113587/af04d68c-image.png)

There are many things you can do, even removing the banner and simply leaving the text over your video.
