## Important Notes

- **Applies globally**: This setting affects **all videos in your workspace**, but only for **future videos**.
- **Captions cannot be edited**: You can only turn them off or on.
- **Existing videos won’t change**: Any videos created **before** updating this setting will **not** be updated.
- **Dynamic Campaigns**: If you’ve already launched a Dynamic Video Campaign with captions enabled and later turn captions off, the old campaign will **not** update. You’ll need to create a **new campaign** for the change to apply.

1) Head to your menu and click on Manage Workspace: [https://sendspark.com/settings/workspace](https://sendspark.com/settings/workspace)

![Sendspark menu with Manage Workspace option highlighted](/images/8940461/8a9f2e52-image.png)

2) Scroll down and you'll find the Captions handle at the bottom:

![Workspace settings page with Captions toggle switch at bottom](/images/8940461/ec032f96-image.png)

Your viewers will now have the option to click [c] to display captions on all new videos created from this point.

And, if you have autoplay enabled on your videos, captions will display automatically, so they can read your video even before they click for sound.

Note: The Global Settings Captions option only works on future videos. If you need to change this setting in older videos kindly follow the next steps

1) Click on the video you want to have the setting changed:

![Sendspark video library with video selected for editing](/images/8940461/cb7cca13-image.png)

2) Once the EDIT page has loaded, click on the 3 dots on the top Navbar and then toggle the Captions setting as needed:

![Video edit page with three-dot menu and Captions toggle option](/images/8940461/17d4f4cb-image.png)
