When generating videos using LinkedIn profile backgrounds, there are important limits to be aware of.

LinkedIn may temporarily block requests if too many profile fetches are made in a short period of time. This can cause video generation attempts to fail or be rejected.

**Best practices:**

- Limit video generation to **20–30 videos per day** when using LinkedIn profile backgrounds.
- Avoid sending large batches all at once.

**If videos fail or are rejected:**

- Wait **24–48 hours** before attempting again.
- After this waiting period, **reset your cookie/session** before retrying.

Following these guidelines helps ensure consistent video generation and prevents temporary blocks from LinkedIn.
