To text a video, click **Share Video** in Sendspark, use **Copy Link** under Share Link, then paste the link into a text message and send. As long as your recipient has your contact info saved, they'll see a video preview they can tap to watch.

## Key takeaways

- Share a video by text in three steps: **Share Video**, **Copy Link**, then paste into your message.
- Recipients see a clickable video preview when they have your contact info saved in their phone.
- The preview renders as a tappable thumbnail in clients like iMessage.

You can easily text videos to clients with Sendspark:

1. Click **Share Video** ​![Share Videos in Text Messages](/images/5837253/012bb740-image.png)
2. Under Share Link, click **Copy Link** ​![Copy Video Share Link](/images/5837253/2a2845ec-image.png)
3. **Paste** in your text messages and send!

![Paste Video in Text Message](/images/5837253/dc6dfba5-image.png)

**As long as your recipient has your contact info saved in their phone**, they'll see a video preview and can click to watch the whole video.
