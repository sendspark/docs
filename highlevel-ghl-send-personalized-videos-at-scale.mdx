This guide walks you through using Sendspark's HighLevel (GHL) integration to send personalized videos at scale through your email sequences and marketing campaigns. You'll learn how to create personalized videos and seamlessly integrate them into your HighLevel workflows.

## Creating Your Personalized Video

1. Create your video in Sendspark by either:
  - Recording a new video using our web-based recorder or Chrome extension
  - Uploading an existing video file
2. Navigate to the **video page editor** to add personalization elements
3. Add personalized text above your video (e.g., "Hi [First Name]" or "Video for [First Name]")
4. Include additional text below the video using **Dynamic variables** as needed
5. Add a clear **call to action** telling viewers what to do after watching

**Tip:** Always include a call to action to guide your viewers on the next steps after watching your video.

## Sharing Videos in HighLevel

1. Click the **Share Video** option in your Sendspark video
2. Select the appropriate HighLevel integration option:
  - **Lead Connector:** For sales communications, one-to-one emails, and automated sequences
  - **Lead Connector Marketing:** For HTML marketing emails and templated campaigns
3. Copy the provided share code
4. Paste the code into the appropriate section of your HighLevel campaign

## Understanding the Two Integration Options

## Lead Connector (Sales & Automation)

Use this option for:

- Sales communications and audits
- One-to-one email sequences (even when automated)
- More natural-looking email integration

## Lead Connector Marketing

Use this option for:

- HTML marketing emails
- Templated email campaigns
- Polished marketing communications

## Final Steps

1. Paste your chosen share code into HighLevel
2. Resize the video preview if needed to ensure optimal display
3. Test your campaign - the integration will automatically pull in each viewer's first name for personalization.

**Note:** No additional editing is required after pasting the code into HighLevel. The personalization will work automatically when your contacts receive the emails.
