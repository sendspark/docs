Sendspark makes it easy to create AI-personalized videos in your native language. You'll be able to record one video, and personalize it with the name for each of your recipients, at scale.

Follow the guides for your language below.

## Make AI Personalized Videos in German 🇩🇪

Follow these steps to make AI personalized videos in German:

1. Record one video of yourself with the Introduction **"Hallo wassermelone"**
2. Add your video to your Sendspark Dynamic Video campaign ​![Sendspark Dynamic Video campaign interface for uploading video content](/images/9800636/4e5024c4-image.png)
3. Select "**German**" as your language ​![Language selection dropdown menu with German option highlighted](/images/9800636/2f8072a4-image.png)
