Sendspark's AI video templates let you create personalized outreach videos in four steps: select a template that fits your message, generate an AI-powered script, record using the built-in teleprompter, then personalize with dynamic variables and send at scale via email, LinkedIn, or embed.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark generates an AI-powered script, plus email content and landing page content, based on the template you choose.
- The built-in teleprompter scrolls your script as you record, and you can adjust the scrolling speed for a natural delivery.
- You can add dynamic variables such as name and company to personalize videos automatically.
- Finished videos can be shared via email, LinkedIn, or embedded in your outreach campaigns.

With AI-generated scripts, a built-in teleprompter, and seamless personalization, you can craft the perfect message effortlessly. This guide will walk you through how to use this feature effectively.

## **Step 1: Select a Video Template**

1. Log in to Sendspark and navigate to the [welcome page](https://sendspark.com/welcome)
2. Choose a template that fits your message (e.g., sales outreach, follow-up, onboarding, etc.).

![Sendspark welcome page with AI video template cards for outreach scenarios](/images/10771229/264e320b-image.png)

## **Step 2: Generate an AI Script**

1. Once you’ve selected a template, Sendspark will generate an AI-powered script based on best practices. We'll provide a video script, email content, and landing page content that you can use. ​![AI-generated script dialog with Script, Email, and Page tabs](/images/10771229/d21e8c65-image.png)
2. You can review and edit the script as needed to match your personal style and audience.

## **Step 3: Record with the Teleprompter**

1. Preview the script and edit it as you'd like ​![Sendspark teleprompter window showing script above the camera preview while recording](/images/10771229/82db4eb7-image.png)
2. Click **Record Video** to start recording. The teleprompter will disappear and come back after the countdown for you.
3. The script will scroll as you record, helping you stay on track.
4. Adjust the scrolling speed to your preference for a natural delivery.

## **Step 4: Personalize and Send at Scale**

1. After recording, use Sendspark’s AI personalization features to tailor the video for individual recipients.
2. Add dynamic variables (e.g., name, company) to customize your message automatically.
3. Click **Share** and send your video via email, LinkedIn, or embed it in your outreach campaigns.
