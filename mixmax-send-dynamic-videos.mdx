## 📧 Send Dynamic Videos via Mixmax

## Overview

Learn how to send Sendspark dynamic videos through Mixmax using HTML embed code. This allows your recipients to see a clickable video thumbnail directly in their email.

## ⚙️ Step-by-Step Setup

## 1. Go to Your Dynamic Campaign

- Navigate to your **Dynamic Video Campaign**
- Make sure your videos are already generated

---

## 2. Copy the Mixmax HTML Code

- Click **Share Campaign**
- Select **Mixmax**

👉 This copies the HTML embed code that displays the video thumbnail in your email

---

## 3. Paste into Mixmax

- Go to your **Mixmax sequence or email**
- Place your cursor where you want the video preview
- Paste using:
  - `Cmd + V` (Mac)
  - `Ctrl + V` (Windows)

👉 A placeholder will appear in your email editor

---

## 4. Adjust the Thumbnail Size

- Hover over the placeholder
- Choose:
  - **Best Fit** (recommended)
  - Or resize manually from the corner

---

## 5. Understand How It Works

- The placeholder will look empty in the editor
- Once sent, it automatically becomes a video thumbnail

👉 The system uses the **recipient’s email** to:

- Match the correct video
- Personalize the experience

---

## 6. Send a Test Email

- Send the email to yourself
- Open your inbox

👉 You’ll see:

- A clickable thumbnail
- A link to your video landing page

---

## ✅ Result

Recipients receive a personalized video thumbnail that opens your full Sendspark landing page, including:

- Buttons
- Calendar
- Any custom elements you added

---

## 💡 Tips

- Always test before sending to a list
- Use “Best Fit” for cleaner formatting
- Make sure your contact emails match your campaign data
