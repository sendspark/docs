If your camera isn't working in Sendspark, it's almost always a permissions issue. Start by using Google Chrome with the Sendspark extension, then set Camera and Microphone to **Allow** for Sendspark in Chrome's site settings, confirm Chrome has OS-level camera access (Mac or Windows), and test the camera in another app. Restarting your computer resolves many remaining cases.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark works best on Google Chrome with the [Sendspark extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph); Edge and Firefox work but with fewer features.
- Set **Camera** and **Microphone** to **Allow** for Sendspark in Chrome's site settings, and grant the same to the Chrome extension if installed.
- Make sure Chrome has operating-system permission to use your camera (Mac: System Preferences → Security & Privacy → Camera; Windows: Camera Privacy Settings).
- If the camera fails in other apps too, restart your computer; if it still doesn't work, contact Sendspark support.

Before you go too far, make sure that you're using a supported browser. Right now, Sendspark works best on Google Chrome with our [Sendspark extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph).

Although you are also able to use our platform through Microsoft's Edge and Firefox, you won't take full advantage of everything we have to offer through Chrome's extension.

## 1. Give Sendspark Permissions on Google Chrome...

- Access your Chrome settings by copying and pasting the following link on your search bar **(do not click it)**to open camera/mic permissions: ​chrome://settings/content/siteDetails?site=https%3A%2F%2Fsendspark.com%2F&search=Camera ​
- Make sure "**Camera" and "Microphone" are explicitly set to "Allow."**Then refresh your page and try again.

![Set Chrome Permissions to Allow Recording](/images/3388387/74977f9c-image.png)

🔥 **If you have the Sendspark Chrome Extension installed, make sure to grant it the necessary permissions as well.**​

- Make sure "**Camera" and "Microphone" are explicitly set to "Allow."**Then refresh your page and try again.

## 2. Make sure Chrome has permission to access your camera

If using Mac:

- Go to System Preferences → Security & Privacy → Camera, and make sure Google Chrome is checked

![Set Mac System Preferences to Allow Recording](/images/3388387/0be2b0ef-image.png)

**If using Windows:**

- Go to Settings → search for "Camera Privacy Settings" → and confirm that "Allow desktop apps to access your camera" is toggled ON.

![Camera settings in Windows](/images/3388387/d7820487-image.png)

## 3. Make sure your camera is working

- Check if the video feed is working on other apps on your computer (i.e. Zoom, Google Meet, FaceTime). If not, you may need to restart your computer.

## 4. Restart your computer

- Still now working? Shoot us a message and we will try to help!

  <Accordion title="How do I give Sendspark camera and microphone permission in Chrome?">

  </Accordion>
