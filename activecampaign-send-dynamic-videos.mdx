## How to Send AI Personalized Video in ActiveCampaign:

1. Set up **dynamic video campaign** in Sendspark
2. Upload list of **contacts** into Sendspark to generate videos
3. Generate videos
4. Click **"Share Campaign"**
5. Copy snippet for **"ActiveCampaign"**
6. Paste directly into an **HTML block**in your ActiveCampaign template
7. Send!
