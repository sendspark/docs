To send AI personalized videos in ActiveCampaign, set up a dynamic video campaign in Sendspark, upload your contacts, and generate the videos. Then click **Share Campaign**, copy the **ActiveCampaign** snippet, paste it into an HTML block in your ActiveCampaign template, and send.

## Key takeaways

- Videos are created from a Sendspark dynamic video campaign after you upload a contact list and generate them.
- Use **Share Campaign** to copy the ActiveCampaign-specific code snippet.
- Paste the snippet into an HTML block in your ActiveCampaign template, then send.

Sendspark makes it easy to share AI personalized videos in ActiveCampaign.

## How to Send AI Personalized Video in ActiveCampaign:

1. Set up **dynamic video campaign** in Sendspark
2. Upload list of **contacts** into Sendspark to generate videos
3. Generate videos
4. Click **"Share Campaign"**
5. Copy snippet for **"ActiveCampaign"**
6. Paste directly into an **HTML block**in your ActiveCampaign template
7. Send!
