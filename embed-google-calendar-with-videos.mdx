### Embed your Google calendar next to your Sendspark videos to help prospects book meetings right after watching.

This procedure only works for Google Workspaces or Business accounts

## Steps to follow In Google:

1. Head to your calendar app: [https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r)

![Google Calendar app homepage with navigation menu and options](/images/9211984/7b97cf43-image.png)

2. On the left-hand side corner, click on +Create and then on Appointment Schedule

![Google Calendar left sidebar with Create button and Appointment Schedule option](/images/9211984/960fc176-image.png)

3. Set your title and schedule windows and click on Next:

![Google Calendar appointment schedule creation form with title and time fields](/images/9211984/1c74ebab-image.png)

4. Finish your desired settings and click on SAVE:

![Google Calendar appointment schedule settings page with configuration options and Save button](/images/9211984/6b6779c6-image.png)

5. A pop-up window will come up. Click on Open Booking Page:

![Google Calendar pop-up confirmation window with Open Booking Page button](/images/9211984/de18dbb8-image.png)

6. You should now be on a new page. Kindly copy its URL:

![Google Calendar booking page URL in address bar ready to copy](/images/9211984/9907767f-image.png)

## Steps to follow In Sendspark:

1. Under your Sendspark video, toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**

2. Select **"Google Calendar"** from the Provider Dropdown List

**3. Add the link**to the Google calendar you want to use

![Sendspark video editor showing Calendar toggle set to On and Google Calendar provider selected](/images/9211984/4fa7c2f3-image.png)

You should be able to see your embedded Google calendar in the preview area!
