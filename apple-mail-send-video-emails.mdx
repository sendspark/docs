Follow these steps to send videos in Apple Mail.

1) Record or upload your video in [Sendspark](https://sendspark.com/my-videos) and Click **Share Video**

![Share Video in Apple Mail](/images/5569060/4b05030c-image.png)

2) Click on**Customize**

![Share modal with Customize option](/images/5569060/14b69f99-image.png)

3) At the bottom, click on the middle option **Edit Preview**and select Animated.

![Edit Thumbnail modal](/images/5569060/a7c06121-image.png)

4) Go back and click on **Copy Linked GIF**

![Share modal with Copy Linked GIF highlighted](/images/5569060/ea51a563-image.png)

**5)**Paste directly into an email draft on Apple Mail
