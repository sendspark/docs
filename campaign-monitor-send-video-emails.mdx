description: "Learn how to send video emails in Campaign Monitor using Sendspark in under 2 minutes with our step-by-step integration guide."
---

Sendspark makes it easy to send video emails in Campaign Monitor email campaigns. Follow the steps below to send your first video email in less than 2 minutes:

1. Create a new video campaign in Sendspark, by clicking "New Video" from the top right of your [video dashboard](https://app.sendspark.com).

![Sendspark video dashboard with New Video button in top right corner](/images/5413254/d8aad5fa-image.png)

2. Record yourself or screen, or upload an existing video

![Sendspark video recording interface with record, screen, and upload options](/images/5413254/5b909e63-image.png)

3. Click "Share Video"

![Sendspark video editor with Share Video button prominently displayed](/images/5413254/b26029a4-image.png)

3. Click to Download Thumbnail (GIF). You'll use this as the preview to your video in Campaign Monitor.

![Sendspark download thumbnail GIF option for video preview creation](/images/5413254/e08001ce-image.png)

4. Copy the link your video to your clipboard. You'll use this to link the video preview to your video for viewer's to watch.

![Sendspark copy video link interface with clipboard button highlighted](/images/5413254/2b03c405-image.png)

5. Add an image block to your email on Campaign Monitor

![Campaign Monitor email editor with image block insertion option](/images/5413254/4c36173e-image.png)

6. Upload the Sendspark video preview to Hubspot in an image block and paste the link to your video in Campaign Monitor

![Campaign Monitor email showing uploaded video preview with linked video URL](/images/5413254/854c482c-image.png)

7. That's it! Send yourself a test video to see how recipients will experience your video before sending it off.
