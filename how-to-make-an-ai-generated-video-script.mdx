Sendspark video messages can increase engagement and replies 300%+ across your sales communication -- from prospecting to demos to follow-ups and more -- but you have to know what to say!

We integrated OpenAI to make an [AI-powered Video Script Generator](https://sendspark.com/video-script-generator). You can just answer a few questions, and then click "generate" to make your very own video script.

Think of this video script as a starting block. You'll want to customize it with your own voice, tone, and flavor.

## How to Use Sendspark's AI Video Script Generator

Watch the video here or follow the steps below!

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/5-srue3Y4ls?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Navigate to [Sendspark's AI Video Script Generator,](https://sendspark.com/video-script-generator) and click "Let's Get Started" ​![AI Video Script Generator for Sales](/images/7192161/f762d469-image.png)
2. Select the use case for your video message.![Video Scripts for Prospecting, Follow Ups, Welcome Videos, and more](/images/7192161/ba4e5ade-image.png)
3. Select the ton of voice you want to use for your video script. ​![Video Script tone of voice](/images/7192161/c1fd884f-image.png)
4. Add the name of your product ​![Video Script for Your Product](/images/7192161/00de45bd-image.png)
5. Add a short description of what your product does, and how it helps people. ​![Video Script Product Description](/images/7192161/70629a01-image.png)
6. Add context that can help our AI make this video script more relevant for your intended viewer. ​![Video Script Context](/images/7192161/596b7aec-image.png)
7. Add the call-to-action. This is what you want your viewer to do after viewing your video. ​![Video Script Call-to-Action](/images/7192161/0c081cde-image.png)
8. Generate your video script!

## Use your Video Script

You'll now see your **video script**, ready to use! You can add this to your teleprompter to read from when you record your video.

![Video Script Example](/images/7192161/e3eb8d7b-image.png)

Additionally, you can click into the "email" tab to see complimentary **email subject line** & **email body content** to use when sharing your video.
