Sendspark makes it easy to support customers in Intercom faster with video messages.

---

## Before you get started:

Please install the [Sendspark Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph) to use the Intercom integration.

As long as you have the Chrome Extension installed, you’ll automatically be able to access Sendspark from Intercom.

Install Chrome Extension

---

## How to Send Videos in Intercom

## 1. Click the Sendspark Icon in Intercom

Create a **new conversation**or open an**existing conversation in Intercom**. You’ll see the Sendspark logo (the purple icon) appear in the compose window next to the native Intercom options.

![Intercom compose window with purple Sendspark icon next to native options](/images/5809228/7545da75-image.png)

**Note:** you must have the [Sendspark chrome extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph/related?hl=en-US) installed and be using a Chrome browser to see the Sendspark icon.

## 2. Record a New Video

Click on the Sendspark icon to launch the extension, and then select **Record New Video**.

This will launch the Sendspark Chrome Extension, so you can record a video of yourself or your screen.

![Sendspark extension menu showing Record New Video and alternative options](/images/5809228/2f2c396a-image.png)

Alternatively, you can choose from the list of options:

- **Request Video** — inserts a [video request link](https://www.sendspark.com/video-platform/request-videos) to easily get a video recording or testimonial from your customer
- **Meet Now**— inserts a Zoom or Google Meet link to connect face-to-face
- **Send Calendar** — inserts a Calendly or scheduling link to coordinate a time to connect
- **Add Last Recording** — inserts the last Sendspark video you recorded
- **Video Library** — opens your Sendspark dashboard to find or manage existing videos

## 3. Complete Recording

To end your video, **click the Sendspark Chrome Extension icon** on the top right of your browser.

![Sendspark Chrome Extension icon in top right browser toolbar for ending recordings](/images/5809352/c327b16f-S6TIIVgdBXX0OYPIohqqGTwagqWJOmoltVnRPoOS_LBobgEGtwAxomuCvgz60DVVPywyW9sx6BWXh6CsHax_I8VNKGoQecF6NaEbIQGUNAXHAYvjZxbV09SPMbWTZ6Zr_OHPKoRk_s0.png)

This will complete your video recording, and launch a modal for you to preview your video before sharing.

## 4. (Optional) Edit Video Page in Sendspark

You can customize your video before sharing it by clicking **Edit Page / Thumbnail**.

![Sendspark video preview modal with Edit Page and Thumbnail customization button](/images/5809228/d0ed1d8b-image.png)

This will open your video in a new window, where you can edit the [video landing page](/customize-your-video-landing-page) or [video thumbnail.](/how-to-edit-video-thumbnails)

**Note**: When you edit the page, you’ll leave Intercom and go to Sendspark. Now, instead of inserting your video, you'll need to copy and paste your video share link into Intercom

## 5. Insert Your Video in Intercom

Click **Insert Video** to place your video in your email draft.

![Sendspark video preview modal with Insert Video button to add to Intercom](/images/5809228/0da87f9a-image.png)
