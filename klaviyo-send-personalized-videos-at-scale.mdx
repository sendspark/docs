To send personalized videos at scale in Klaviyo, create your video in Sendspark and add merge tags that update for each recipient, then click Share Video and select Klaviyo to copy the snippet. In a Klaviyo email text block, click Source, paste the snippet into the HTML, and save to embed the video in-line.

## Key takeaways

- Merge tags automatically personalize the video for each recipient, and a fallback is shown when a recipient's information is unknown.
- Create your video by recording, uploading, or requesting it, then add a title, message, or call-to-action, or edit the thumbnail.
- Click Share Video, then Klaviyo, to copy the share snippet to your clipboard; modify your shortcuts if Klaviyo isn't listed.
- In a Klaviyo email text block, click Source to edit the HTML, paste the snippet, and save to embed the video in-line.

## Why Send Hyper-Personalized Videos in Klaviyo?

Sendspark makes it easy to record and [embed videos in Klaviyo](/klaviyo-send-video-emails). With our hyper-personalization feature, you can automatically personalize viewers for each viewer to add a special touch and increase engagement.

## How to Send Personalized Videos at Scale in Klaviyo

Watch the video or follow the steps below!

## 1. Create your video in Sendspark

You can record, upload, or request a video.

## 2. Personalize your video

Feel free to add a title, message, or call-to-action on your video page or [edit the video thumbnail](/how-to-edit-video-thumbnails).

You can also use merge tags to automatically personalize videos for viewers. These merge tags will automatically update for each recipient. If the recipient's information is unknown, they will see a [fallback](/how-merge-tag-fallbacks-work).

![Personalize video in Sendspark](/images/6503935/baccd0bd-image.png)

## 3. Click "Share Video"

![Share personalized video in Klaviyo](/images/6503935/7e76927c-image.png)

## 4. From the Sendspark share modal, click "Klaviyo"

By clicking "Klaviyo," you will copy your video share snippet to your clipboard.

Note, if you don't see Klaviyo on the share modal, you can [modify your shortcuts](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal)

![Copy Video Thumbnail for Klaviyo](/images/6503935/b5938d43-image.png)

## 5. Paste into your Klaviyo email

Go to any Klaviyo email. From a text block, click **"source."** This will let you edit the HTML of your email.

![Edit Klaviyo email](/images/6503935/bffbfe75-image.png)

Now, paste your Sendspark video snippet directly into the source code. Click **"save,"**and you will see your hyper-personalized video embedded in-line in your Klaviyo email!

![Preview video in email](/images/6503935/5ca1bb03-image.png)

That's it, you're ready to send your video!
