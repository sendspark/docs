## Why Send Videos in Sendfox?

With the Sendspark + Sendfox integration, you can easily send video emails to your audience.

## How to Send Personalized Videos at Scale in Sendfox

Watch the video or follow the steps below:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/9eWali4qQlQ?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

## 1. Create your video in Sendspark

You can record, upload, or request a video.

## 2. Personalize your video

Feel free to add a title, message, or call-to-action on your video page or to [edit the video thumbnail](/how-to-edit-video-thumbnails).

You can also use merge tags to automatically personalize videos for viewers. These merge tags will automatically update for each recipient. If the recipient's information is unknown, they will see a [fallback](/how-merge-tag-fallbacks-work).

![Personalize video in Sendspark](/images/6503935/baccd0bd-image.png)

## 3. Click "Share Video"

![Share personalized video in Sendfox](/images/6503935/7e76927c-image.png)

## 4. From the Sendspark share modal, click "Sendfox"

By clicking "Sendfox," you will copy your video share snippet to your clipboard.

Note, if you don't see Sendfox on the share modal, you can [modify your shortcuts](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal).

![Copy personalized video in Sendspark](/images/6503886/4443a173-image.png)

## 5. Paste into your Sendfox email

From any email in Sendfox, just paste in your Sendspark video!

![Send video email in Sendfox](/images/6503886/680f9907-image.png)

That's it, you're ready to send your video!
