Instant Integrations let you record and share Sendspark videos directly inside the platforms you already use — Gmail, LinkedIn, HubSpot, Outreach, Zendesk, Intercom, and Close CRM. They appear the moment you install the Sendspark Chrome extension, adding a Sendspark icon to each platform's compose window so you can record, preview, and insert a video without leaving the page.

At Sendspark, we strive to make it as fast as possible to send videos to clients and prospective customers.

## Key takeaways

- Instant Integrations let you record and share videos directly inside Gmail, LinkedIn, HubSpot, Outreach, Zendesk, Intercom, and Close CRM.
- They're enabled automatically the second you install the Sendspark Chrome extension.
- A Sendspark icon appears in each platform's compose window — record a new video, preview it, add templates or trim, then insert and send.
- You can toggle instant integrations off in your integration settings; that only disables them for you, not for the rest of your workspace.

So we built "Instant Integrations" to make it blazing fast for you to record and share videos right within the popular communication platforms, including:

When using Sendspark instant integrations, you can simply...

1. See a Sendspark icon in the compose windows of all of these messaging platforms ​![Sendspark icon visible in Gmail compose window toolbar](/images/5717802/1b8b6db4-image.png)
2. Select "Record new video" to launch the Sendspark recorder (or choose from the other menu items)![Sendspark menu showing Record new video option selected](/images/5717802/b9949710-image.png)
3. Preview your video, and use shortcuts to add templates, combine videos, trim, and more. ​![Sendspark video preview editor with trim and template shortcuts](/images/7887983/6b881019-image.png)
4. Insert, and send your video email! ​

All of these instant integrations are enabled the second that you [install the Sendspark chrome extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph?hl=en-US).

Install the Chrome Extension to use Instant Integrations

However, if you do not want to use any of the instant integrations, you can toggle them off in your [integration settings.](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations)

![Integration settings panel with toggle switches for instant integrations](/images/7887983/bcb4ea2e-image.png)

When you toggle off integrations, they are disabled for you, but still enabled for other members of your workspace (who have the chrome extension installed).

Of course, you can toggle instant integrations back on to use them at any time!
