To send a video in MailerLite, record your video in Sendspark, click **Share Video**, choose **Embed**, and copy the **Email Embed Code**. Then, in your MailerLite email draft, open a text block, click the **HTML icon**, paste the code, and click **Save**. Recipients whose email client supports video watch it in-email; everyone else sees an animated GIF preview that opens the full video in their browser.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark embeds videos into your existing MailerLite emails using an **Email Embed Code** that you paste into a MailerLite text block's HTML editor.
- Video emails have been shown to drive **2-3x higher click-through rates** than emails without video.
- Whether the video plays inside the email depends on the recipient's email client. Clients that support video play it in-email; the rest show an **animated GIF preview** that links to the full video.
- The embed takes five clicks in Sendspark and MailerLite combined: Share Video → Embed → copy code → paste into the HTML block → Save.

## Will Videos in MailerLite Play In-Email?

The short answer is sometimes! Videos will play in email if your recipient's email client supports video. Not all email clients support video though. You can see and up-to-date list of [email clients that support video here](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos).

If your recipient's email client does not support video, no worries! They will see an animated GIF preview of your video, and be able to watch the full video in their browser. Still an awesome user experience!

## How to Send Videos in MailerLite

Watch the video, or follow the steps below!

1. Record your video in [Sendspark](https://sendspark.com/my-videos)

6. **Paste** your video ![Embed Video in MailerLite](/images/5776653/568711bd-image.png)

That's it! You're all set to send your video email and "de-Lite" your recipients!
