## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Q: Can I use my Sendspark affiliate link to run ads?

A: You can run ads using your Sendspark affiliate link, but you are prohibited from bidding on Sendspark brand keywords (i.e. "Sendspark").

## Q: Can I manually add referrals to my affiliate account?

A: If you know that you've referred a customer, but you aren't sure they clicked on your affiliate link, please email partners(at)sendspark(dot)com and we will add your referral to your account to ensure you get credit.
