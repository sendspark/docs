To send personalized videos at scale in Snov.io, record or upload your video in Sendspark, personalize the header, message, and thumbnail with placeholders, click Share Video and select Snov.io, then paste the snippet into a Snov.io email draft and send. Sendspark automatically pulls in each contact's first name, company name, and job title.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark can automatically pull in each contact's first name, company name, and job title using placeholders.
- Personalize the video header, message, and thumbnail before sharing.
- Click Share Video, select Snov.io, then paste the snippet into a Snov.io email draft (if Snov.io isn't listed, modify your share modal).
- If you've customized the thumbnail, the personalization shows when you preview videos in Snov.io.

Send personalized videos in Snov.io emails to cut through the noise and connect at scale.

Sendspark makes it easy to automatically pull ina your viewer's first name, company name, and job title, just follow the steps below!

## How to Send Bulk Personalized Videos in Snov.io Emails

## 1. Record or upload your video

Record or upload your video in Sendspark [your Sendspark video library](https://sendspark.com/my-videos)

![Record or upload video in Sendspark](/images/6676528/d1dc5ed7-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_4.45.49_PM.png)

## 2. Personalize content around video

Customize the **video header**, **message**, and **thumbnail**. Feel free to use placeholders to automatically pull in contact information from Snov.io when you send your video!

![Personalize video](/images/6676528/e4302b71-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_4.46.27_PM.png)

## 3. Copy share snippet for Snov.io

Click **"Share Video,"** and then select Snov.io (if you don't see Snov.io, follow these steps to [modify your share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal)).

![Share Video in Snov.io](/images/6609597/d12a3199-image.png)

## 4. Paste in Snov.io

Just paste directly into a Snov.io email draft

![Paste video into Snov.io](/images/6609597/6b669ef0-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_5.00.32_PM.png)

## 5. Preview and send!

If you've customized your video thumbnail, you should be able to see the personalization when you preview videos in Snov.io

![Send Personalized Video in Snov.io](/images/6609597/3c54d0b3-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_5.00.56_PM.png)
