To update your payment method in Sendspark, open your account menu and click Billing, then Manage Card, then Edit Information. Fill in your new card details and click Update Information. Updating your card does not trigger a payment, so if you have an unpaid invoice you'll still need to pay it separately.

## Key takeaways

- Update your card under Account menu → Billing → Manage Card → Edit Information.
- Enter the new card details and click Update Information to save.
- Updating your card does not trigger a payment on its own.
- If you have an unpaid invoice, follow the Review and Pay Our Invoices guide to settle it.

In this article, you will learn how to update your payment method

1) Head to your account Menu and click on BILLING:

![Sendspark account menu with Billing option highlighted](/images/8856714/ba5b04ca-image.png)

2) Click on Manage Card:

![Billing page displaying Manage Card button and current payment method](/images/8856714/c5a02fba-image.png)

3) Click on Edit Information:

![Card management interface with Edit Information button visible](/images/8856714/d5205b5c-image.png)

4) Fill out the form with the new information and click on Update Information:

![Payment information form with fields for new card details and Update button](/images/8856714/56e423d3-image.png)
