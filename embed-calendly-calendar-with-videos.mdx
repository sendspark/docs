---

To embed a Calendly calendar next to your Sendspark video, toggle "Calendar" to "On," select "Calendly" from the Provider dropdown, and paste the link to your Calendly calendar (not the embed code). Sendspark embeds it automatically, and you'll see the Calendly preview appear to the right of your video.

## Key takeaways

- Embedding a Calendly calendar lets your viewers schedule a meeting with you without ever leaving your video page.
- Turn the "Calendar" toggle to "On," choose "Calendly" from the Provider dropdown, and add the link to the Calendly calendar you want to use.
- Add the calendar link only, not the embed code; Sendspark automatically embeds the calendar from the link.
- You'll know it's working when the Calendly preview appears to the right of your video.

Sendspark makes it easy to embed Calendly calendars next to videos, so your viewers can schedule a meeting with you without ever leaving your video page.
