title: "Facebook Messenger — How to Share Videos"
description: "Learn how to share Sendspark videos in Facebook Messenger by copying your video link and pasting it into any chat conversation."
noindex: true
---

Follow these steps to share Sendspark videos in Facebook Messenger:

- Record or upload your video
- Click **Share Video**
- Under **Share Link**, click **Copy Link**

![Copy Video Share Link](/images/5568925/cb6c4782-image.png)

- Paste this link into your Facebook Messenger chat
- Once the link is sent, you will see a preview of your video unfurl, and recipients can click to play your video. Note that the preview won't display until it is sent through the chat.

![Send Video in Facebook Messenger](/images/5568925/40c8ec12-image.png)
