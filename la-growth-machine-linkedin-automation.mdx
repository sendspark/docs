This guide will walk through how you can send your Sendspark AI-personalized videos in LaGrowthMachine LinkedIn automation.

## How to Send Dynamic Videos in LaGrowthMachine LinkedIn Automation

1. First, **create your dynamic videos** in Sendspark. If you don't know how to make AI-personalized videos in Sendspark, start with this guide on [how to make dynamic videos](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions).
2. Then, click **"Download CSV"** to download the list of successfully generated videos for each contact.
3. Next, **upload this CSV to LaGrowthMachine**. ​
4. ![LaGrowthMachine CSV upload interface with field mapping options](/images/9658445/8dd29867-image.png)When you upload this CSV, make sure to map in the field **"Video Link" to the LaGrowthMachine "Custom Att 1"** ​![Video Link field being mapped to Custom Att 1 in LaGrowthMachine](/images/9658445/edf8c599-image.png)
5. Now, in your LaGrowthMachine workflow, you can **use "Custom Att 1"**where you want to send your Sendspark video to appear! ​![LaGrowthMachine workflow builder showing Custom Att 1 variable placeholder](/images/9658445/c412ee03-image.png)

That's it! Let us know if you have questions!
