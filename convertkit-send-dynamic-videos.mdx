To send dynamic videos in ConvertKit, build your dynamic video campaign in Sendspark, then click Share Campaign and copy the ConvertKit code. Paste it as plain text into a ConvertKit email template, save it, and select that template under Send → Broadcasts. Send to the same contacts you generated videos for, using Sendspark's Download CSV to be sure.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark's Dynamic Video feature places one recorded video over any background, so it looks like you're recording over each prospect's website or URL.
- Copy the ConvertKit-specific code from Share Campaign and paste it as plain text (CMD/CTRL + V) into a ConvertKit email template.
- Use the template under Send → Broadcasts, then preview and send a test email to confirm the result.
- ConvertKit's preview may show the video as a broken link because of how dynamic videos work. It still renders correctly in the recipient's inbox.
- Send to the same list of contacts you generated videos for; use Sendspark's Download CSV to match them exactly.

## How to Send Dynamic Videos in Convertkit:

1. Create your email campaign in Convertkit

2. Upload your contacts to your Convertkit account.

![ConvertKit add subscribers](/images/8499990/6f2b2d64-image.png)

To make sure that you're using the same contacts you've generated Sendspark dynamic videos for, you can click the "Download CSV" button in Sendspark.

![Download CSV modal](/images/8511040/7fb48639-image.png)

3. Click on Share Campaign, look for Convertkit, and click on it to copy the code.

![Share Dynamic video modal](/images/8499990/405bdbb5-image.png)

4. Create an email Template and paste the code as plain text "CMD or CTRL + V"

![ConvertKit email template](/images/8499990/90fa6ef4-image.png)

5. Save the Template and go to Send --&gt; Broadcasts --&gt; and select the Template you just created.

![ConvertKit Select Template](/images/8499990/1802879b-image.png)

6. Click on "preview" and Send a Test Email to see the final result

![ConvertKit Preview email](/images/8499990/d0f49384-image.png)

7. Notice that Convertkit will display the video as a broken link because of how Dynamic Videos work. But no worries, the system just doesn't understand how to display the preview. Sending the test email will confirm that it works once it is in the recipient's inbox.

![ConvertKit link warning message](/images/8499990/b64685c7-image.png)

8. And that's all to it!

![ConvertKit Final Result](/images/8499990/bdb489a1-image.png)

Note: it's important that you send the video in Convertkit to the same list of contacts you generated videos for in Sendspark. To be extra safe, you can click "download CSV" in Sendspark to get a list of contacts who we've generated videos for. Start your free
