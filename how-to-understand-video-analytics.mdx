Sendspark's Analytics modal shows who is watching your videos so you can measure engagement. Open it by clicking the **Analytics** button on any video in your video dashboard. You'll see Total Engagement metrics (views, plays, clicks, and likes) plus Viewer Insights that break down each visitor's location, device, browser, and interactions.

## Key takeaways

- Open the Analytics modal from the **Analytics** button on any video in your [video dashboard](https://sendspark.com/my-videos).
- **Total Views** counts page opens, not plays. **Total Plays** counts actual video plays, **Total Clicks** counts call-to-action clicks, and **Total Likes** counts thumbs-up clicks.
- **Viewer Insights** shows each session's visitor location, device, browser, and IP, plus per-session play, click, and like counts and the time of their last interaction.
- Total Engagement data goes back to when you created your account; Viewer Insights data starts January 14, 2022.

The Sendspark Analytics modal provides insight into **who is watching your videos**. This can help you understand how your videos are adding value to your organization.

You can launch the Analytics modal by clicking the **Analytics** button on videos in your [video dashboard](https://sendspark.com/my-videos).

![Sendspark video dashboard with Analytics button highlighted on a video](/images/5171294/bddb03b4-image.png)

## Total Engagement Metrics

- **Total Views —**The number of page views your video has. This does not mean that your recipient/s played your video, just that they opened your video page.
- **Total Plays —**The number of times your video was played
- **Total Clicks —**The number of times your call-to-action button was clicked
- **Total Likes —**The number of times the "thumbs up" button on your video was clicked

![Total Engagement Metrics panel showing Views, Plays, Clicks, and Likes counters](/images/5171294/8bef682c-image.png)

## Viewer Insights

- **Visitor —**Who is opening your page. We'll show their location, device, browser, and IP information to help you figure out who they are. Each line item represents a page session.
- **Play Icon —** How many times the visitor played your video in that session.
- **Click Icon —**How many times the visitor clicked your call-to-action button during their session.
- **Like Icon —**How many times the visitor liked the video during their session.
- **When —**The time the viewer performed their last interaction of the session (last play, thumbs up, etc.).

## FAQs

## 1. Will my views show up in Analytics?

We do our best to **scrub your views from the analytics**, but if you're not logged into Sendspark, your views might end up there, too. [Check your IP address](https://whatismyipaddress.com/) so you're able to identify yourself if you accidentally end up there. If you are currently logged into Sendspark, then don't worry, because if you want to preview a video, you'll just have this analytics banner saying that it won't be considered:

![Sendspark preview mode banner indicating analytics will not be recorded](/images/5171294/5e5060e0-image.png)

## 2. When does video analytics history start?

- You can see the **Total Engagement** data from the moment you created your Sendspark account
- You can see **Viewer Insights**starting on January 14th, 2022 (when we released that feature)

## 3. What is Sample Data?

You can **toggle on sample data** to see what video analytics would look like if you have video engagement and access to this feature. This is all fake data, just there to help you understand how the feature works.

![Sample data toggle option in analytics settings with example engagement data](/images/5171294/45f747c9-image.png)

## 4. What other data is in Sendspark?

Head over to your [Admin Dashboard](https://sendspark.com/admin) to see higher-level insights regarding your top-performing videos and team members.

![Sendspark Admin Dashboard displaying top-performing videos and team insights](/images/5171294/fd7478af-image.png)
