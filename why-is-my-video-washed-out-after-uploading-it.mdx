If your video colors look washed out after uploading, it's because Sendspark converts videos to the standard H.264 codec so they play on any device. Videos originally recorded in HEVC or another non-standard codec can shift color during that conversion. To avoid this, record your videos using the standard H.264 codec.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark converts uploaded videos to **H.264** so they play on any device (mobile and desktop, Windows, Android, Mac, TV, and others).
- Color changes happen when the original video used **HEVC** or another non-standard codec, because that codec is converted to the standard format.
- To prevent washed-out colors, record with the standard **H.264** codec from the start.
- Change your device's video capture codec to H.264 before recording (see the screenshot below).
