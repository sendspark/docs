This guide shows you how to integrate Sendspark with Lemlist to send automatically personalized videos at scale. You'll learn how to set up dynamic properties in your videos and embed them into your Lemlist email campaigns.

## Setting Up Dynamic Properties in Sendspark

First, you'll need to configure your video with personalization elements that will automatically change for each recipient.

1. Record or upload your video to Sendspark
2. Navigate to the video editing page
3. Click the **Personalize** button
4. Add dynamic properties such as:
  - First name
  - Company name
  - Job title
  - Other contact variables

## Customizing Your Video Thumbnail

You can also add personalization to your video thumbnail for maximum impact:

1. Go to the video thumbnail customization section
2. Use the same personalization options to add merge tags
3. Configure how the personalized elements will appear on the thumbnail

## Connecting to Lemlist

Once your video is configured with dynamic properties, you can easily integrate it with Lemlist.

1. Click the **Share Video** option in Sendspark
2. Select **Lemlist** from the integration options
3. If you don't see Lemlist in the list:
  - Click **Edit** then **Add**
  - Browse all available integrations
  - Select Lemlist from the complete list
4. Copy the integration code to your clipboard

## Adding Your Video to Lemlist Templates

Now you'll embed your personalized video into your Lemlist email campaigns.

1. Open Lemlist and navigate to your email template
2. Paste the Sendspark video code into your template
3. Adjust the video size and positioning as needed
4. Click the **Preview** button to test the personalization
5. Test with different contact names to ensure the dynamic properties work correctly

**Tip:** Always preview your video with different contact information to make sure the personalization is working properly before sending your campaign.

## Variable Mapping

The integration automatically maps common fields between Sendspark and Lemlist:

- First name variables sync automatically
- Company name fields align between platforms
- Job titles and other custom fields transfer seamlessly

**Note:** Make sure your Lemlist contact fields match the dynamic properties you've set up in Sendspark for optimal personalization results.
