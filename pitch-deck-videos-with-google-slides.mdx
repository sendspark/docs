## Using Google Slides as a Background in Sendspark Dynamic Videos

While Sendspark is built for video content, you can leverage its dynamic features to create personalized videos with Google Slides presentations as backgrounds.

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/4_JN5pNIlFc?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

## ​Here's how to achieve this:​

![Google Slides presentation open in browser showing pitch deck content](/images/9553068/2eedcd33-image.png)

## 1. Grab the Unique Slide URL:

- Open your Google Slides presentation
- Look at the address bar at the top of your browser window and copy the entire URL ![Google Slides URL highlighted in browser address bar for copying](/images/9553068/bea7694a-image.png)

## 2. Make the Slide Public:

- Make sure the slide is publicly viewable in Google Slides while the Dynamic Video Campaign is active.

![Google Slides share settings dialog with public access permissions enabled](/images/9553068/6bbf4d91-image.png)

## 3. Create your Sendspark Dynamic Video:

- Follow the usual process for [creating a dynamic video in Sendspark](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions).
- Make sure you have the dynamic background toggled **"On"**
- For Slides, you likely want to have the setting to ***scroll*** ***down***, but ***not back up***
- ![Sendspark dynamic video editor with background toggle and scroll settings](/images/9553068/423d5bb6-image.png)

## 4. Set the Google Slide as Background:

- When editing the Background URL field, paste the complete URL grabbed from the Shareable Option: ![Sendspark background URL field with Google Slides URL pasted in](/images/9553068/84e5c9aa-image.png)

## 5. Generate & Send your Personalized Video Message:

- With the Google Slide set as the background, Sendspark will personalize the message with your recipient data.
