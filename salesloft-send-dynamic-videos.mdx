To send dynamic videos in a Salesloft cadence, record one video in Sendspark and say **"watermelon"** wherever you want each recipient's first name to go, then toggle on **Voice Cloning**. Customize your share page, import your contacts to generate personalized videos, click **Share Campaign**, select **Salesloft**, and paste the snippet into a Salesloft email template. Each contact then receives a video personalized with their own name and website using AI.

## Key takeaways

- Record **one** video and Sendspark's AI voice cloning personalizes it for every contact, so you don't re-record per person.
- Say **"watermelon"** in your recording as a placeholder wherever you want the viewer's first name spoken; the AI swaps in each contact's real name.
- Toggle on **Voice Cloning** before generating, then import contacts, click **Share Campaign**, and select **Salesloft**.
- Paste the snippet into a Salesloft **email template**. Preview it against Salesloft's default contact "George" (g.p.burdell@example.net) before sending.

**Definition:** A dynamic video is one video, recorded once, that Sendspark automatically personalizes for each contact using merge variables and AI voice cloning.

## Quick steps

1. Record one video in Sendspark, saying **"watermelon"** where each viewer's first name should go (pause briefly after it).
2. Toggle on **Voice Cloning**.
3. Customize your dynamic video share page.
4. Import your contacts and generate the personalized videos.
5. Click **Share Campaign** and select **Salesloft**.
6. Paste the snippet directly into your **email template** in Salesloft.
7. Send! Each contact receives their own personalized video.

  <Accordion title="How do I personalize a video for each contact in Salesloft?">

    Yes. Use Salesloft's default contact "George" (whose email is g.p.burdell@example.net) to preview how the personalized video renders for a real contact.

  <Accordion title="How long should my recording be for AI voice cloning?">
