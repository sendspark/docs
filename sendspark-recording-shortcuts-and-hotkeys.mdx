Sendspark provides keyboard shortcuts so you can control recording without clicking buttons. In the desktop app, press Space to skip the countdown, ⌥+⌘+Q to exit, and ⌥+⌘+X to log out. In the Chrome Extension, ⇧+X toggles spotlight, and while recording you can delete, restart, pause, or stop with ⇧+⌥ combinations.

## Key takeaways

- **Desktop app:** Space skips the countdown, ⌥+⌘+Q exits, and ⌥+⌘+X logs out.
- **Chrome Extension:** ⇧+X toggles spotlight.
- While recording in the Chrome Extension, use ⇧+⌥+X to delete, ⇧+⌥+D to restart, ⇧+⌥+R to pause, and ⇧+⌥+S to stop the current recording.
- Shortcuts let you interact with Sendspark hands-free, without clicking toolbar buttons.

Sendspark provides shortcuts to help you interact with the Chrome Extension without clicking buttons.

## **Sendspark Desktop App Shortcuts:**

- Space - Skip countdown
- ⌥+⌘+Q - Exit
- ⌥+⌘+X - Logout

## **Sendspark Chrome Extension Shortcuts:**

- ⇧+X - Toggle spotlight
- While recording (recording toolbar options):
  - ⇧+⌥+X - Delete current recording
  - ⇧+⌥+D - Restart current recording
  - ⇧+⌥+R - Pause current recording
  - ⇧+⌥+S - Stop current recording
