Sendspark tracking snippets let CRMs and analytics tools measure the impact of your videos. Supported platforms include Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Meta Pixel, Pipedrive, Salesloft, Google Analytics, Service Bell, and LG&CRM. Connect one from the Integrations page by adding your unique platform ID, and Sendspark fills in the rest so video page visits are tracked like any page view.

## Key takeaways

- Tracking snippets connect Sendspark to your CRM or analytics platform so you can measure video's impact on your business.
- Supported platforms: Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, Meta Pixel (formerly Facebook Pixel), Pipedrive, Salesloft, Google Analytics, Service Bell, and LG&CRM (formerly SharpSpring).
- To connect, go to the Integrations page, click **Connect**, add your unique ID from the platform, and save — Sendspark fills in the rest of the snippet for you.
- Video page visits then get tracked like any website page view, so you can build reports and dashboards on engagement per lead.

Sendspark supports several CRM integrations & tracking snippets to help you measure the impact video has on your business.

## How to Connect Your Tracking Snippet:

![Sendspark Integrations page displaying list of supported CRM and tracking platforms](/images/5970988/afe34703-image.png)

To connect any of these integrations....

1. Navigate to the [Sendspark Integrations page](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations)
2. Click **Connect** next to the platform you're looking to connect to.
3. Add your unique ID from the platform and click **Save**
4. Behind the scenes, Sendspark fills in the rest of the tracking snippet for you
5. With the tracking code enabled, those page visits will get tracked in your CRM or analytics platform like any website page view.

This helps you **track video engagement** for individual leads and customers, and make detailed **reports and dashboards** to visualize the overall impact Sendspark is having on your business.
