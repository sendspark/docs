## Which Tracking Snippets Can You Use with Sendspark?

Sendspark currently supports tracking snippets from these platforms:

- Salesforce
- HubSpot
- Zoho
- Meta Pixel (formerly Facebook Pixel)
- Pipedrive
- Salesloft
- Google Analytics
- Service Bell
- LG&CRM (formerly SharpSpring)

---

## How to Connect Your Tracking Snippet:

![Sendspark Integrations page displaying list of supported CRM and tracking platforms](/images/5970988/afe34703-image.png)

To connect any of these integrations....

1. Navigate to the [Sendspark Integrations page](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations)
2. Click **Connect** next to the platform you're looking to connect to.
3. Add your unique ID from the platform and click **Save**
4. Behind the scenes, Sendspark fills in the rest of the tracking snippet for you
5. With the tracking code enabled, those page visits will get tracked in your CRM or analytics platform like any website page view.

This helps you **track video engagement** for individual leads and customers, and make detailed **reports and dashboards** to visualize the overall impact Sendspark is having on your business.
