You can turn any recipient's email address into a personalized video background using a Sendspark agentic workflow. Add a Data Cropping step that extracts the domain after the "@" (for example, dan@sendspark.com becomes sendspark.com), then feed that domain into the Background URL field of your Dynamic Video Campaign to generate branded backgrounds automatically.

## Key takeaways

- A three-part workflow powers this: a **trigger**, a **Data Cropping** step, and a **Dynamic Video Campaign** step.
- The **Data Cropping** tool extracts everything after the **"@"** in an email field — for example, `dan@sendspark.com` becomes `sendspark.com`.
- You insert that cropped domain into the **Background URL** field of your Dynamic Video Campaign step so the background becomes dynamic per contact.
- Any trigger works (a meeting booked, a form submission, a new contact created), making personalization at scale fully automated with no manual editing.

Turn any email address into a personalized background URL using agentic workflows.

## ⚙️ How It Works

You’ll build a simple workflow with three parts:

## 1. Trigger your workflow

Start with any trigger, for example:

- A meeting booked
- A form submission
- A new contact created

---

## 2. Extract the email domain (Data Cropping)

Click the ➕ between steps and select:

**Tool → Data Cropping**

In this step:

- Select the **email field**
- Configure the cropping to extract everything **after the “@”**

👉 Example:  
`dan@sendspark.com` → `sendspark.com`

This will give you a clean domain you can reuse dynamically.

---

## 3. Use it in your Dynamic Video Campaign

In your **Dynamic Video Campaign** step:

- Go to the **Background URL** field
- Insert the output from the Data Cropping step

Now your background becomes dynamic based on the email domain.

---

## 💡 Example Use Case

If your contact is:

- `john@nike.com` → background could be Nike-themed
- `sarah@shopify.com` → background could be Shopify-themed

This creates a more personalized and relevant video experience automatically.
