Turn any email address into a personalized background URL using agentic workflows.

This allows you to automatically generate branded or customized video backgrounds based on your contact’s email domain.

## ⚙️ How It Works

You’ll build a simple workflow with three parts:

## 1. Trigger your workflow

Start with any trigger, for example:

- A meeting booked
- A form submission
- A new contact created

---

## 2. Extract the email domain (Data Cropping)

Click the ➕ between steps and select:

**Tool → Data Cropping**

In this step:

- Select the **email field**
- Configure the cropping to extract everything **after the “@”**

👉 Example:  
`dan@sendspark.com` → `sendspark.com`

This will give you a clean domain you can reuse dynamically.

---

## 3. Use it in your Dynamic Video Campaign

In your **Dynamic Video Campaign** step:

- Go to the **Background URL** field
- Insert the output from the Data Cropping step

Now your background becomes dynamic based on the email domain.

---

## 💡 Example Use Case

If your contact is:

- `john@nike.com` → background could be Nike-themed
- `sarah@shopify.com` → background could be Shopify-themed

This creates a more personalized and relevant video experience automatically.

---

## 🚀 Why This Matters

- Personalization at scale
- No manual editing
- Works with any trigger
- Fully automated workflows

---

## ✅ Summary

By adding a **Data Cropping** step between your trigger and your video campaign, you can:

- Extract the email domain
- Convert it into a usable variable
- Power dynamic backgrounds automatically
