To delete a folder in Sendspark, first make sure it's empty by deleting the videos inside it or moving them to another folder. Then hover over the folder, click the ellipses that appears, and select **Delete**.

## Key takeaways

- A folder must be empty of all videos before it can be deleted.
- Empty a folder by deleting the videos inside it or moving them into a different folder.
- To delete, hover over the folder, click the ellipses, and select **Delete**.

You can delete folders anytime in Sendspark.

---

**Please note: your folder must be empty of all videos before you delete it.**

To empty your folder, you can either delete the videos inside or move them into a different folder.

---

## How to Delete a Folder

1. **Hover** over the folder you would like to delete
2. Click the **ellipses** that appears
3. Select **Delete**![Delete Folders](/images/6299801/5776f611-image.png)
