To send video emails in Intercom, create your video in Sendspark, click Share and then Copy Email Embed Code, open your Intercom email editor and switch to Edit HTML, then paste the embed code. Send a test to yourself to confirm it looks right. Sendspark automatically supplies a fallback preview for clients that don't support in-email video.

## Key takeaways

- You can embed videos in Intercom emails, and Sendspark automatically supplies a fallback preview so recipients on clients that don't support in-email video can still click to watch on the web.
- Embedding uses Intercom's Edit HTML mode: paste the Copy Email Embed Code snippet from Sendspark into the HTML of your email.
- Video works well for welcoming new users, announcing new features, and onboarding users with short how-to videos.
- Send a test to yourself first to confirm the video email looks right before sending.

Before we get started, let's address a few common questions:

## 🤔 Can you embed videos in Intercom emails?

Yes, you can embed videos in Intercom emails.

However, embedding videos in email is NOT the same as embedding videos on the web. With email, you have to take into account that not every email client (looking at you, Gmail 👀) supports playing video online, so you have to supply fallback scenarios.

([See which email clients support embedding videos in email](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos))

When you embed videos in emails with Sendspark, we automatically supply the fallback scenario, so when a video is not supported, your viewer will see a video preview, and be able to click to watch the video on the web.

**💌 When should you embed videos in Intercom emails?**

Videos are great for humanizing the customer experience. There are a few touch points where videos can add a ton of value, both for increasing email engagement, and for building personal relationships with your customers.

- Welcome new users
- Announce new features
- Onboard users with short "How to" videos

Okay, let's get get to it! Here's how to embed videos in Intercom Emails.

## How to embed videos in Intercom emails:

1. Make your video [in Sendspark](https://app.sendspark.com), by either recording a video, uploading a video, or requesting a video from someone else. Note, if you want to record your screen, you'll have to use the [Sendspark Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph?hl=en-US).

![Sendspark video editor interface with a recorded or uploaded video ready to share](/images/4384415/00e63f2d-Screen_Shot_2020-08-27_at_9.43.26_AM.png)

2. Click "Share" and then "Copy Email Embed Code"

![Sendspark share menu displaying Copy Email Embed Code option highlighted](/images/4384415/cdacda9b-Screen_Shot_2020-08-27_at_9.45.21_AM.png)

3. Go over to your Intercom Email Editor, and switch to "Edit HTML"

![Intercom email editor interface with HTML editing mode selected and active](/images/4384415/dd4f4040-Edit_HTML_in_Intercom.gif)

4. Paste your video email embed code there

![Intercom HTML editor with video embed code pasted into the email template](/images/4384415/836e42bf-Paste_email_embed_code.gif)

5. Send a test to yourself to make sure you're happy with your video email.

![Intercom email test preview showing the embedded video with play button visible](/images/4384415/68aab425-image.png)

6. It should should something like this!

![Final rendered Intercom email displaying embedded video in recipient inbox view](/images/4384415/344185b3-image.png)
