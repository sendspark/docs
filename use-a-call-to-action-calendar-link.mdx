title: "Use a Call-to-Action (Calendar Link)"
description: "Learn how to add calendar links as call-to-action buttons in your videos to boost meeting bookings and viewer engagement."
---

To add a calendar link as a call-to-action on your video, toggle on the Call-to-Action field, write your button text (such as "Schedule a Meeting"), then paste your calendar link from Calendly, HubSpot, Undock, or a similar tool into the URL field. You can confirm it looks right in the preview area on the right. Video CTAs require the Starter Plan or higher.

## Key takeaways

- Video CTAs (Call-To-Action) require the Starter Plan or a higher plan.
- Toggle on the Call-to-Action field, then set your button text and paste your calendar link.
- Calendar links from tools like Calendly, HubSpot, and Undock are supported in the URL field.
- Use the preview area on the right of your page to confirm the button looks right.

**Note**: you must be on the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or a higher plan to use video CTAs (Call-To-Action)

---

Follow these steps to add your calendar link to your video:

- **Toggle** on the Call-to-Action field

![Call to Action](/images/5566693/6f97cc32-image.png)

- Write your desired **button text**(ex: Schedule a Meeting)
- Add your calendar link (from Calendly, Hubspot, Undock, etc.) in the **URL field**

![Call to action display](/images/5566679/3ca14386-image.png)

You'll be able to check that everything looks good in the preview area on the right of your page 👍
