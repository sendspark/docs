Sendspark has three libraries: My Library, Team Library, and Request Videos. Only you can access My Library, while everyone on your account can access Team Library and Request Videos. To share a video with teammates, click the Move icon next to it, pick the destination library and folder, then click Move.

## Key takeaways

- You have three libraries: **My Library** (only you), **Team Library**, and **Request Videos**. Every team member on your account can access and manage videos in Team Library and Request Videos.
- By default, all videos recorded from the Chrome Extension appear in **My Library**.
- To let teammates view or edit a video you created, move it into the Team Library.
- Move a video by clicking the **Move** icon next to it, selecting the destination library and folder, and clicking **Move**; a toast confirms it moved successfully.

## **Why Move Videos Between Folders**

In Sendspark, you have 3 libraries: **(1) My Library**, **(2) Team Library**, and **(3) Request Videos**.

![Sendspark Video Libraries](/images/5512835/e756dc3e-image.png)

Only you can access videos in "**My Library"**.

However, every team member on your Sendspark account can access and manage videos in "**Team Library"**and**"Request Videos"**.

---

By default, all videos recorded from the Chrome Extension will appear in "**My Library".**

If you want other people on your team to be able to view or edit a video you've created, you can move it to the Team Library.

---

## How to Move Videos Between Folders

1. To move a video into another library click on the **Move** icon next to the video.

![Select Move Icon](/images/5512835/3975979b-image.png)

2. You'll see a modal pop up where you can select the library and folder where you want to organize your video.

![Select Destination Library](/images/5512835/333e2653-image.png)

3. When you have selected the new destination, click **Move**.

![Move Video](/images/5512835/2c0a091a-image.png)

4. You'll see a toast confirming that your video was moved successfully.

![Confirmation Toast](/images/5512835/39633c60-image.png)

That's it! Happy organizing!
