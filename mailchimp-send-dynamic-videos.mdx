Send dynamic Sendspark videos in Mailchimp by generating an integration code in your campaign and pasting it into a Mailchimp Code block. Each recipient automatically receives a personalized video based on your CSV data. Verify your videos are processed, click Share Campaign into MailChimp, drag a Code block into the email builder, paste the code, then send a test.

## Key takeaways

- Requires a Sendspark campaign with processed dynamic videos, a CSV of contact data uploaded to the campaign, and a Mailchimp account with campaign creation access.
- Generate the code with the **Share Campaign into MailChimp** button, then paste it into a **Code block** in Mailchimp's email builder.
- The dynamic video preview won't render in the Mailchimp editor; you'll see the main placeholder instead of each personalized version, which is normal.
- Send a test email to confirm the video displays correctly. The integration scales from 10 to 10,000 recipients, each getting their own personalized video.

This guide walks you through integrating Sendspark dynamic videos into your Mailchimp email campaigns. Dynamic videos automatically personalize content for each recipient, allowing you to create thousands of customized videos without manual work.

## Prerequisites

- A Sendspark campaign with processed dynamic videos
- CSV file with contact data uploaded to your campaign
- Active Mailchimp account with campaign creation access

## Step 1: Access Your Sendspark Campaign

1. Navigate to your Sendspark campaign dashboard
2. Verify your dynamic videos have been processed successfully
3. Confirm your CSV contact file has been uploaded

**Tip:** You can test individual videos by clicking on any contact to preview their personalized video with the selected background.

## Step 2: Generate Mailchimp Integration Code

1. In your Sendspark campaign, locate the **"Share Campaign into MailChimp"** button
2. Click the button to generate your integration code
3. Select **Mailchimp** from the sharing options
4. Copy the generated code

## Step 3: Add Code to Your Mailchimp Campaign

1. Open your Mailchimp campaign and navigate to the email builder
2. In the template editor, drag a **Code block** to your desired location
3. Click on the code block to open the editing panel
4. Paste the Sendspark integration code into the code section
5. Save your changes

**Note:** The dynamic video preview won't display correctly in the Mailchimp editor. You'll see the main video placeholder instead of the personalized version with backgrounds. This is normal behavior.

## Step 4: Test Your Dynamic Video Email

1. In your Mailchimp campaign, click **"Send a test email"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **"Send test"**
4. Check your inbox to verify the dynamic video displays correctly

## Final Result

Once delivered, recipients will see their personalized video with the custom background you've configured. The dynamic video will automatically display the content specific to each contact based on your CSV data.

**Tip:** This integration works seamlessly for large contact lists. Whether you're sending to 10 or 10,000 recipients, each person will receive their personalized video automatically.
