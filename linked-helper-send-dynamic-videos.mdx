## How to Send AI-Personalized Videos in LinkedIn Helper Automation

1. Create your [AI personalized videos](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions) in Sendspark
  1. When you add contacts into Sendspark, make sure to use the**"LinkedInUrl" field**in Sendspark for their LinkedIn Profile URLs. You'll need this to make sure the right person gets the right video later! ​![](/images/10470178/6cbd8258-image.png)
2. **Export CSV**of generated videos in Sendspark. ​![](/images/10470178/3797ed1f-image.png)
3. Upload this list to LinkedIn Helper, and use the Sendspark "Video Link" variable as a **custom variable in Linked Helper**
  1. Note: you will need to use a plugin to use [custom variables in Linked Helper](https://support.linkedhelper.com/hc/en-us/articles/9035773558034-Custom-template-variables-plug-in)
4. Use the Sendspark video link variable in Linked Helper where you want your video to appear in your LinkedIn connection requests and LinkedIn messages.
5. Although you're sending the Sendspark video as a link, the link will unfurl and show a nice preview when you're connected to your prospect. ​![](/images/10470178/fb001b68-image.png)
