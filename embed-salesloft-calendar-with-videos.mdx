Want to book more meetings for you Salesloft video outreach? You can embed your calendar with your video, so viewers can book immediately after watching, without ever leaving the page.

![Embedded Salesloft Calendar Next to Video](/images/9052061/a997c6c1-image.png)

## **How to Embed Salesloft Calendar Link Next To Videos**

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/ioL0S7yVYnk?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Under your Sendspark video, toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**
2. Select **"Salesloft"** from the Provider Dropdown List
3. **Add the link**to the Salesloft calendar you want to use

![Embed Salesloft Calendar with Video](/images/9052061/ba1ec7f6-image.png)

You should be able to see your embedded Salesloft calendar in the preview area!

![Preview Salesloft Embedded Calendar with Video](/images/9052061/6511ab95-image.png)
