To embed your Salesloft calendar next to a Sendspark video, toggle **Calendar** to **On** under your video, select **Salesloft** from the provider dropdown, and paste your Salesloft calendar link. Sendspark builds the embed automatically, so viewers can book a meeting the moment they finish watching, without ever leaving the page.

## Key takeaways

- Turn the **Calendar** toggle **On** under your Sendspark video, choose **Salesloft** as the provider, and paste your Salesloft calendar link.
- Paste the calendar **link**, not an embed code. Sendspark builds the embed for you.
- The embedded calendar appears next to your video in the preview area, so viewers can book without leaving the page.
- Save your calendar in a [Template](/how-to-embed-calendars-with-videos) to add it to future videos automatically instead of pasting the link each time.

Want to book more meetings for you Salesloft video outreach? You can embed your calendar with your video, so viewers can book immediately after watching, without ever leaving the page.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I paste the Salesloft calendar link or an embed code?">
    Use the **link** to your Salesloft calendar, not an embed code. Sendspark automatically builds the embed using the information from the link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where does the embedded calendar appear for viewers?">
    Next to your video on the Sendspark video page. Viewers can book a meeting immediately after watching, without leaving the page. You'll see it in the preview area as soon as you add the link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I add the calendar to every video without redoing the setup?">
    Yes. Save your calendar in a [Template](/how-to-embed-calendars-with-videos), then select that template after recording. This avoids copying your calendar link every time you create a video.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I use a scheduling tool other than Salesloft?">
    Sendspark also supports Calendly, Chili Piper, HubSpot, HighLevel, Mixmax, Motion, Outreach, Tidycal, and Youcanbookme. See [How to Embed Calendars with Videos](/how-to-embed-calendars-with-videos). If your platform isn't listed, you can request a direct integration or add your booking link to a call-to-action button in the meantime.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
