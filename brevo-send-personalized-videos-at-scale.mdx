To send personalized videos at scale in Brevo, first create a dynamic video campaign in Sendspark and generate videos for your contact list. Then click **Share Campaign**, copy the **Brevo** snippet, and paste it into an HTML snippet block in your Brevo email template. Preview for a contact to confirm the right video loads, then send — each recipient receives their own personalized video.

## Key takeaways

- A **dynamic video campaign** is a Sendspark campaign that generates a unique personalized video for each contact in your list, so you can send them at scale through Brevo.
- Generate the videos in Sendspark first, then click **Share Campaign** and copy the **Brevo** snippet.
- Paste the snippet into an **HTML snippet block** in your Brevo email template — it renders each contact's own personalized video thumbnail.
- Always **preview for a contact** before sending to confirm the correct personalized video loads.

## How to Create and Share AI Personalized Videos in Brevo

1. Create a [dynamic video campaign in Sendspark](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions) & generate videos for your list of contacts
2. Click **"Share Campaign"** ​![](/images/10254553/f84d2b08-image.png)
3. Copy the snippet for **Brevo** ​![](/images/10254553/5921068a-image.png)
4. Paste into an HTML snippet in your Brevo email template ​![](/images/10254553/2a67933f-image.png)
5. Preview for a contact to make sure everything is working ​![](/images/10254553/c8a52f53-image.png)

And that's it! You're ready to send!
