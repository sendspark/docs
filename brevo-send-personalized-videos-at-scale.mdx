## How to Create and Share AI Personalized Videos in Brevo

1. Create a [dynamic video campaign in Sendspark](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions) & generate videos for your list of contacts
2. Click **"Share Campaign"** ​![](/images/10254553/f84d2b08-image.png)
3. Copy the snippet for **Brevo** ​![](/images/10254553/5921068a-image.png)
4. Paste into an HTML snippet in your Brevo email template ​![](/images/10254553/2a67933f-image.png)
5. Preview for a contact to make sure everything is working ​![](/images/10254553/c8a52f53-image.png)

And that's it! You're ready to send!
