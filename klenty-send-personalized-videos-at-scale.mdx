To send personalized videos in Klenty, record or upload your video in Sendspark, then customize the header, message, and thumbnail with placeholders that pull in each contact's details from Klenty. Click Share Video and select Klenty, paste the snippet into a Klenty email draft, then preview and send.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark automatically pulls in your viewer's first name, company name, and job title using placeholders.
- Record or upload your video in your Sendspark video library, then personalize the video header, message, and thumbnail.
- Share by clicking Share Video and selecting Klenty; if you don't see Klenty, modify your share modal shortcuts.
- Paste the snippet directly into a Klenty email draft, then preview to confirm the personalization renders before you send.

Send personalized videos in Klenty emails to cut through the noise and connect at scale.

Sendspark makes it easy to automatically pull in your viewer's first name, company name, and job title, just follow the steps below!

## 1. Record or upload your video

Record or upload your video in Sendspark [your Sendspark video library](https://sendspark.com/my-videos)

![Record or upload video in Sendspark](/images/6676528/d1dc5ed7-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_4.45.49_PM.png)

## 2. Personalize content around video

Customize the **video header**, **message**, and **thumbnail**. Feel free to use placeholders to automatically pull in contact information from Klenty when you send your video!

![Personalize video](/images/6676528/e4302b71-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_4.46.27_PM.png)

## 3. Copy share snippet for Klenty

Click **"Share Video,"** and then select Klenty (if you don't see Klenty, follow these steps to [modify your share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal)).

## 4. Paste in Klenty

Just paste directly into a Klenty email draft

![Paste video in Klenty](/images/6649438/cf460489-image.png)

## 5. Preview and send!

If you've customized your video thumbnail, you should be able to see the personalization when you preview videos in Klenty

![Send personalized video in Klenty](/images/6649438/eb24d91f-image.png)
