You can use Sendspark and Gong Engage together to create hyper-personalized videos outreach.

We recommend you watch the video below the first time you set up this automation. Let us know if you need any help!

## How to Send AI-Personalized Videos in Gong Engage

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/bd-z1uJid-Q?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

First create your dynamic video campaign in Sendspark. Leave placeholders for where your viewer's first name, or other dynamic fields should go.

![Sendspark dynamic video campaign with placeholder fields for personalization](/images/11382784/148094e6-image.png)

Then, create an automated workflow that pushes the Sendspark email embed snippet to your CRM after videos are created.

This works with Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho, HighLevel, and Close CRM. Just make sure your CRM is synced with Gong, so you can use this field in Gong!

![Automated workflow configuration showing CRM integration options](/images/11382784/f0c9dc1e-image.png)

You can set this to trigger when all videos from your Sendspark workspace are generated.

![Trigger settings panel configured for all videos generated event](/images/11382784/71de739c-image.png)

And then map the Sendspark "HTML Embed Email Snippet" to field in your CRM like "Sendspark Embed Embed Snippet"

![Field mapping interface showing Sendspark HTML embed snippet to CRM field](/images/11382784/f448628a-image.png)

(You could also have this automated trigger just from one dynamic video campaign, and map it to a specific field, if you want to have more than one Sendspark dynamic video snippet in your CRM saved at one time).

Once the Sendspark email embed snippet is saved in your CRM, you will be able to use that CRM field in your Gong Engage emails to insert the video.

![Gong Engage email editor with CRM field containing Sendspark video embed](/images/11382784/a004e280-image.png)

Now, when you add contacts to your Gong Engage flows, you'll see their videos in the email.

![Gong Engage email preview displaying personalized video from Sendspark](/images/11382784/214d9bbc-image.png)
