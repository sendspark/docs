If your Dynamic Videos fail to generate when using a LinkedIn profile background URL, it's usually because your LinkedIn cookie was disconnected. Fix it by clearing the LinkedIn cookie in your browser's developer tools, logging back in, copying the new cookie, and pasting it into your Dynamic Video campaign. To avoid it, generate only 20-30 videos a day.

## Key takeaways

- Videos failing to generate with a LinkedIn background URL are usually caused by a disconnected LinkedIn cookie.
- To fix it, disconnect the current cookie, then clear the LinkedIn cookie in your browser's dev tools (**Application → LinkedIn**), log back in, and copy the new cookie.
- Paste the new cookie into your Dynamic Video campaign and try generating the video again.
- Prevent recurrence by generating only 20-30 videos a day and not over-extending the number of profiles you request.

4. Click on APPLICATION header and look for the LINKEDIN cookie on the left-hand side panel

6. Log back in to LinkedIn and look for the new COOKIE.
7. Copy that and paste it in your Dynamic Video campaign
8. Try to generate the video again

## How to avoid this from happening again?

-Do not over-extend the number of profiles you're requesting to avoid a disconnection of your cookie.-Generate between 20-30 videos a day.

The more videos you generate, the more likely you are to provoke LinkedIn to disable the cookie.
