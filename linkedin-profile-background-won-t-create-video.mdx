This video will help you process videos that are failing to generate when you have a LinkedIn background URL.

1. Disconnect your current Cookie
2. Head to LinkedIn and access YOUR PROFILE from your menu
3. Right-click on an empty space and access your inspect tools
4. Click on APPLICATION header and look for the LINKEDIN cookie on the left-hand side panel
5. Right-click it and hit CLEAR ![Browser developer tools showing APPLICATION header with LINKEDIN cookie selected for clearing](/images/9921973/24a3a7e4-Refresh_cookie.gif)
6. Log back in to LinkedIn and look for the new COOKIE.
7. Copy that and paste it in your Dynamic Video campaign
8. Try to generate the video again

---

## How to avoid this from happening again?

-Do not over-extend the number of profiles you're requesting to avoid a disconnection of your cookie.-Generate between 20-30 videos a day.

The more videos you generate, the more likely you are to provoke LinkedIn to disable the cookie.
