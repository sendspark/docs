---
title: "What Video Formats Can I Upload to Sendspark?"
description: "Learn which video formats work with Sendspark uploads. MP4 and MOV are supported - discover all compatible file types here."
---

## Video formats that WILL upload to Sendspark:

- .mp4
- .mov

## Video formats that will NOT upload to Sendspark:

- .avi
- .wmv
- .3gp
- .flv
- .mkv
- .mpeg
- .mpg
- .mts
- .vob
- .m4v

(If you need to upload one of these formats, [make a request](mailto:hello@sendspark.com) and we'll see what we can do for you).

## Using another file format?

Let us know and we will test it for you!
