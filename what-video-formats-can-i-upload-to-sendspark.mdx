Sendspark accepts **.mp4** and **.mov** video files for upload. Formats such as .avi, .wmv, .3gp, .flv, .mkv, .mpeg, .mpg, .mts, .vob, and .m4v will not upload. If you need to use one of the unsupported formats, [email us](mailto:hello@sendspark.com) and we'll see what we can do.

## Key takeaways

- Only **.mp4** and **.mov** files will upload to Sendspark.
- Common formats that will **not** upload include .avi, .wmv, .3gp, .flv, .mkv, .mpeg, .mpg, .mts, .vob, and .m4v.
- Need one of the unsupported formats? [Make a request](mailto:hello@sendspark.com) and the team will see what they can do.
- Using a file format not listed here at all? Let the team know and they'll test it for you.

## Video formats that WILL upload to Sendspark:

- .mp4
- .mov

## Video formats that will NOT upload to Sendspark:
