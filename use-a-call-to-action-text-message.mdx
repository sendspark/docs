To add a "Text Me" button to your video, toggle on the Call-to-Action field, set the button text (such as "Text Me"), and in the URL field enter **sms:** followed by your phone number (for example, `sms:1234567890`). Viewers can then tap the button to text you directly. This requires the Starter Plan or higher.

## Key takeaways

- Video CTAs require the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or higher.
- Toggle on the Call-to-Action field, then set your button text (for example, "Text Me").
- In the URL field, enter **sms:** followed by your phone number, like `sms:1234567890`.
- Check the preview area on the right of the page to confirm the button looks correct before sharing.

**Note**: you must be on the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or a higher plan to use video CTAs (Call-To-Action)

## How do I format the phone number for a text-message button?

Enter the number in the URL field as **sms:** immediately followed by the digits, with no spaces, like `sms:1234567890`. The `sms:` prefix is what tells the viewer's device to open a text message to you when they tap the button.
