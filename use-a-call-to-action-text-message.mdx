---
title: "Use a Call-to-Action (Text Message)"
description: "Add a Text Me call-to-action button to your Sendspark videos with SMS integration. Configure text message CTAs using simple URL formatting for viewers."
---

**Note**: you must be on the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or a higher plan to use video CTAs (Call-To-Action)

---

Follow these steps to add a "Text Me" button to your videos:

- **Toggle** on the Call-to-Action field

![Call to Action](/images/5566693/6f97cc32-image.png)

- Write your desired **button text**(ex: Text Me)
- Write**sms:** and **your phone number** where it says **URL**(ex: sms:1234567890)

![Call to Action display](/images/5566693/a5e47316-image.png)

You'll be able to check that everything looks good in the preview area on the right of your page 👍
