On the Sendspark Starter plan, get set up by doing four things: set your Global Styles with your logo and brand colors, turn on a video Call-to-Action, download videos as .MP4 files, and record unlimited videos. Each is configured from your workspace settings or the video share modal.

## Key takeaways

- **Set Global Styles** in your [workspace settings](https://app.sendspark.com/settings/workspace) to apply your logo and brand colors across every video.
- **Use a Call-to-Action** to convert viewers: add a [calendar link](/use-a-call-to-action-calendar-link), [phone number](/use-a-call-to-action-phone-number), [text message](/use-a-call-to-action-text-message), or [URL link](/use-a-call-to-action-url-link).
- **Download videos** as .MP4 files from the share modal to edit or share them anywhere.
- **Record unlimited videos** on Starter, and [invite team members](/invite-team-members) to record more.

## 1. Set Global Styles

Use your own logo and brand colors to make videos look polished and professional.

Go to your [workspace settings](https://app.sendspark.com/settings/workspace), and click **Set Global Styles.**

Here, you can upload your logo, and change all of the color fields.

![Global Styles modal](/images/5566759/01c8fd7b-image.png)

**Save changes** to see your new styles appear for all videos in your workspace.

## 2. Use Video Call-To-Action

Toggle on the Call-to-Action feature to convert viewers after they watch your video. Here, you can add a [calendar link](/use-a-call-to-action-calendar-link), [phone number](/use-a-call-to-action-phone-number), [text message](/use-a-call-to-action-text-message), or [URL link](/use-a-call-to-action-url-link).

![Edit page](/images/5566759/d28c99df-image.png)

## 3. Download Videos

Download your videos as .MP4 files to edit or share them anywhere. Just launch the share modal, and then **Download**

![Download option](/images/5566759/1ae8ec32-image.png)

## 4. Unlimited Videos

Nothing can stop you now -- certainly not a 30-video limit! Record unlimited videos, and [invite team members](/invite-team-members) to record even more 🙌

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I apply my brand's look to every video?">
    Go to your [workspace settings](https://app.sendspark.com/settings/workspace) and click **Set Global Styles**. Upload your logo and change the color fields, then save changes to see your new styles appear across all videos in your workspace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What kinds of call-to-action can I add?">
    You can toggle on a Call-to-Action and add a [calendar link](/use-a-call-to-action-calendar-link), a [phone number](/use-a-call-to-action-phone-number), a [text message](/use-a-call-to-action-text-message), or a [URL link](/use-a-call-to-action-url-link) to convert viewers after they watch.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I download my videos?">
    Yes. Launch the share modal and click **Download** to save your video as an .MP4 file, so you can edit or share it anywhere.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit on how many videos I can record?">
    No. The Starter plan lets you record unlimited videos, and you can [invite team members](/invite-team-members) to record even more.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
