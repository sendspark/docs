## 1. Set Global Styles

Use your own logo and brand colors to make videos look polished and professional.

Go to your [workspace settings](https://app.sendspark.com/settings/workspace), and click **Set Global Styles.**

Here, you can upload your logo, and change all of the color fields.

![Global Styles modal](/images/5566759/01c8fd7b-image.png)

**Save changes** to see your new styles appear for all videos in your workspace.

## 2. Use Video Call-To-Action

Toggle on the Call-to-Action feature to convert viewers after they watch your video. Here, you can add a [calendar link](/use-a-call-to-action-calendar-link), [phone number](/use-a-call-to-action-phone-number), [text message](/use-a-call-to-action-text-message), or [URL link](/use-a-call-to-action-url-link).

![Edit page](/images/5566759/d28c99df-image.png)

## 3. Download Videos

Download your videos as .MP4 files to edit or share them anywhere. Just launch the share modal, and then **Download**

![Download option](/images/5566759/1ae8ec32-image.png)

## 4. Unlimited Videos

Nothing can stop you now -- certainly not a 30-video limit! Record unlimited videos, and [invite team members](/invite-team-members) to record even more 🙌
