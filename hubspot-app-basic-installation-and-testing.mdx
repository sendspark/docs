The Sendspark HubSpot app syncs video engagement data into HubSpot so you can see how contacts interact with your videos, build workflows off that engagement, and report on pipeline impact. Connect it from your Sendspark Integrations tab, send videos using a HubSpot share option so tracking is attached, then confirm the events appear on the contact's Timeline.

## Key takeaways

- Connect the HubSpot app from your [Sendspark Integrations tab](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations) to sync engagement data between the two.
- Always send using a **HubSpot** share option (from the normal or dynamic share modal) so videos carry HubSpot tracking parameters.
- Confirm it's working by checking the contact **Timeline** for Sendspark events, with "Sendspark" selected in the Activity list.
- Use the synced data to build lists, workflows, and reports — for example, follow up with contacts who watched 80%+ but haven't booked a meeting.

Sendspark has a HubSpot App that helps you easily sync data between Sendspark and HubSpot. This helps you...

- See how HubSpot contacts are engaging with Sendspark videos
- Create workflows & sales automation based on Sendspark engagement (ex: reach out to contacts who watched 80%+ of a video, but don't click the video call-to-action)
- Build reports to see how Sendspark videos are impacting pipeline & revenue.

## Frequently Asked Questions

## Q: Do I need to send videos in HubSpot to see analytics in HubSpot?

**A:**You do NOT need to send emails in HubSpot to see the analytics in HubSpot, BUT you do need to make sure that Sendspark tracking code is on your video.

If you just "copy the link" or "copy GIF" to share your video, you will not have any tracking code on your video, and you will not be able to see analytics.

However, if you use any of our [personalization at scale integrations](https://help.sendspark.com/en/collections/3620374-email-share-with-personalization) (with Apollo, Outreach, Instantly, etc.) or share any [dynamic videos](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions) (which automatically have tracking) then you will be able to see the engagement data in HubSpot.
