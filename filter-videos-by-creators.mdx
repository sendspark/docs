You can easily filter videos by Creators in your Sendspark workspace,

---

## Note: you can only see videos made by other team members if they are in the Team Library.

Videos created in someone's personal library are private, and will not show up here. If a team member wishes, they can easily [move videos](/how-to-move-videos-between-folders) between their personal and team video library.

---

## How to Filter Videos by Creator

1. Navigate to your [Team Library](https://sendspark.com/team-library)
2. Under **Creator**, select the team members you would like to see videos from. ![Filter Videos by Creator](/images/6299956/d9b69d13-image.png)
3. Now, only their videos will show up below.
