---
title: "Permissions missing"
description: "Troubleshooting steps for permissions missing"
---

If you've been able to record with our Chrome extension without any issues, but suddenly clicking **Record** does nothing, the recording timer stays at **0:00**, and the extension displays a "Permissions Missing"  message, it's possible that your operating system has revoked Chrome's permissions.

In many cases, simply re-enabling the permissions isn't enough. A full permissions reset may be required.

Please follow the steps below to reset the permissions:

![](/images/15879611/f262bfdb-image.png)

Open your extension menu and click on Manage Extensions:

![](/images/15879611/376c313e-image.png)

Find Sendspark and click on details:

![](/images/15879611/d9b2aa03-image.png)

Find the option that says Site Settings:

![](/images/15879611/eda63fa2-image.png)

And finally, click on Reset Permissions.

![](/images/15879611/43ff689b-image.png)

Now open a new tab and attempt to record a video again.

The system will prompt you to accept the corresponding permissions for both the camera and the microphone

![](/images/15879611/333e5f1d-image.png)

![](/images/15879611/69f16b65-image.png)

Please be sure to accept them both. Once it's done, click away to close the Chrome extension, reopen it, and everything should be ready.
