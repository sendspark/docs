In this guide, we'll walk through how to automatically create and send a video when someone videos your website.

Whether you use RB2B, Warmly, Vector, or another tool, this guide should work for you.

## 1. Create New Automation in Sendspark

- Go to the [automated workflow](https://sendspark.com/automated-workflows) section of Sendspark
- Create**"New Automation"** ​![Purple outlined New automation button](/images/11173898/5e0ee9d3-image.png)

## 2. Add Your Trigger: Website Visit

There are a few ways to do this. The simplest way is to simply add a webhook from your website de-anonymization tool to the trigger.

When you use the webhook as the trigger, it means that any time this webhook fires (as in a website visitor is identified), it will trigger this workflow.

![Set up a trigger dialog with Webhook app and incoming webhook event](/images/11173898/d04794b1-image.png)

This is great, but could also mean that you're catching website visitors who are unqualified, or who are already customers.

The more advanced way to set this up would be to create a list or a property in your CRM for "qualified recipients" and then use that as the trigger here.

![Step 1 trigger set to HubSpot New Contact in List](/images/11173898/3d2e095a-image.png)

Either way will work, just do what you're most comfortable with!

## 3. Add Action: Generate Sendspark Video

Next, add the action **Sendspark** and select **Create Dynamic Video.**

Make sure to map the fields from the previous step that you would like to use in the video (first name, email, etc).

![Map your fields dialog for Sendspark Create dynamic video action](/images/11173898/56ab1d26-image.png)

Now, when leads meet the trigger criteria, they will have personalized videos made for them!

## 4. Send Video

Add another action to send your video out. You can send your video directly in an email, LinkedIn message, text message, or another message.

![Three-step workflow with HubSpot trigger, Sendspark video, and Gmail send](/images/11173898/e407b70c-image.png)

You can also add your lead into an automated email or LinkedIn campaign

(read more guides on s[haring automated video campaign here](https://help.sendspark.com/en/collections/12626407-automatically-send-videos)!).
