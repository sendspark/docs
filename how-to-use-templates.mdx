Sendspark templates let you pre-write your header, message, and call-to-action content so you can send videos faster instead of retyping the same copy. Create templates in the Sendspark Templates library, then apply one when recording by clicking **Select Template** in the Chrome extension preview modal. Your video share page is pre-filled automatically.

## Key takeaways

- Templates pre-write the **header**, **message**, and **call-to-action** so you don't paste the same content below every video.
- Create and manage templates in your [Sendspark Templates library](https://sendspark.com/templates); suggested text is available if you're not sure what to say.
- Apply a template by clicking **Select Template** in the Sendspark Chrome extension preview modal when you record.
- Templates are shared across your whole workspace, so name workspace-specific ones clearly (for example, "My Calendar - Autumn").

## Why Use Templates?

Sick of pasting the same call-to-action below each video?

Templates let you pre-write call-to-action, header, and message content, so you can send videos much faster!

## Templates FAQ

## Q: Who can see templates I’ve created?

A: Everyone in your workspace can see all video templates created for that workspace. (So if you have a template called “My Calendar” that automatically adds your calendar to videos, make sure to add your name – i.e. “My Calendar - Autumn”

## Q: If I update my template, what happens to videos I’ve sent out with that template?

If you update your template, the previous videos you’ve sent out will NOT be affected. The updated template will only affect new videos using that template.
