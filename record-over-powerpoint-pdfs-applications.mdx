We typically recommend using the [Sendspark Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph) to record videos, because it's super fast to use from Chrome, and you get access to our[instant integrations](https://help.sendspark.com/en/collections/3341935-instant-integrations) with Gmail, LinkedIn, and other messaging platforms.

However, the Chrome Extension does *not* let you record yourself over PowerPoint, PDFs, or other applications. With the Chrome Extension, your camera bubble is restricted your Chrome browser.

**To record yourself on applications outside of Chrome, you need to use the Sendspark desktop recorder.**

The Sendspark Desktop Recorder lets you...

- Record over PowerPoint, PDF, and other applications on your computer
- Record over Slides in "fullscreen" mode
- Record multiple videos back-to-back, without waiting for the first video to upload

(If you do not have the Sendspark Desktop Recorder already,**[install it here](https://www.sendspark.com/download-desktop-application))**.

## How to Record Yourself Over PowerPoint

![Sendspark Desktop Recorder](/images/8505212/d418ad5c-image.png)

1. Install the [Sendspark Desktop App](https://www.sendspark.com/download-desktop-application)
2. Select **"Camera + Screen"** as the recording option
3. Open your PowerPoint presentation
4. Click **"Start Recording"**
