## Why Embed Videos in ActiveCampaign Emails

Sending videos in email can increase engagement and click-through-rates up to 400%. Whether you're embedding a personal video, product demo, or customer testimonial, follow the steps below to send videos in your ActiveCampaign emails.

## Will Embedded Videos from ActiveCampaign Play In Email?

When you use Sendspark email embed code, the video will play in email for recipients IF their email clients support embedded videos. You can see the complete list of [which email clients support embedded video](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos) here.

## What if Embedded Video is Not Supported?

If embedded video is not supported for your recipients — no worries! Sendspark will automatically generate a GIF preview for your video, and your recipients can just click on the video preview to open the video in their browsers.
