To send video emails in Customer.io, create a new email and drag in an HTML content block. Then record or upload your video in Sendspark, click Share, copy the email embed code, and paste it into your Customer.io HTML block. The video plays in-email wherever it's supported, with a clickable fallback image where it isn't.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark embeds your video so it plays directly in-email wherever the recipient's email client supports it.
- Where video isn't supported, Sendspark supplies a fallback preview image that links to an external landing page.
- The steps: create a Customer.io email, drag in an HTML content block, make your video in Sendspark, copy the email embed code, and paste it into the HTML block.
- Always send a test email to yourself to check how the video appears across different email providers.

Videos are a great way to make your emails more engaging and effective. Especially if you're using Customer.io to onboard new users, videos will help your customers see exactly what steps they should be taking - right from their inbox!

In this article, we will show you how to **embed videos in your Customer.io email.** This means that the video will play directly in-email wherever it is supported.

In cases where video is not supported in email, Sendspark supplies a fallback image, so your recipient will see a preview image of the video, and be able to click to play the video on an external landing page. ([See which email clients support video](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos)).

## Let's take a look at how to embed videos in Customer.io emails:

## 1. Create a new email in Customer.io

[Log in](https://fly.customer.io/login) to Customer.io and create a new email. You can use any of their email options: drag-and-drop, rich text, code, or a pre-built template. All emails will work!

## 2. Drag in an HTML content block

![Drag Video Into Customer.io](/images/4396835/ab3c3eb6-Screen_Recording_2020-09-01_at_09.02_AM.gif)

## 3. Make your video in Sendspark

Record, request, or upload your video in Sendspark. If you don't have an account yet, [sign up here](https://app.sendspark.com/signup).

## 4. Click "share," and then copy the email embed code

![Share Video in Customer.io](/images/4396835/b44d3c05-Screen_Recording_2020-09-01_at_09.12_AM.gif)

## 5. Paste the code snippet into your Customer.io HTML block

![Create Video Email in Customer.io](/images/4396835/7cd9d19b-Screen_Recording_2020-09-01_at_09.20_AM.gif)

That's it!

Always make sure to send a test email to yourself to make sure you're happy with how the video shows up in different email providers.
