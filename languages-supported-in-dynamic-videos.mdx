Sendspark AI Voice Cloning supports 10 languages for dynamic videos: English (US), Danish, Dutch, French, German, Italian, Polish, Portuguese (Brazil), Spanish (Spain), and Swedish. Each has a starting phrase built around the word "watermelon." You can also select OTHER to test an unlisted language, though results may vary.

## Key takeaways

- Ten languages are officially supported: English (US), Danish, Dutch, French, German, Italian, Polish, Portuguese (Brazil), Spanish (Spain), and Swedish.
- Each language has its own starting phrase — for English it's "Hey, watermelon."
- The **OTHER** option lets you test a language that isn't listed, because the AI is flexible and adjusts to different scenarios, though results may vary.
- The format for OTHER is a short greeting in your language + "Watermelon" said in English (for example, "Hey" + "Watermelon").

In this article you'll find all supported languages and their starting phrase to use with AI Voice Cloning:

1. 🇺🇸 **English (United States)** – “Hey, watermelon”
2. 🇩🇰 **Danish (Denmark)** – “Hej vandmelon”
3. 🇳🇱 **Dutch (Netherlands)** – “Hoi watermeloen”
4. 🇫🇷 **French (France)** – “Salut pastèque”
5. 🇩🇪 **German (Germany)** – “Hallo wassermelone”
6. 🇮🇹 **Italian (Italy)** – “Ciao anguria”
7. 🇵🇱 **Polish (Poland)** – “Cześć granatowiec”
8. 🇧🇷 **Portuguese (Brazil)** – “Olá melancia”
9. 🇪🇸 **Spanish (Spain)** – “Hola manzana”
10. 🇸🇪 **Swedish (Sweden)** – “Hej vattenmelon”

The option OTHER is there because our AI is flexible and it adjust to different scenarios. You can definintely test a different language if it is not in the list however, results may vary.

The format for OTHER is:  Hey + Watermelon. The "Hey" must be a short greeting in your language + Watermelon needs to be said in English
