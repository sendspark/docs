title: "How to Review and Pay our Invoices"
description: "Learn how to access, review, and pay invoices through your billing account menu with step-by-step instructions for online payments."
---

In this article, you will learn how to access your invoices

1) Head to your account Menu and click on BILLING:

![Billing menu](/images/8856714/ba5b04ca-image.png)

2) Scroll all the way down and click on the arrow to the right to deploy the invoices:

![Billing History](/images/8856645/cb0e9746-image.png)

3) You can then View the Invoice online or download the invoice:

![Invoices](/images/8856645/13827907-image.png)

4) If you need to pay an Invoice manually, click on VIEW INVOICE and then head to Pay Invoice Online.

![Pay Invoice Online](/images/8856645/20e6904a-image.png)
