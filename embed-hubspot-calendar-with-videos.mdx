Easily embed your HubSpot Meeting calendar next to your Sendspark videos, so viewers can schedule meetings without ever leaving your video

![Embedded HubSpot Meeting Link with Video](/images/9052029/568de416-image.png)

## How to Embed a HubSpot Meeting Link with Videos

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/2XGBcabiHiY?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Under your Sendspark video, toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**
2. Select **"HubSpot"** from the Provider Dropdown List
3. **Add the link**to the HubSpot calendar you want to use

![Embed HubSpot Meeting Link Next to Video](/images/9052029/dc38028f-image.png)

You should be able to see your embedded HubSpot calendar in the preview area!

![Preview HubSpot Meeting Link with Video](/images/9052029/87b87422-image.png)
