To embed a HubSpot Meeting calendar next to your Sendspark video, toggle Calendar to On under your video, select HubSpot from the Provider dropdown, then add the link to the HubSpot calendar you want to use. Your embedded calendar appears in the preview area, letting viewers schedule meetings without leaving your video page.

## Key takeaways

- You can embed a HubSpot Meeting calendar directly next to your Sendspark video so viewers schedule meetings without ever leaving the video.
- Setup takes three steps: toggle Calendar to On, select HubSpot from the Provider dropdown, and add your HubSpot calendar link.
- Your embedded calendar shows up in the preview area so you can confirm it looks right before sharing.

## How to Embed a HubSpot Meeting Link with Videos

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/2XGBcabiHiY?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Under your Sendspark video, toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**
2. Select **"HubSpot"** from the Provider Dropdown List
3. **Add the link**to the HubSpot calendar you want to use

![Embed HubSpot Meeting Link Next to Video](/images/9052029/dc38028f-image.png)

You should be able to see your embedded HubSpot calendar in the preview area!
