To send AI-personalized videos in Amplemarket, create one Dynamic Video campaign in Sendspark, import your contacts, then click **Share Campaign** and select **Amplemarket** to get a snippet you paste into an Amplemarket email. Each recipient receives a video with their own name and website, and you can track views in Sendspark analytics.

## Key takeaways

- You only record **one** placeholder video in Sendspark; Amplemarket sends each recipient a version with their own name and website.
- Build the campaign in the [Dynamic Video section](https://sendspark.com/dynamic-videos) with options like AI voice cloning and simulated website scrolling.
- Add contacts by typing data manually, importing a CSV, or generating videos via automation (Zapier, API, etc.).
- Share by clicking **Share Campaign → Amplemarket**, then pasting the snippet into an Amplemarket email.
- Track who watched and how much via **See Engagement** at the bottom of the Dynamic Video Builder.

## How to Send AI-Personalized Videos in Amplemarket

## 1. Create Dynamic Video Campaign in Sendspark

Go to the [dynamic video section](https://sendspark.com/dynamic-videos) of Sendspark.

Click **"Create Dynamic Video"** to create a new campaign.

![Add Video to Dynamic Campaign](/images/9146071/4a84587a-image.png)

From here, you can add a video, and select options to...

- Use AI to clone your voice, so you can automatically greet each viewer by name
- Simulate scrolling through different website pages
- and more!

You can also customize video landing pages, so prospects will see more than just the video, and be able to book meetings or take actions immediately after watching.

![Edit Dynamic Video](/images/9146071/d34154e3-image.png)

Learn more about [creating dynamic videos here](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions).

## 2. Import Contacts

You can create dynamic videos for contacts in a few ways:

1. Manually type contact data
2. Import a CSV
3. Generate videos via automation (Zapier, API, etc)

![Import Contacts for DV](/images/9146071/d2ca0fbb-image.png)

Once dynamic videos have been created, you'll see them populate, and can play them to test out the AI and make sure everything looks great.

![Preview Dynamic Video](/images/9146071/6fd00ef2-image.png)

## 3. Share in Amplemarket

To share videos in Amplemarket, simply click **"Share Campaign"**

Then, select **"Amplemarket"**

![Share Videos in Amplemarket](/images/8869736/bf119c70-image.png)

Now, you can take that snippet and paste it in an email in Amplemarket.

## 4. Track Views & Engagement

Once people start to watch your video, you'll be able to see who's watching, what percentage they've watched, and what other they've taken in your video analytics.

![See Dynamic Video Engagement Data](/images/9146071/abd3bad3-image.png)

Just click **"See Engagement"** at the bottom of your Dynamic Video Builder to view the analytics modal.

![Launch Video Analytics Modal](/images/8869736/1d1d630b-image.png)
