![Sendspark video player with Calendar toggle switch set to On](/images/9052008/416e18c6-image.png)

## How to Embed a Chili Piper Calendar with Videos

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/t7dO5HiYXJg?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Under your Sendspark video, toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**
2. Select **"Chili Piper"** from the Provider Dropdown List
3. **Add the link**to the Chili Piper calendar you want to use

![Sendspark calendar settings form with Chili Piper provider selected and link field](/images/9052008/a7c52510-image.png)

You should be able to see your embedded calendar in the preview area!

![Sendspark video preview showing embedded Chili Piper calendar below video player](/images/9052008/9140e920-image.png)
