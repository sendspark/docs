To embed a Chili Piper calendar with your Sendspark video, toggle "Calendar" to "On" under your video, select "Chili Piper" from the Provider dropdown, and paste the link to your Chili Piper calendar (not the embed code). Sendspark embeds it automatically, so prospects can book a meeting without ever leaving your video page.

## Key takeaways

- Embedding a Chili Piper calendar lets prospects book a meeting with you without ever leaving your video page.
- Toggle the "Calendar" switch to "On," choose "Chili Piper" from the Provider dropdown, and add the link to the Chili Piper calendar you want to use.
- Add the calendar link only, not the embed code; Sendspark automatically embeds the calendar from the link.
- You'll know it's working when your embedded Chili Piper calendar appears in the preview area.

## How to Embed a Chili Piper Calendar with Videos

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/t7dO5HiYXJg?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Under your Sendspark video, toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**
2. Select **"Chili Piper"** from the Provider Dropdown List
3. **Add the link**to the Chili Piper calendar you want to use

![Sendspark calendar settings form with Chili Piper provider selected and link field](/images/9052008/a7c52510-image.png)

**Note:** you're just adding the link to your Chili Piper calendar, not the embed code. Sendspark automatically embeds your calendar from the link.

You should be able to see your embedded calendar in the preview area!
