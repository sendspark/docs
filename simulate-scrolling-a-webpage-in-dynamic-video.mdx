Simulate Scrolling makes a dynamic video look like you're scrolling through each contact's own webpage. Create a dynamic video campaign, add a video of yourself that will appear over the website, then toggle on **"Simulate Scrolling."** When you add contacts and generate videos, each one shows you scrolling through that contact's site.

## Key takeaways

- Simulate Scrolling overlays your video of yourself on top of each contact's own webpage as it appears to scroll.
- Set it up in three steps: create a dynamic video campaign, add your video, then toggle on **"Simulate Scrolling."**
- Each contact's webpage is handled automatically when you import contacts and generate videos.
- The result is a unique video per contact — for example, one scrolling sendspark.com and another scrolling hubspot.com.

Want to generate dynamic videos that makes it look like you're scrolling through each of your contacts' webpages?

Follow the steps below!

## How to Add Website Scroll to Dynamic Videos

1. Create a [dynamic video campaign](https://sendspark.com/dynamic-videos)
2. Add the video that you would like to use of yourself (that will appear over the website for each of your contacts)
3. Toggle on **"Simulate Scrolling"**

![Simulate Scrolling Over Webpages in Dynamic Videos](/images/8781145/cdc2f25d-image.png)

Now, when you add contacts and generate videos, it will look like you're scrolling through each of their webpages.
