The **Floating Menu** or Widget gives you **quick access to start recording from anywhere** on the web — without opening the full Sendspark dashboard.

## Menu Options

From top to bottom:

1. **❌ Close Button** – Hides the floating menu.
2. **🎥 Camera Only** – Record using your webcam only.
3. **🖥️ Screen Only** – Record your screen only (no camera feed).
4. **📸 Full Screen (Camera + Screen)** – Record both your screen and camera at the same time.
5. **⚙️ Settings** – Adjust recording or display options.
6. **▶️ “Start Recording!” Button** – Begins the recording with your selected mode.

## How to Enable/Disable

Click on the Chrome Extension's Sendspark Icon, head to Settings, and toggle the **Floating Menu ON/OFF**
