To upload an existing video, go to your Sendspark dashboard, click the **Upload Video** icon, then either drag your file into the drag & drop area or click **browse files** to choose one from your computer. Select the file and it will appear in your library shortly.

## Key takeaways

- Start from your [Sendspark dashboard](https://sendspark.com/my-library) and click the **Upload Video** icon.
- Add your video by drag & drop or by using **browse files** to select it from your computer.
- Sendspark accepts **.mp4** and **.mov** files (see [supported formats](/what-video-formats-can-i-upload-to-sendspark)).

To upload an existing video into Sendspark, follow these steps:

- Go to your [Sendspark dashboard](https://sendspark.com/my-library)
- Click the **Upload Video**icon

![Upload button](/images/5566661/0e9ed904-image.png)

- Either drag a video into the drag & drop area, or click where it says **browse files**to access the files on your computer

![Upload Video](/images/5566661/5dde37cd-image.png)

- Select the file you want to upload
- That's it! Just wait patiently and your video should be there soon 😊

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which video formats can I upload?">
    Sendspark supports **.mp4** and **.mov** files. Formats like .avi, .wmv, .flv, .mkv, and .m4v will not upload. See [supported video formats](/what-video-formats-can-i-upload-to-sendspark) for the full list.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add my video file?">
    Either drag the video into the drag & drop area, or click **browse files** to select it from your computer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is my upload taking a long time?">
    A slow upload is often related to your internet upload speed. See [why is my video uploading slowly](/why-is-my-video-uploading-slowly) for troubleshooting steps.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
