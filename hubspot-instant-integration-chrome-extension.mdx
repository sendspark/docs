## Before you get started:

Please install the [Sendspark Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph) to use the Hubspot integration.

As long as you have the Chrome Extension installed, you’ll automatically be able to access Sendspark from Hubspot.

## How to Send Videos in Hubspot

## 1. Click the Sendspark Icon in Hubspot

Create a **new email in Hubspot**. You’ll see the Sendspark logo appear in the compose window next to the native Hubspot options.

![Sendspark icon button visible in Hubspot email compose window toolbar](/images/5809352/14ace081-image.png)

**Note:** you must have the [Sendspark chrome extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph/related?hl=en-US) installed and be using a Chrome browser to see the Sendspark icon.

## 2. Record a New Video

Click **Record New Video**.

This will launch the Sendspark Chrome Extension, so you can record a video of yourself or your screen.

![Sendspark menu showing Record New Video and alternative options](/images/5809352/271949f0-image.png)

Alternatively, you can choose from the list of options:

- **Request Video** — inserts a [video request link](https://www.sendspark.com/video-platform/request-videos) to easily get a video recording or testimonial from your customer
- **Meet Now**— inserts a Zoom or Google Meet link to connect face-to-face
- **Send Calendar** — inserts a Calendly or scheduling link to coordinate a time to connect
- **Add Last Recording** — inserts the last Sendspark video you recorded
- **Video Library** — opens your Sendspark dashboard to find or manage existing videos

## 3. Complete Recording

To end your video, **click the Sendspark Chrome Extension icon** on the top right of your browser.

![Sendspark Chrome Extension icon location in browser top right corner](/images/5809352/c327b16f-S6TIIVgdBXX0OYPIohqqGTwagqWJOmoltVnRPoOS_LBobgEGtwAxomuCvgz60DVVPywyW9sx6BWXh6CsHax_I8VNKGoQecF6NaEbIQGUNAXHAYvjZxbV09SPMbWTZ6Zr_OHPKoRk_s0.png)

This will complete your video recording, and launch a modal for you to preview your video before sharing.

![Video preview modal after completing recording in Sendspark](/images/5809352/1f16915c-image.png)

## 4. (Optional) Edit Video Page in Sendspark

You can customize your video before sharing it by clicking **Edit Page / Thumbnail**.

![Edit Page Thumbnail button in video preview modal dialog](/images/5809352/234d97d5-image.png)

This will open your video in a new window, where you can edit the [video landing page](/customize-your-video-landing-page) or [video thumbnail.](/how-to-edit-video-thumbnails)

---

**Note**: When you edit the page, you’ll leave Hubspot and go to Sendspark. Now, instead of inserting your video, you'll need to copy and paste your video preview into Hubspot.

---

## 5. Copy Linked GIF

Click **Linked GIF** to copy the video preview to your clipboard.

![Linked GIF copy button in Sendspark video sharing interface](/images/5809352/a485217d-image.png)

## 6. Paste into your Hubspot email

Now paste directly into your Hubspot email. You will see a GIF preview of your video that's linked to your video share page.

![Pasted video GIF thumbnail preview displayed in Hubspot email compose](/images/5809352/d773320b-image.png)

Feel free to resize the video thumbnail and edit the email text until it looks right to you!
