Sendspark provides video analytics to help you gauge how viewers are interacting with your videos.

---

## Launch The Video Analytics Modal

From either **My Library** or **Team Library**, click on the analytics icon on your video to launch the video analytics modal.

![My library video](/images/5759031/762603fc-image.png)

## How to Read Sendspark Video Analytics

On the video analytics modal, you'll see several categories. Here's what they all mean:

![Sendspark Analytics](/images/5759031/628c3df4-1-wva-image-hero.png)

## **Total Views:**

The number of times viewers open your video page. This is a page view, NOT a video view.

## **Total Plays:**

The number of times viewers click to play your video. Note that if someone starts and stops your video multiple times, it will count as multiple plays.

## **Total Clicks:**

The number of clicks on the **call-to-action** button below your video.

## **Total Likes:**

The number of times viewers hit the like button on the bottom right of your video. Note that one viewer can like the video multiple times.

## Viewer Insights & Watch Time

At the bottom of the video modal, you'll also see a breakdown of **views, plays, clicks, likes, and watch time**separated by individual. The views, plays, clicks, and likes correspond to the data above.

**Watch time** displays a video heatmap showing which part of your video each viewer actually played.

Next to**Watch Time**, you'll see the **percentage watched**by the viewer during that session.

## Frequently Asked Questions about Video Analytics

## **1. Are my views included in the video analytics?**

No. Your visits are excluded from video analytics as long as you play the video inside your account. Please note that if you do use the share link on a new tab and play it, it will count your views. Sendspark excludes creator views using mechanisms like cookies, IP addresses, and account associations. This ensures that only genuine viewer interactions are captured in your analytics, maintaining the accuracy of engagement metrics.

## 4. How can I test if my analytics are working correctly?

To verify Sendspark's analytics functionality, consider these testing methods:

- **Use an incognito or private browsing mode**: Open your browser in incognito mode (Google Chrome) or private browsing mode (Safari/Edge), and then play the video link. This method prevents the browser from associating your identity with the analytics.
- **View the video from another device**: Access the shared link using a device unconnected to your Sendspark account, like a smartphone or another computer, to log a genuine view.
- **Send the video to someone else**: Share the video link with others or use a secondary email and device to simulate a viewer not associated with the account. By applying these approaches, you can assess if the analytics track interactions as expected.

## 3. Can I see video engagement data prior to January 19th, 2022?

[You cannot see video engagement data prior to January 19th, 2022](/analytics-update-implications-for-videos-recorded-before-jan-19-2022) (when we released video analytics) in the Sendspark application. If you message us, we can look up the total video views, plays, likes, and clicks for an individual video, but we will not be able to pull viewer insight data.
