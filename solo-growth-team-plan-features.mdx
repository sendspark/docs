**Solo Plan**

💲 **$49 /mo** (1 seat included)  
👤 Best for individuals who want to create AI-personalized videos.

## What’s Included:

- **100 Dynamic Video Minutes** per month
- **AI Name Personalization** (automatically personalizes videos with viewer names)
- **Dynamic Video Backgrounds** (add branded/customized backdrops)
- **Send Videos Anywhere**: Gmail, Outlook, LinkedIn
- **Remove Sendspark Branding** (white-label videos)
- **Custom Domain (CNAME)** (host videos under your own domain for branding consistency)

---

## **Growth Plan**

💲 **$99 /mo** (includes 3 seats)  
👥 Best for small teams that want to scale video selling and integrations.

## Everything in Solo, plus:

- **250 Dynamic Video Minutes** per month (5x more than Solo)
- **3 Seats Included** in the price (expandable for larger teams)
- **50+ Email & CRM Integrations** (including HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, Pipedrive, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Outreach, SalesLoft, Apollo, Greenhouse, Gong Engage, Instantly, and many more)
- **Native Integrations**: Clay, Expandi, Zapier
- **API & Webhooks Access** (automate workflows, advanced customizations)
- **Admin Dashboard** (manage teams, permissions, and usage centrally)

---

## **Team Plan**

💲 **$299 /mo** (includes 10 seats)  
👥 Best for small teams that want to scale video selling and integrations.

- **50+ Email & CRM Integrations** (including HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, Pipedrive, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, Outreach, SalesLoft, Apollo, Greenhouse, Gong Engage, Instantly, and many more)

## ✅ Which Plan Should I Choose?

- **Choose Solo** if you’re an individual creator or salesperson who only needs 1 seat and moderate video volume.
- **Choose Team** if you’re a team that needs multiple users, advanced integrations, API access, or larger monthly video limits.
