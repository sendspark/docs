Sendspark offers three main plans. The **Solo Plan ($49/mo)** includes 1 seat and 100 dynamic video minutes, the **Growth Plan ($99/mo)** includes 3 seats and 250 minutes, and the **Team Plan ($299/mo)** includes 10 seats and 1,000 minutes. AI personalization, dynamic backgrounds, branding removal, and a custom domain are available on every plan.

## Key takeaways

- **Solo ($49/mo)**: 1 seat, 100 dynamic video minutes, AI personalization, dynamic backgrounds, branding removal, and custom domain (CNAME).
- **Growth ($99/mo)**: 3 seats, 250 minutes, plus 50+ email/CRM integrations, API & webhooks, and an admin dashboard.
- **Team ($299/mo)**: 10 seats, 1,000 minutes, with the same integrations, API access, and admin dashboard as Growth.
- API/webhooks and multiple variables (beta) are available on Growth and Team, but not on Solo.

**Solo Plan**

💲 **$49 /mo** (1 seat included)  
👤 Best for individuals who want to create AI-personalized videos.

## What’s Included:

- **100 Dynamic Video Minutes** per month
- **AI Name Personalization** (automatically personalizes videos with viewer names)
- **Dynamic Video Backgrounds** (add branded/customized backdrops)
- **Send Videos Anywhere**: Gmail, Outlook, LinkedIn
- **Remove Sendspark Branding** (white-label videos)
- **Custom Domain (CNAME)** (host videos under your own domain for branding consistency)

## ✅ Which Plan Should I Choose?

- **Choose Solo** if you’re an individual creator or salesperson who only needs 1 seat and moderate video volume.
- **Choose Team** if you’re a team that needs multiple users, advanced integrations, API access, or larger monthly video limits.
