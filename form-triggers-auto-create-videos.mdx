Sendspark makes it easy to automatically create AI personalized videos when forms are submitted.

This can be useful if you want to send an automated video message to a lead who fills out a form, to capture their attention and help them move forward in your sales process.

## How to Create AI Personalized Videos from Form Submissions

## 1. Create New Automation in Sendspark

Start by heading to the automated workflow tab in Sendspark, and clicking **"new automation"**

## 2. Add Your Trigger: Form Submission

For the trigger, add your form submission.

![](/images/11173982/f13b193f-image.png)

This could be a form submitted through your website, CRM, or even Airtable/Google Sheets. Check out the app section to see which apps are supported

Make sure to add the specific form that should trigger this automation!

![](/images/11173982/e50b4339-image.png)

## 3. Add Action to Generate Video

Now, add an action.

![](/images/11173982/acf82537-image.png)

For the action, select**Sendspark: Create Dynamic Video.**

![](/images/11173982/13a30af3-image.png)

Select the dynamic video campaign you'd like to use, and map the fields from your trigger in that you would like to use in the video (first name, email, etc.)

![](/images/11173982/99eca1a0-image.png)

---

Note: if you are missing any contact data, you can add a "find contact" action before this step to look up more information about this contact in your CRM. Then, you will have more data fields available!

![](/images/11173982/3200eaab-image.png)

---

## 4. Add Action to Send Video

Add a final step to send this video out. You can embed videos directly in Gmail or Outlook, add the contact to an automated sequence, or choose another way to share.

Check out all ways to share automated workflows that are supported in Sendspark.

In the end, your automated workflow will look something like this:
