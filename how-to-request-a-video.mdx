Watch this video to learn how to use Sendspark to request videos from anyone with a link:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/h9tIvKOdyO0?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

---

1. Navigate to your [Video Request library](https://sendspark.com/requested-videos) or click here: ![](/images/5208425/3cdc9af8-image.png)
2. Click **Copy Request Link** ![Request a video](/images/5208425/24729ce5-image.png)
3. Share that link with anyone!
4. They will be able to record videos for you on that page. ![Video Request Page](/images/5208425/82793b82-image.png)
5. Videos will automatically upload to your Video Request Library for you to view, download, or share. ![View Requested Videos](/images/5208425/746508b2-image.png)
