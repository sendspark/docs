Camera Spotlight lets you shift focus between your screen and your camera while recording, so you can highlight yourself during a product demo. It requires the Sendspark Chrome Extension. Launch the recorder with "Both" selected, then click the camera spotlight icon on your camera bubble to enter spotlight mode, either before or anytime during the recording.

## Key takeaways

- Camera Spotlight shifts focus between your screen and yourself to make product demos more engaging.
- It requires the Sendspark Chrome Extension, so install that first if you don't have it.
- Select "Both" as your recording option, then click the camera spotlight icon on the camera bubble to launch spotlight mode.
- You can toggle spotlight before or during recording; click anywhere on the spotlight bubble to return the camera to its original position.

[Camera Spotlight](https://blog.sendspark.com/camera-spotlight) helps you personalize your product demos by shifting the focus between your screen and yourself.

## How to Use Camera Spotlight

1. Launch the Sendspark recorder.
2. Make sure you have “Both” selected as your recording option: ![Recording example](/images/6040365/5fcfa096-Launch_Camera_Spotlight.png)
3. Click on the camera spotlight icon on your camera bubble to launch spotlight mode. You can do this before you start recording, and anytime during the recording. ![Spotlight mode on](/images/6040365/31a50ec9-Record_with_Camera_Spotlight.png)
4. Click anywhere on the spotlight bubble to revert your camera bubble to its original position.

## FAQ about Camera Spotlight

## 1. Is Camera Spotlight free?

Yes. Camera Spotlight is included on every Sendspark plan — including the free tier.

## 2. Can I use Camera Spotlight while recording?

Yes. You can switch the Camera Spotlight either before you start recording, or anytime during your recording.
