Sendspark search helps you find videos based on titles. Check out additional options to filter videos by [engagement](/filter-videos-by-plays-likes-and-cta-clicked) or [creators](/filter-videos-by-creators).

## How to Search Videos

1. Navigate to your [Sendspark dashboard](https://sendspark.com/my-library)
2. Click into the **Search bar**. (Note: if you don't see the search bar, make sure your viewport is open wide enough). ![Video Search](/images/6299854/5e6a7b06-image.png)
3. Type out your search term ![Search Sendspark Library](/images/6299854/3a5a1e84-image.png)
4. If you don't see the video you're looking for, try clicking **show more,** or updating the text in your search
