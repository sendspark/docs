To search videos in Sendspark, open your [Sendspark dashboard](https://sendspark.com/my-library), click into the **Search bar**, and type your search term. Search matches on video titles. If you don't see the video you want, click **show more** or refine your text, or use the engagement and creator filters instead.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark's search bar finds videos by their **titles**. Type a term into the Search bar on your dashboard to filter your library.
- If you don't see the Search bar, widen your browser viewport until it appears.
- Not finding a video? Click **show more** or adjust your search text.
- To find videos another way, filter by [engagement](/filter-videos-by-plays-likes-and-cta-clicked) (plays, likes, CTA clicks) or by [creators](/filter-videos-by-creators).

Sendspark search helps you find videos based on titles. Check out additional options to filter videos by [engagement](/filter-videos-by-plays-likes-and-cta-clicked) or [creators](/filter-videos-by-creators).

## How to Search Videos

1. Navigate to your [Sendspark dashboard](https://sendspark.com/my-library)
2. Click into the **Search bar**. (Note: if you don't see the search bar, make sure your viewport is open wide enough). ![Video Search](/images/6299854/5e6a7b06-image.png)
3. Type out your search term ![Search Sendspark Library](/images/6299854/3a5a1e84-image.png)
4. If you don't see the video you're looking for, try clicking **show more,** or updating the text in your search

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What does the search bar match on?">
    Search finds videos based on their titles. Type a word or phrase from the video's title into the Search bar and your library filters to matching videos.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't see a search bar. Where is it?">
    If the Search bar isn't showing, your viewport may be too narrow. Widen your browser window until the Search bar appears at the top of your dashboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My video isn't showing up in search. What can I do?">
    Try clicking **show more** to load additional results, or update the text in your search. You can also switch to filtering by [engagement](/filter-videos-by-plays-likes-and-cta-clicked) or by [creators](/filter-videos-by-creators) to narrow down your library a different way.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I find videos without searching by title?">
    Yes. Instead of searching, you can filter your library by [plays, likes, and CTA clicks](/filter-videos-by-plays-likes-and-cta-clicked) to see only engaged videos, or filter by [creators](/filter-videos-by-creators) in your Team Library to see videos from specific team members.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
