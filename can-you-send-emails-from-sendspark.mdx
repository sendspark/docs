Sendspark focuses on creating and personalizing videos, and you deliver those videos by email using its tools and integrations. You have three options: connect one of 60+ email and CRM platforms, use the Chrome extension to record and insert videos into Gmail or copy a thumbnail into any email client, or set up an Agentic Workflow to auto-send through Gmail or Outlook.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark connects with **over 60 email and CRM platforms**, including HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, Outreach, Expandi, and Clay.
- The **Chrome extension** lets you record and insert videos directly into Gmail, or copy animated/static thumbnails to paste into any other email platform.
- **Agentic Workflows** can automate sending (for example, "when a video is generated, send it through Gmail or Outlook") without a separate email automation platform. This feature is available on specific plans.
- Whether you send manually, through an integration, or via automation, Sendspark hosts the video and you deliver it wherever your audience already is.

Sendspark is designed to help you create, personalize, and share videos — and you can easily send those videos through email using our many integrations and tools.  
​

While Sendspark focuses on video generation, you can deliver your videos through Gmail, Outlook, CRMs, and automation platforms connected to your account.
