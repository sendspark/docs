Sendspark is designed to help you create, personalize, and share videos — and you can easily send those videos through email using our many integrations and tools.  
​

While Sendspark focuses on video generation, you can deliver your videos through Gmail, Outlook, CRMs, and automation platforms connected to your account.

---

## ✅ Ways to Send Sendspark Videos via Email

## 🧩 1. Using Sendspark Integrations

Sendspark connects with **over 60 email and CRM platforms**, including **HubSpot, Salesforce, Mailchimp, Outreach, Expandi, Clay**, and many more.

**You can check the complete list of integrations:**

[👉 View all available integrations](https://www.sendspark.com/integrations)

## ⚙️ 2. Automate sending with Agentic Workflows in bulk without an email automation platform

Sendspark includes its own automation system, similar to Zapier.  
You can create automations such as:

- “When a video is generated → send it automatically through Gmail or Outlook.”

[👉 View Outlook Instructions](/outlook-automatically-send-ai-personalized-videos)

## 💌 3. Send Videos with any email platform

The simplest way to share your videos is with our **Chrome extension**, which lets you:

- Record and insert videos directly into **Gmail**.
- Copy **animated or static thumbnails** and paste them into any other email platform. ![Chrome extension menu displaying video insertion and thumbnail copy options for Gmail](/images/12701549/b83a9678-image.png)

Sendspark lets you **generate personalized videos** and then **send them through 60+ supported platforms** — including Gmail, Outlook, and popular CRMs.

Whether you send them manually, through integrations, or via automations, you can always deliver your Sendspark videos exactly where your audience is.
