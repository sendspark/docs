## Using Sendspark Video Thumbnails in supported Email Automation Platforms

Sendspark allows you to create Dynamic Videos and Personalized (non-dynamic) videos, enhancing your email marketing campaigns with engaging content.

To ensure your video thumbnails display correctly in emails, it's crucial to use email automation platforms that support HTML editing, that way you can use our snippet code in your platform to use our AI Voice cloning, Text Variables, and correct Dynamic Background.

**How to Embed Sendspark Video Thumbnails in Emails**

1. **Create Your Video or Campaign:** Start by creating a dynamic video or a regular video campaign in Sendspark.
2. **Copy the Snippet Code:** Once your video is ready, copy the provided snippet code.
3. **Paste the Snippet Code:** In your email automation platform, navigate to the HTML editing section of your email. Paste the snippet code into the body of the email. ![HTML editing section in email automation platform with Sendspark video snippet code pasted](/images/9583536/edb9250f-image.png)

Here's a complete Help Article to understand how to create a Dynamic Video Campaign for the first time: [create a dynamic video campaign](/how-to-create-a-dynamic-video-campaign)

This process ensures the thumbnail of your video is correctly embedded in your email. When recipients click on the thumbnail, they are directed to your video, allowing you to collect valuable analytics.

**Advantages of Using Supported Email Automation Platforms**

1. **Seamless Integration:** Easily add Sendspark video thumbnails into your emails with just a snippet code.
2. **Enhanced Engagement:** Increase click-through rates and engagement with video thumbnails.
3. **Detailed Analytics:** Track viewer interactions and gain insights into video performance.
4. **Efficient Campaigns:** Automate your email campaigns while incorporating dynamic video content.
5. **Personalization:** Personalize video content for your audience, boosting relevance and impact.

By using these supported email automation platforms, you can maximize the effectiveness of your Sendspark videos and gain comprehensive analytics to refine your marketing strategies.
