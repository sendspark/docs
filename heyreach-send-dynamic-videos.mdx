To send AI personalized videos in HeyReach, create your dynamic videos in Sendspark, download the campaign CSV, and upload it to HeyReach. Map Sendspark's **Video Link** column to a HeyReach custom variable, then insert that variable into your LinkedIn connection requests or messages so each prospect receives their own personalized video.

## Key takeaways

- The flow is: create AI personalized videos in Sendspark → download the CSV → upload to HeyReach → map **Video Link** → use it in your LinkedIn outreach.
- HeyReach is a LinkedIn automation tool, so the personalized video goes into **connection requests or LinkedIn messages**, not email.
- The key field to map is **Video Link**, the unique link to each prospect's generated video, mapped to a HeyReach **custom variable**.
- When the video link is sent in a LinkedIn message, it unfurls into an animated preview that recipients can click to watch.
