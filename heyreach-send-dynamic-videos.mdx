## How to Send AI Personalized Videos in HeyReach

1. Create AI Personalized Videos in Sendspark
2. Download CSV from Sendspark ​![Cursor hovering the Download Generated Videos as CSV icon in Sendspark](/images/10470112/c127ad2b-image.png)
3. Upload CSV to HeyReach ​![Import from CSV option in the HeyReach lead source menu](/images/10470112/87548d91-image.png)
4. Map "Video Link" variable from Sendspark to a Custom Variable in HeyReach ​![HeyReach mapping Video Link to a Sendspark_video_link custom variable](/images/10470112/5a0561c0-image.png)
5. Use the "Video Link" variable where you want your personalized video to appear in LinkedIn Connection Requests or LinkedIn Messages ​![HeyReach message editor inserting the Sendspark_video_link variable into a LinkedIn message](/images/10470112/7e06bc45-image.png)
