To send personalized videos in Instantly, record or upload your video in Sendspark and personalize the header, message, and thumbnail with placeholders. Click "Share Video," select Instantly, then paste the snippet into Instantly's HTML editor (the &lt; &gt; icon). Switch back to the visual editor, resize the video to about 50%, preview, and send.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark can automatically pull in your viewer's first name, company name, and job title, so you can personalize videos at scale in Instantly.
- Copy your share snippet by clicking "Share Video" and selecting Instantly. If Instantly isn't listed, modify your share modal to add it.
- Paste the snippet into Instantly's HTML editor by clicking the &lt; &gt; icon, then switch back to the visual editor to resize the video (50% is recommended for the best appearance).
- Use the preview icon to review your video email before sending, so you can confirm your thumbnail personalization renders correctly.

Send personalized videos in Instantly emails to cut through the noise and connect at scale.

Sendspark makes it easy to automatically pull in your viewer's first name, company name, and job title, just follow the steps below!

## How to Send Bulk Personalized Videos in Instantly Emails

## 1. Record or upload your video

Record or upload your video in Sendspark [your Sendspark video library](https://sendspark.com/my-videos)

![Record or upload video in Sendspark](/images/6676528/d1dc5ed7-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_4.45.49_PM.png)

## 2. Personalize content around video

Customize the **video header**, **message**, and **thumbnail**. Feel free to use placeholders to automatically pull in contact information from Instantly when you send your video!

![Personalize video](/images/6676528/e4302b71-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_4.46.27_PM.png)

## 3. Copy share snippet for Instantly

Click **"Share Video,"** and then select Instantly (if you don't see Instantly, follow these steps to [modify your share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal)).

![Copy video share snippet for Instantly](/images/6676528/4259783a-image.png)

## 4. Paste in Instantly (HMTL)

From Instantly, click on the**&lt; &gt;** to get the HTML editor. Then, paste your Sendspark video snippet right where you want it to appear in your email.

![Paste video in Instantly](/images/6676528/0ae3c4f4-image.png)

## 5. Resize video

Click the &lt; &gt; again to get back to the visual email editor. You'll notice your video is a little large, but you can click on the video and then the rule icon to resize your video. We recommend 50% for the best appearance.

![Resize video in Instantly](/images/6676528/53e53574-image.png)

## 6. Preview and send!

Click on the preview icon to review your video email before sending it. If you've customized your video thumbnail, you should be able to see the personalization when you preview videos in Instantly.

![Send video in Instantly](/images/6676528/ed047515-image.png)
