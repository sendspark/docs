To send personalized videos in Growth-X, record or upload one video to Sendspark, customize the landing page and thumbnail with personalization tokens, then click Share and select Growth-X. Paste the snippet into your Growth-X message and send. Sendspark automatically personalizes that single video for each recipient in your LinkedIn outreach.

## Key takeaways

- Create one video in Sendspark and it is automatically personalized for each person you reach out to through Growth-X.
- Use [personalization tokens](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale) on the [landing page](/customize-your-video-landing-page) and [thumbnail](/how-to-edit-video-thumbnails) to add each viewer's information automatically.
- Share by clicking **Share**, selecting **Growth-X**, then pasting into your Growth-X message. If Growth-X isn't listed, [modify the share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal).
- Send videos as a follow-up after connecting, not in connection requests. Recipients only see the video preview if they've accepted your LinkedIn connection or InMail request.

## Why Send Personalized Videos in Growth-X

Sending personalized videos in Growth-X can help you see better results from LinkedIn automation. (Nothing beats a personal touch!)

The Sendspark Growth-X integration makes it easy to create one video, and automatically personalize it for each person you're reaching out to via Growth-X.

## Note About Sending Videos on LinkedIn

In order for your recipient to see the preview of your video, and not just a link, they must have either (A) accepted your LinkedIn request, or (B) accepted your InMail request.

For this reason, we do NOT recommend sending videos in connection requests. **Videos are better used as a follow up message, after you are already connected.**

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I record a separate video for each person?">
    No. You record or upload one video to Sendspark, and it is automatically personalized for each recipient you reach out to through Growth-X. Personalization tokens pull in each viewer's information when they watch.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't see Growth-X in the share options — what do I do?">
    The Growth-X shortcut may be disabled in your share modal. Follow the steps to [modify the share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal) and enable the Growth-X shortcut so it appears when you click Share.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why does my recipient see a link instead of the video preview?">
    For a recipient to see the video preview rather than just a link, they must have accepted either your LinkedIn connection request or your InMail request. Until then, LinkedIn shows the raw link.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Should I send a video in a LinkedIn connection request?">
    We don't recommend it. Because the preview only appears once someone has accepted your connection or InMail request, videos work best as a follow-up message after you're already connected.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
