---
title: "How to Customize Your Request Video Pages"
description: "Customize your Request Video pages in Sendspark with branding options, logos, colors, and custom domains for a professional branded recipient experience."
---

Right now, there are a few ways you can customize your [Request Video](https://sendspark.com/requested-videos) page in Sendspark.

Note, you must be on Sendspark Starter or Solo to use most of the branding options here.

---

## 1. Add Your Name & Avatar

Update your name and avatar in your [account settings](https://app.sendspark.com/settings/account). This will show up on the video request page, so your customer will know who is asking for the video, and where it will send when they’re done recording.

![My account screen](/images/4324798/d28e7225-image.png)

## 2. Set Your Global Styles

Navigate to your [workspace settings](https://app.sendspark.com/settings/workspace), and then select Set Global Styles.

![Set Global Style button](/images/4324798/84634d5d-image.png)

Here, you can add your company logo and brand colors. These will show up on your video request pages for a polished, branded experience.

![Set Global Styles modal](/images/4324798/12059915-image.png)

## 3. Use a Custom Domain

[Use your custom domain](/use-a-custom-domain-for-videos) to 100% white-label your video request page. Instead of using a Sendspark URL (i.e. request.sendspark.com/xyz) you'll be able to use a custom branded url (like request.yoursite.com/xyz).

---

## Start Sharing!

To generate the Request Video link,  click the **Copy Request Link** button from your [Requested Videos](https://sendspark.com/requested-videos) library.

![Copy request link button](/images/4324798/b41ec408-image.png)

Make sure you're happy with your Video Request page before you start sharing. Depending on your brand, it might look something like this:

![Video request recording options](/images/4324798/0e13b724-image.png)

**You can edit the styles at any time if you want to make changes -- even after you've sent out the link.**
