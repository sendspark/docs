Yes. Your Request Video link is unique to each workspace and always stays the same, so you can share it as many times as you like. Find it in the **Request Library** under **Video Libraries** (or at sendspark.com/requested-videos). The only time it changes is when you switch workspaces, since requested videos are added to the active workspace.

## Key takeaways

- The Request Video link **stays the same** and can be shared as many times as you like.
- Find it in the **Request Library** under **Video Libraries**, or at [sendspark.com/requested-videos](https://sendspark.com/requested-videos).
- The link is **unique per workspace** — if you change workspaces, the link changes, because requested videos are added to the active workspace.

Requesting a Video involves using a unique link for each workspace. You simply need to access your "Request Library" section under "Video Libraries". Or you can click on the following link: [https://sendspark.com/requested-videos](https://sendspark.com/requested-videos)

![Copy Request Link](/images/5568246/dfaef2f0-image.png)

This link will always remain the same and you can share it as many times as you like.

If you do change your workspace, you'll find that the Request Video Link is now different, as the videos sent to you will be added to that workspace and not others.
