---
title: "Can I use the Request Video link multiple times?"
description: "Learn how to reuse your Sendspark Request Video link multiple times and when it changes. Discover how workspace assignments affect your request video URL."
---

Requesting a Video involves using a unique link for each workspace. You simply need to access your "Request Library" section under "Video Libraries". Or you can click on the following link: [https://sendspark.com/requested-videos](https://sendspark.com/requested-videos)

![Copy Request Link](/images/5568246/dfaef2f0-image.png)

This link will always remain the same and you can share it as many times as you like.

If you do change your workspace, you'll find that the Request Video Link is now different, as the videos sent to you will be added to that workspace and not others.
