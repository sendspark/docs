## How to Get Slack Notifications When Someone Watches Your Video

Get notified in real time when someone watches your video or clicks on a call to action.

## 1. Create Automated Workflow In Sendspark

- Go to the [Agentic Workflow](https://sendspark.com/agentic-workflows) section of Sendspark
- Click **"New Automation"**to create a new automated workflow ​![](/images/10769516/e25afde7-image.png)

## 2. Set Trigger: Sendspark Video Watched

- Click**"Add a Trigger"** ​![](/images/10769516/85d179ae-image.png)
- For the trigger, select **"Sendspark: New Video Watched - All Campaigns"**
  - This means that when a video is watched from any Sendspark dynamic video campaigns in your workspace, this automation will start. ​![](/images/10769516/492bb1cf-image.png) ​

## 3. Set Action: Send Slack Notification

- Add an action to your automated workflow ​![](/images/10769516/920e54f1-image.png)
- Select **"Slack,"** **"Send a message to a public channel"** and then connect your slack ​![](/images/10769516/3899dd43-image.png)
- Select the slack channel you want to send notifications to, and write your message. You can use any of the fields that Sendspark returns in your message to get more context on your video. In the end, it might look something like this: ​![](/images/10769516/f5930631-image.png)

Save, and hit **"Start"** to turn on your automation!
