To find your Workspace ID in Sendspark, open [Workspace Settings](https://sendspark.com/settings/workspace), click the **Manage Workspace** tab at the top, then click the hidden Workspace ID on the right-hand side to copy it. This alphanumeric ID identifies your workspace and is sometimes needed by the support team.

## Key takeaways

- The Workspace ID is a unique alphanumeric identifier for the workspace you're working in.
- Support may request it for specific procedures, and it's also used with API credentials and agency workspace activation.
- Find it in three clicks: **Workspace Settings** → **Manage Workspace** tab → click the hidden Workspace ID to copy.
