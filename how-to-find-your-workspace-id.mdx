---
title: "How to find your Workspace ID"
description: "Learn how to locate your Workspace ID in Sendspark settings to organize teams and get support. Find your unique workspace identifier in just a few clicks."
---

Sendspark allows you to create multiple workspaces to organize your teams and produce videos separately, all in a single account.

This alphanumeric ID is fundamental to identifying the workspace you are working on, and it can be required by the support team for specific procedures.

See the following steps:

1) Click on your company and go to "Workspace Settings": https://sendspark.com/settings/workspace

![Account modal](/images/8425340/5129e372-Screenshot_2023_09_28-1.png)

2) Click on the Manage Workspace tab on the top

![Manage Workspace tab](/images/8425340/231dff2f-Screenshot_2023_09_28-1_-_Copy.png)

3) Find and click on the hidden Workspace ID on the right-hand side, to copy it

![Copy Workspace ID ](/images/8425340/8f06f5a3-Screenshot_2023_09_28-1_-_Copy__2_.png)
