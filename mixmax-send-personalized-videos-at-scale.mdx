Send personalized videos in Mixmax emails to cut through the noise and connect at scale.

Sendspark makes it easy to automatically pull in your viewer's first name, company name, and job title, just follow the steps below!

## How to Send Bulk Personalized Videos in Mixmax Emails

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/6k3IFu6-t6w?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

## 1. Record or upload your video

Record or upload your video in Sendspark [your Sendspark video library](https://sendspark.com/my-videos)

![Record or upload video in Sendspark](/images/6676528/d1dc5ed7-Screen_Shot_2022-11-06_at_4.45.49_PM.png)

## 2. Personalize content around video

Customize the **video header**, **message**, and **thumbnail**. Feel free to use placeholders to automatically pull in contact information from Mixmax when you send your video!

![Personalize video in Sendspark](/images/6604948/a525ce68-image.png)

## 3. Copy share snippet for Mixmax

Click **"Share Video,"** and then select Mixmax (if you don't see Mixmax, follow these steps to [modify your share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal)).

![Copy video share snippet for Mixmax](/images/6604948/73e2e523-image.png)

## 4. Paste in a Mixmax template

Create a new template in Mixmax, and then paste your video share snippet directly in the template.

![Paste video into a Mixmax template](/images/6604948/24efade1-image.png)

Note: the video pastes pretty large. Feel free to resize it by dragging the bottom right corner of the video thumbnail inwards.

## 5. Send your video email!

Use your video email template when sending emails in Mixmax or Gmail using the Mixmax extension. Your video will automatically personalize for each recipient!
