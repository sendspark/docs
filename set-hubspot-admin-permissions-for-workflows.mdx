title: "Set HubSpot Admin Permissions for Workflows"
description: "Learn how to set HubSpot admin permissions for automated workflows. Configure CRM objects and automation settings in just a few steps."
---

This article will show you where to find your Hubpost permissions level needed to successfully create workflows

While standing on your Home Page, click on the Settings button located in the top right-hand side corner:

![Hubspot Settings](/images/10922558/e4df5894-image.png)

Now, on the left-hand side panel and under Account Management, you will find the option USERS & TEAMS

![User & Terms button](/images/10922558/95ecf7ec-image.png)

Select the right user will enable the EDIT PERMISSIONS button:

![Edit Permissions](/images/10922558/738ce590-image.png)

Find the CRM OBJECTS and AUTOMATION options in the left-hand side menu and be sure to turn them ON. Both will be important when creating Automated Workflows

![Permissions](/images/10922558/74d6eeca-image.png)

![permissions](/images/10922558/f88ebe16-image.png)

That`s all!
