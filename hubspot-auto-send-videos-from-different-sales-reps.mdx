When demos are round-robined across sales reps, you can still auto-send the right video from the right person. Build a HubSpot Workflow, use branches to determine the assigned rep, add a step that uses the Sendspark HubSpot App to create the video, then send that rep's email with the Sendspark dynamic video snippet embedded.

## Key takeaways

- Use a **HubSpot Workflow** with **branches** to route each contact to their assigned rep (for example, after a round-robin demo booking).
- Each branch uses the **Sendspark HubSpot App** step to automatically create the video for that rep.
- Follow the create-video step with the **rep's own email**, which has the **Sendspark dynamic video snippet** embedded, so the video comes from the correct person.

Let's say that you want to automatically send AI-personalized videos to prospects after they book a demo with your company.

But you're using a round robin to automatically assign demos to sales reps...

How would you automatically send the right video from the right person?

## How to Automatically Send Videos from Assigned Reps

1. Create a Workflow in HubSpot
2. Use branches to distinguish which rep should be creating the video
3. Use the Sendspark HubSpot App to automatically create the video
4. Follow that step with the right email from the rep (with the Sendspark dynamic video snippet embedded in that email)
