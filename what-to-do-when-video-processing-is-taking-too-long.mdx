If a video is still showing "processing" long after you recorded it, the processing was most likely unsuccessful and the video cannot be recovered, so you'll need to re-record. To avoid this, never close the recording modal or tab until you see "Your video is done processing" and the **Copy Link** button appears. On an unstable connection, download the video for a later re-upload.

## Key takeaways

- A video that still says "processing" a long time after recording means the process failed. It cannot be recovered or retrieved, so you'll have to re-do the video.
- While recording, do not close the modal or the tab. You should see your icon whirling, then a "Your video is done processing" message.
- Once done, use the **Copy Link** button at the bottom, and paste the link into a new tab to confirm it opens.
- If your internet connection feels unstable, download the video (bottom-left icon) so you can re-upload it later.

If you're experiencing any of the following issues:

- The video is taking more than usual to process.
- The video never finished uploading.
- The video is still processing after 24 hours.

If it's been a long time since you recorded the video and the message "processing" still comes up, it means the process was unsuccessful. Unfortunately, the video cannot be recovered or retrieved and you will have to re-do the video.

## How do I know if my video processed successfully?

After you stop recording, the modal shows a whirling icon while it processes, then displays "Your video is done processing" and a **Copy Link** button at the bottom. Copy that link and open it in a new tab to confirm the video plays. If the modal is still stuck on "processing" long after recording, the process failed.
