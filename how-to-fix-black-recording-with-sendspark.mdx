---
title: "How to fix black recording with Sendspark"
description: "Fix black screen recording issues in Sendspark by disabling hardware acceleration and Camera Effects for clear video capture."
---

You recorded an awesome video but when checking the preview you only get a black screen and audio? I am sorry for your loss, let's figure out why this happens so we can solve it.

There are a lot of things that can go wrong when recording a video but getting a black screen usually means one thing: **your browser had trouble processing the video encoder with your computer hardware.**

Additionally, if you are using the Sendspark Chrome extension, the "Camera Effects" feature might also cause this issue. Camera Effects can be resource-intensive and may lead to a black screen if the computer struggles with the processing demands.

The easiest fix is to **disable hardware acceleration** on the browser. Let's see how to do that.

Similarly, if you are using the Sendspark Chrome extension, consider disabling its Camera Effects feature as it may resolve the issue.

1. First, open your **Chrome settings** by clicking on the 3 dots located on the top right-hand side of the browser.

![Chrome settings](/images/6226849/41ecc7a5-image.png)

2. Inside settings, click on the “System” tab on the left side of the screen. Or, you can navigate directly here by typing **chrome://settings/system** on the address bar.

![System option](/images/6226849/24983be7-image.png)

3. Next, disable “Use hardware acceleration when available”, and click on ReLaunch to restart your browser. After that, you should be able to record with no problems.

![Hardware acceleration toggle](/images/6255804/6ef1e2d4-image.png)

4. If your problem continues and you have a dedicated graphic card, go into its configuration and try to disable hardware acceleration (or enable it if it was disabled).

If you are using the Sendspark Chrome extension, follow these steps to disable Camera Effects:

1. **Access the Sendspark Chrome Extension**: Click the Sendspark icon in your Chrome toolbar.
2. **Disable Camera Effects**: Look for "Camera Effects" within the extension's settings or toolbar and toggle it off.

There is a lot that can go wrong from software to hardware, so there is not only one solution. I hope this helps you resolve your recording problems.

For users of the Sendspark Chrome extension, disabling Camera Effects could also be a helpful solution as it reduces the computational load of the extension and prevents the black screen issue.
