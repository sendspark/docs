If you're having trouble logging into Sendspark, the fix is usually to clear your browser cache for sendspark.com. This page has step-by-step instructions for Edge, Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. If clearing your cache doesn't work, you can reset your password or log in via Google authentication.

## Key takeaways

- Most Sendspark login problems are resolved by clearing your browser's cache for sendspark.com.
- Instructions are provided for Edge, Google Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.
- If you still can't log in after clearing your cache, reset your password or log in via Google authentication.

Here are the steps for how to clear your cache on different web browsers. Once you clear your cache, you should be able to log into Sendspark again.

## Edge:

- Go to edge://settings/siteData?searchSubpage=sendspark.com
- Click "Remove all shown"

## Google Chrome

- Go to chrome://settings/content/all?searchSubpage=sendspark.com
- Click "Delete displayed data"

## Firefox

- Go to `about:preferences#privacy`
- Head to "Cookies and Site Data" section
- Click "Manage Data"
- Search for [sendspark.com](https://sendspark.com/)
- Click "Remove all shown"

## Safari

- Select Preferences under the Safari menu.
- Select the Privacy tab.
- Select Manage Website Data.
- Search for [sendspark.com](https://sendspark.com/)
- Click "Remove all"

After you've cleared your cache, if you STILL can't log in, you can try reseting your password or logging in via Google authentication.
