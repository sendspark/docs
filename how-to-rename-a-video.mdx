## There are 4 different ways and/ or locations where a video can be renamed:

**1) Hover your video and head to the 3 dots to the right:**

![Video thumbnail with three dots menu button visible on hover](/images/9591974/7a82fd9e-23.gif)

**Click on Rename to enable the editing mode**

---

**2) If you are in the Edit Section of the video (by clicking on the video), you can simply hit the Name to enable the Edit mode:**

![Video edit section with clickable name field for renaming](/images/9591974/20d8a18e-33.gif)

---

**3) When finishing a recording with the Chrome Extension, you can also set the name for the video:**

![Chrome extension recording completion dialog with video name input field](/images/9591974/2b6d54fd-44.gif)

---

**4) Finally, the same procedure applies to a recording made through the Desktop App**

![Desktop app recording finish screen with name assignment option](/images/9591974/26202476-55.gif)
