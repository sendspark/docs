You can rename a Sendspark video in four places: hover a video thumbnail and use the three-dot menu, click the name directly in the video's edit section, or set the name when you finish a recording in the Chrome Extension or the Desktop App. Each option opens an inline editing mode.

## Key takeaways

- There are four ways to rename a video: the three-dot menu on hover, clicking the name in the edit section, at the end of a Chrome Extension recording, or at the end of a Desktop App recording.
- Hovering a thumbnail and clicking the three dots reveals a Rename option that opens editing mode.
- Clicking a video opens its edit section, where clicking the name enables inline editing.
- You can name a recording the moment you finish it in both the Chrome Extension and the Desktop App.

## There are 4 different ways and/ or locations where a video can be renamed:

**1) Hover your video and head to the 3 dots to the right:**
