To remove breakpoints from combined videos, open Workspace Settings from your profile menu in the bottom left, scroll to Miscellaneous Options, and toggle off "Show Breakpoints for Combined Videos." This is a global setting, so it applies only to newly created videos, not to ones you recorded before.

## Key takeaways

- A **breakpoint** is the small gap Sendspark shows on the progress bar of a **combined video** to mark the transition from one clip to the next.
- You remove it from **Workspace Settings**: open your profile menu (bottom left), then find **Miscellaneous Options** at the bottom of the page.
- Toggle off **Show Breakpoints for Combined Videos** to get a seamless timeline.
- This is a **global setting** that applies only to **newly created videos** — videos recorded before the change are not affected.

When you create **combined videos** in Sendspark, the video player shows a small gap (called a **breakpoint**) on the progress bar to indicate the transition between one video and the next.

![Sendspark video player progress bar showing breakpoints between combined video segments](/images/12099743/852a3e96-image.png)

If you’d prefer a seamless timeline without that gap, you can turn this option off in your workspace settings.

⚠️ **Important:** This is a **global setting**, which means it will only apply to **newly created videos**. Any videos you recorded before will not be affected.

---

## Steps to Remove Breakpoints

1. **Open Workspace Settings**
  - Click on your profile menu in the **bottom left-hand corner** of your Sendspark dashboard.
  - From the dropdown, select **Workspace Settings**.
2. **Scroll Down to Miscellaneous Options**
  - At the bottom of the Workspace Settings page, you’ll find a section labeled **Miscellaneous Options**.
3. **Turn Off Breakpoints**
  - Find the option labeled **Show Breakpoints for Combined Videos**.
  - Toggle it **off** to remove breakpoints from the timeline.

![Workspace Settings page with Show Breakpoints for Combined Videos toggle in Miscellaneous Options](/images/12099743/28855380-image.png)

---

## That’s It! 🎉

Once this setting is turned off, any new combined videos you create will play seamlessly without showing breakpoints in the progress bar.
