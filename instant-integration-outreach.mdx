## Before you get started:

Please install the [Sendspark Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph) to use the Outreach integration.

As long as you have the Chrome Extension installed, you’ll automatically be able to access Sendspark from Outreach.

Install Chrome Extension

---

## How To Share Videos in Outreach

## 1. Open an Email or Template in Outreach

[Log in](https://app1e.outreach.io/360) to Outreach, and open the email you template you want to add a video to. You'll start seeing a Sendspark icon in your email toolbar.

![Outreach email toolbar with Sendspark icon visible among other formatting tools](/images/5724793/89468174-image.png)

**Note:** you must have the [Sendspark chrome extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph/related?hl=en-US) installed and be using a Chrome browser to see the Sendspark icon.

## 3. Click on the Sendspark icon

Click on the icon to launch video the Sendspark menu. You'll want to click **Record New Video**to record a video.

![Sendspark menu dropdown showing Record New Video option selected](/images/5724793/cebffd3c-image.png)

## 4. Record your video

Use the Sendspark extension to record yourself or your screen (or both together). After recording, you'll be able to preview your video in a modal over Outreach.

![Sendspark video recorder interface with preview of recorded video in modal window](/images/5724793/5e73b9dd-image.png)

## 5. Copy Linked GIF

Click t**he Copy Linked GIF**button to copy the video information to your clipboard.

## 6. Paste into your Outreach email

Now, you can just paste your video preview into Outreach. You'll see an animated GIF preview of your video appear in your email, all ready to send!

![Outreach email with animated GIF video preview pasted into message body](/images/5724793/a536b2c0-image.png)

**Note:** Outreach asks if you want to paste with formatted text, or unformatted text. it doesn't matter – either option will work!

When you send these video emails to your prospects, they'll see a captivating GIF preview of your video, and be able to click to watch the full video in their browser.

Let us know if you have any questions by clicking the chatbot on the bottom right of the page.

Happy recording!
