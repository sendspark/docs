To embed your Mixmax calendar with a Sendspark video, open your video, scroll down to the **Calendar** section, toggle it **On**, select **Mixmax** from the provider dropdown, and paste the link to your Mixmax calendar. As soon as you paste the link, the calendar appears in the preview on the right, so viewers can book a meeting right after watching.

## Key takeaways

- Adding a Mixmax calendar takes three steps: toggle **Calendar** on, choose **Mixmax**, and paste your calendar link.
- Paste the **link** to your Mixmax calendar, not embed code. Sendspark builds the embed from the link for you.
- The calendar appears live in the preview area on the right the moment you add the link.
- Viewers can book a meeting from your calendar without leaving the video page.

Embed your [Mixmax](https://www.mixmax.com?utm_source=sendspark&utm_medium=partner&utm_campaign=sendspark_referral_2024) calendar next to your Sendspark videos to help prospects book meetings right after watching.

## How to Embed Mixmax Calendar Link Next To Videos

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/OAfwyQw1zs0?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Open your Sendspark video and scroll down to the **Calendar** section.
2. Toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**.
3. Select **"Mixmax"** from the provider dropdown list.
4. **Add the link** to the Mixmax calendar you want to use.

![Select Mixmax calendar option](/images/9052037/287d9523-image.png)

As soon as you paste the link, your embedded Mixmax calendar appears in the preview area on the right, where your viewers can book a meeting directly.
