## Before you start

✅ A Sendspark account with an active subscription on the Growth, Business, or Scale plan. Other plans (including Free and trials) don’t have access to the MCP integration.

✅ An AI assistant that supports custom (remote) MCP connectors (e.g. Claude, ChatGPT, Mistral Le Chat, Perplexity, Grok).

✅ The Sendspark MCP URL:

```
https://apiv2.sendspark.com/api/mcp
```

---

## How to connect

In your AI assistant, add a custom (remote) MCP connector and paste the Sendspark MCP URL above. Your assistant will then open a Sendspark sign-in page — log in and approve access. There are no API keys or tokens to copy, and the connection stays linked to your account until you remove it.

## What information your assistant can access

🔒 The connection is read-only — your assistant can look things up but cannot create, edit, or delete anything. It only sees data in the workspaces you belong to (and that are active). Specifically:

| Area | What it can read |
| --- | --- |
| Workspaces | The workspaces you're a member of and your role in each. |
| Dynamic Video campaigns | Campaign name, status, settings, and share-page setup (title, message, layout, CTA buttons, calendar). |
| Prospects | Per prospect: name, email, company, job title, status/processing state, and (for completed ones) the share/embed links and engagement stats. |
| Prospect status | Detailed per-prospect state — generation/validation steps and why a prospect may be invalid or still processing. |
| Analytics | Lifetime engagement for a Dynamic Video: visits, plays, CTA clicks, and thumbs-up, plus a visitor breakdown (location, device, watch %). (Full engagement analytics — including the per-visitor breakdown — are available on the Growth, Business, and Scale plans.) |

🚫 It does not access billing details, passwords, other users' private data, or workspaces you're not part of.

## Managing or removing access

⚙️ You can disconnect Sendspark at any time from your AI client's connector/MCP settings. Revoking there immediately stops the assistant from accessing your data.

## Good to know

ℹ️ Access requires a workspace with an active subscription on the Growth, Business, or Scale plan. Workspaces on any other plan (or without an active subscription) won’t have their data available through the assistant.
