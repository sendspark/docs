## Before you get started:

Please install the [Sendspark Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph) to use the Zendesk integration.

As long as you have the Chrome Extension installed, you’ll automatically be able to access Sendspark from Zendesk.

Install Chrome Extension

---

## How to Send Videos in Zendesk

## 1. Click the Sendspark Icon in Zendesk

Create a **new conversation**or open an**existing conversation in Zendesk**. You’ll see the Sendspark logo appear in the compose window next to the native Zendesk options.

![Sendspark logo icon in Zendesk compose window next to native options](/images/5809245/6ee173c7-image.png)

**Note:** you must have the [Sendspark chrome extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph/related?hl=en-US) installed and be using a Chrome browser to see the Sendspark icon.

## 2. Record a New Video

Click **Record New Video**.

![Record New Video button in Sendspark menu within Zendesk](/images/5809245/1c590890-image.png)

This will launch the Sendspark Chrome Extension, so you can record a video of yourself or your screen, right from here!

![Sendspark Chrome Extension video recording interface with camera and screen options](/images/5809245/84fc7dac-image.png)

Alternatively, you can choose from the list of options:

- **Request Video** — inserts a [video request link](https://www.sendspark.com/video-platform/request-videos) to easily get a video recording or testimonial from your custome
- **Add Last Recording** — inserts the last Sendspark video you recorded
- **Video Library** — opens your Sendspark dashboard to find or manage existing videos

## 3. Preview Video

After you record your video, you'll be able to preview and edit the video before sending it to your customer.

![Video preview window showing recorded video with playback controls](/images/5809245/d5308c91-image.png)

## 4. Edit Video

You can trim your video, add a template, or combine it with another video, all without leaving Zendesk.

- Click on **"Select Template"** to add page text, call-to-action buttons, and an embedded calendar to videos in one click. You can edit templates in the Templates tab in Sendspark. ​![Select Template button and template options for adding text and call-to-action](/images/5809245/9d121520-image.png)
- Click the**"video +"**button to stitch another video to your recorded video. This is helpful when you want to share a personalized note along with a pre-recorded demo video. ​![Video plus button for stitching multiple videos together](/images/5809245/79a7f1f4-image.png)
- Click the **"scissors"** button to trim your video before sending it off ​![Scissors button for trimming and editing video length](/images/5809245/ba9bb5cd-image.png)
- And click the**"pencil"** icon to open this video in a new tab inside of Sendspark for more customization. ​![Pencil icon to open video in Sendspark for advanced customization](/images/5809245/b0677ca4-image.png) ​

## 5. Insert into Message

When you're ready to send, click **Insert Video** to insert it into your Zendesk message

![Insert Video button to add the recorded video to Zendesk message](/images/5809245/f159abc1-image.png)

## 6. Send

That's it! You're all set to send your video to your customer.

![Final Zendesk message with video inserted ready to send to customer](/images/5809245/cd68b889-image.png)
