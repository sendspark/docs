To send dynamic videos in QuickMail, create a dynamic video campaign in Sendspark, add your contacts and generate their videos, then click Share Campaign and select QuickMail to copy the snippet. Paste it directly into a QuickMail email campaign, where it appears as a Sendspark placeholder that updates to each recipient's personalized video when the campaign sends.

## Key takeaways

- Send AI-personalized videos in QuickMail by pasting a Sendspark **Share Campaign** snippet into a QuickMail email campaign.
- Generate videos for your contacts in Sendspark **before** sharing, so each recipient has a personalized video ready.
- The pasted snippet shows as a **Sendspark placeholder** in QuickMail and resolves to the right video per recipient when the campaign runs.
- Use QuickMail's **preview** to confirm contacts will receive their dynamic videos before turning the campaign on.

Want to see up to 3x engagement from QuickMail campaigns? Try sending AI personalized videos to stand out and boost engagement.

## How to Send AI Personalized Videos in QuickMail

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/T51LLyFvRFE?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

Sendspark makes it easy to send AI-personalized videos in QuickMail. Simply follow these steps.

1. Create your **dynamic video campaign**in Sendspark
2. After adding contacts and generating videos, click **"Share Campaign"**
3. Select the option for **QuickMail**![Share AI Videos in QuickMail](/images/9141642/ef157e33-image.png)
4. **Paste** directly in a QuickMail email campaign. You should see the Sendspark placeholder now. ​![Paste Into QuickMail](/images/9141642/12977e17-image.png)
5. You can **preview this campaign** for your contacts to make sure they will be receiving the dynamic videos ​![Preview AI Videos in QuickMail](/images/9141642/2604fd72-image.png)

And that's it! When you're happy, just turn on the campaign :)
