To send personalized videos in Skylead, record or upload one video to Sendspark, customize the landing page and thumbnail (adding personalization tokens if you want each viewer's details), click Share, select Skylead, then paste the snippet into your Skylead message and send. Sendspark automatically personalizes the video for each recipient.

## Key takeaways

- You create one video and Sendspark automatically personalizes it for each person in your Skylead outreach.
- Use [personalization tokens](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale) on the landing page and thumbnail to add each viewer's information automatically.
- If Skylead isn't in the Share list, [modify the share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal) to add it.
- Recipients only see the video preview (not just a link) if they've accepted your LinkedIn connection or InMail request, so videos work best as a follow-up, not in connection requests.

## Why Send Personalized Videos in Skylead

Sending personalized videos in Skylead can increase replies and meetings from your LinkedIn automation.

The Sendspark Skylead integration makes it easy to create one video, and automatically personalize it for each person you're reaching out to via Skylead.

6. Send!

## Note About Sending Videos on LinkedIn

In order for your recipient to see the preview of your video, and not just a link, they must have either (A) accepted your LinkedIn request, or (B) accepted your InMail request.

For this reason, we do NOT recommend sending videos in connection requests. **Videos are better used as a follow up message, after you are already connected.**
