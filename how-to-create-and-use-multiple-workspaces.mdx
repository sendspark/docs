To create a new workspace in Sendspark, click your avatar in the bottom-left corner, choose **Change Workspace**, click **Create Workspace**, and enter your company name. Each workspace is fully independent — with its own brand styles, team members, and videos — and you can toggle between them anytime from the same menu.

## Key takeaways

- Workspaces work like Slack workspaces: each is completely independent (brand styles, team members, video content), but easy to switch between.
- They're useful for managing multiple brands within one company, running Sendspark for 2+ companies, or helping clients use Sendspark.
- Create one via avatar → **Change Workspace** → **Create Workspace** → add your **Company Name**.
- Toggle back anytime with **Change Workspace**, then select the workspace you want.

## How Workspaces Work

Sendspark workspaces are a great way to manage multiple brands or teams. They are similar to Slack workspaces -- every workspace is completely independent of another (with unique brand styles, team members, video content, etc.), but it's easy for you to toggle between them.

You might enjoy using multiple workspaces if you work with multiple brands within your company, want to use Sendspark for 2+ companies, or are helping your clients use Sendspark.

## How to Create a New Workspace

To create a new workspace simply click on the avatar in the bottom left corner of your account and then click on **Change Workspace**

![Create new workspace](/images/4873966/c004fb82-image.png)

Now click **Create Workspace:**

![Change Workspace modal](/images/4873966/db843709-image.png)

Add your **Company Name** and you're all set!

![Add Company Name](/images/4873966/0b85a70c-image.png)

## How to Toggle Between Workspaces

You can get back to your original workspace the same way: just click **Change Workspace**, and then select the workspace you want to be in.

![Change Workspace modal](/images/4873966/c15e43d0-image.png)

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are workspaces independent from each other?">
    Yes. Like Slack workspaces, every Sendspark workspace is completely independent — each has its own brand styles, team members, and video content — while still being easy to toggle between from one account.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I switch between workspaces?">
    Click your avatar in the bottom-left corner, choose **Change Workspace**, and select the workspace you want to be in. You use the same menu to switch back.

  <Accordion title="When should I use multiple workspaces?">
    Multiple workspaces are helpful if you manage multiple brands within your company, use Sendspark for two or more companies, or help your clients use Sendspark.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I find my Workspace ID?">
    Open [Workspace Settings](https://sendspark.com/settings/workspace), click the Manage Workspace tab, and click the hidden Workspace ID on the right-hand side to copy it. See [How to find your Workspace ID](/how-to-find-your-workspace-id) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I'm on an agency plan — how do I activate a new workspace?">
    Create the workspace, then copy its Workspace ID and share it with Tech Support so they can allocate minutes to it. See [How to create and activate a new workspace](/how-to-create-and-activate-a-new-workspace).
