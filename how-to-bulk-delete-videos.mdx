To bulk-delete videos in Sendspark, drag to select the videos you want to remove, hover over any of them and click the 3 dots to open the BULK DELETE option, then type DELETE in all caps and confirm. Deleted videos cannot be retrieved, so move any you might reuse into a folder first.

## Key takeaways

- Bulk-delete in three actions: drag to select videos, click the 3 dots to open BULK DELETE, then type DELETE in all caps to confirm.
- Deletion is permanent. Once videos are deleted they cannot be retrieved.
- If you are unsure, create a new folder and move the videos there so you can re-use them in a later campaign instead of deleting them.
- There are several other places you can delete videos individually, covered in the [how to delete a video](/how-to-delete-a-video) article.

Sendspark makes it easy to delete many videos at once.

Deleting videos is always a good idea to maintain a neat library, but keep in mind that once the videos are deleted, they cannot be retrieved.

If you are not entirely sure about deleting the video, we suggest creating a new folder and transferring the videos over to that folder so that you can re-use them at a later campaign.

There are many ways and places where you can delete videos. Take a look at [this article](/how-to-delete-a-video) to learn more about it.
