Sendspark has an instant integration with ZoomInfo that allows you to record and insert videos directly into ZoomInfo emails.

This can be used with ZoomInfo Copilot to create AI-personalized video emails at scale.

## How The Sendspark + ZoomInfo Copilot Works:

1. Install the [Sendspark chrome extension](https://chrome). This is how you access the the Sendspark + ZoomInfo integration.
2. Navigate to ZoomInfo and draft your email. If you don't know what to say, try using ZoomInfo's AI Copilot feature to draft the email for you. ​![ZoomInfo email compose window with AI Copilot draft email ready to send](/images/11726880/24587d42-image.png)
3. Select the Sendspark icon from the email compose window. ​![ZoomInfo email compose toolbar with Sendspark icon highlighted and clickable](/images/11726880/b47fc5c0-image.png)
4. Now you'll see 4 options... ​![Sendspark menu showing four options: Record New Video, Request Video, Add Last Recording, Video Library](/images/11726880/96d5c8ee-image.png)
5. This is what each of them does:
  1. **Record New Video** — Lets you record a personalized video of yourself, your screen, or yourself talking over your screen in a floating bubble. After recording, you can preview the video and insert it right into your email.
  2. **Request Video** — Lets you send a "video request link," which is a quick way to get a video from someone else.
  3. **Add Last Recording** — Lets you quickly add the last recording you made on Sendspark into your email
  4. **Video Library**— Lets you see your entire Sendspark video library and select the video you'd like to use.

## Frequently Asked Questions About the ZoomInfo Integration

## Who can use the Sendspark + ZoomInfo integration?

- You must have active Sendspark and ZoomInfo accounts to use the integration. This integration is available on every plan.

## Who is the Sendspark + ZoomInfo Integration best for?

- The Sendspark + ZoomInfo integration is best for BDRs, SDRs, and sales folks who want to send AI-personalized video emails. This integration is not necessarily for sending massive email campaigns, but rather, for using AI to automate some of the tedious work creating personalized emails.
