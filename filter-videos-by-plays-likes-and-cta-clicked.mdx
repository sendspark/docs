To filter videos by engagement in Sendspark, navigate to the video library or folder you want, then under **Filters** choose CTA Clicked, Liked, or Played. This shows only videos that have had that activity. Refresh the page to clear the filters.

## Key takeaways

- Filters let you see only videos that have had activity, so you can focus on your most-engaged content.
- **CTA Clicked** shows only videos where a call-to-action button was clicked.
- **Liked** shows only videos that received a thumbs up from a viewer; **Played** shows only videos played by someone other than yourself.
- Refresh the page to clear the filters. You can also [search by title](/how-to-search-videos-in-sendspark) or [filter by creators](/filter-videos-by-creators).

Sendspark makes it easy to filter for videos that have been engaged with, so you can see only videos that have had activity.

## How to Filter Videos

1. Navigate to the correct video library or folder ![Filter Videos](/images/6299939/756987dc-image.png)
2. Under **Filters**, select the filter you would like to use:
  1. **CTA Clicked:**shows only videos that have had a call-to-action button clicked on.
  2. **Liked**: shows only videos that have a received a thumbs up from a viewer
  3. **Played**: shows only videos that have been played by someone other than yourself
3. Refresh the page to clear filters

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What do the three filters mean?">
    **CTA Clicked** shows only videos that have had a call-to-action button clicked. **Liked** shows only videos that received a thumbs up from a viewer. **Played** shows only videos that have been played by someone other than yourself.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does 'Played' count my own views?">
    No. The **Played** filter shows videos that have been played by someone other than yourself, so your own views don't cause a video to appear.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I clear the filters?">
    Refresh the page to clear any filters you've applied and return to your full video list.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I combine this with other ways of finding videos?">
    Yes. You can also [search videos by title](/how-to-search-videos-in-sendspark) or [filter by creators](/filter-videos-by-creators) in your Team Library to narrow down your library.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
