## 🔧 How to Edit Prospect Variables and Regenerate Videos in the Workspace

In your Sendspark workspace, you can now **edit prospect variables** after a video has been generated. Here's how it works and what to expect regarding video regeneration limits.

---

## ✏️ Step 1: Click the Edit Button

Once you've generated a prospect, you'll see an **“Edit”** button next to it.

Clicking this button opens a drawer with options to update the prospect’s information.

![Sendspark prospect list with Edit button visible next to a generated prospect](/images/11887180/24e66ab6-image.png)

---

## 🧩 Step 2: Understand What Fields Trigger Video Regeneration

Inside the drawer:

- **Fields inside the DYNAMIC VIDEO tab will regenerate the video:**

![Dynamic Video tab in prospect editor drawer showing variable fields that trigger regeneration](/images/11887180/6323b460-image.png)

You can safely change non-regeneration fields in the PAGE tab without triggering a new video.

![Page tab in prospect editor drawer showing non-regeneration fields safe to edit](/images/11887180/cc046023-image.png)

---

## 🔁 Step 3: Regeneration Limits

To avoid overuse, **you can regenerate a video up to 3 times for free**. After that, regenerations may incur charges.

You'll see a visual indicator of how many regenerations are left:

![Video regeneration counter display showing remaining free regenerations out of three total](/images/11887180/881afdd0-image.png)

---

## ✅ Step 4: Update Page Without Regenerating

If you only update fields **in the PAGE section**, the video page is updated immediately without any regeneration needed — and there's **no limit** on how many times you can do this.
