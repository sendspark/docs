Uploading avatars to Sendspark takes about five minutes and personalizes your whole experience. There are two to add: your **headshot** in your account settings and your **company logo** in your workspace settings. Once uploaded, they appear in the Sendspark dropdown menu, on your video request pages, and when you switch between workspaces.

## Key takeaways

- Add your headshot in your account settings and your company logo in your workspace settings — two separate places.
- The whole process takes about five minutes.
- Uploaded avatars appear in the Sendspark dropdown menu, on your video request pages, and when you toggle between workspaces.

It takes 5 minutes to upload avatars to Sendspark to make your whole experience a lot nicer. There are two places you want to upload avatars:

1. Add your **headshot** in your [account settings](https://sendspark.com/settings/account)

![Upload Your Avatar to Sendspark](/images/5548696/2e82a9b2-image.png)

2. Add your **company logo** to your [workspace settings](https://sendspark.com/settings/workspace)

![Upload Your Company Logo to Sendspark](/images/5548696/5a67c0ba-image.png)

---

You'll see these avatars in a few places once they've been uploaded:

1. In the Sendspark dropdown menu

![Check Your Sendspark Avatars](/images/5548696/0b784ce9-image.png)

2. On your video request pages

![Sendspark Video Request Page](/images/5548696/1994ea15-image.png)

![Sendspark Workspace Manager](/images/5548696/60fca75a-image.png)
