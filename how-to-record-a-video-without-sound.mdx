To record a video without sound in Sendspark, uncheck **"Share tab audio"** at the bottom left of the recording modal. If you're recording the **Entire Screen**, also disable **"Share system audio."** To remove your voice, open **Settings** and toggle the **Microphone** option off. Muting the mic only works when recording with **Screen** or **Both** — you cannot record a face-only video without sound.

## Key takeaways

- To silence a video, turn off each audio source you don't want: tab audio, system audio, and your microphone.
- Uncheck **"Share tab audio"** in the recording modal; if recording the Entire Screen, also disable **"Share system audio."**
- Mute your voice by opening **Settings** and toggling the **Microphone** off. This works only with **Screen** or **Both** recordings, not face-only videos.
- The microphone setting saves to your defaults, so remember to toggle sound back on the next time you want to speak while recording.

## How to disable each audio source

| Audio source | How to turn it off |
| --- | --- |
| Tab audio | Uncheck **"Share tab audio"** at the bottom left of the recording modal |
| System audio | Disable **"Share system audio"** (shown when recording the **Entire Screen**) |
| Microphone (your voice) | Open **Settings** and toggle the **Microphone** option off |

To record videos without sound from the Sendspark Chrome Extension, simply uncheck the "Share tab audio" at the bottom left of the **Recording**modal:

![Share tab audio](/images/4293032/3339caac-image.png)

If you chose **Entire Screen**,disable the option **Share system audio** as well:

---

You can also mute your microphone entirely when recording a video. Simply click on **Settings**and toggle the Microphone option off:

![Toolbar](/images/4293032/aeaee911-image.png)

## Please note:

- This will only work when recording with **Screen** or **Both**. You can NOT record a **face** video without sound.
- This setting will save to your defaults, so make sure to toggle the sound on again next time you would like to speak while recording! 🔇

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to turn the sound back on next time?">
