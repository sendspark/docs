## 🤖 Use Agentic Workflows to automatically generate personalized Dynamic Videos whenever new data is added to a Google Sheet Row.

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Nh8u40hbBcg?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

## 🎥 Step 1: Create a Dynamic Video Campaign

Create your Dynamic Video Campaign and configure all personalization variables.

https://sendspark.com/dynamic-videos

---

## 📊 Step 2: Prepare Your Google Sheet

Create a Google Sheet and add the columns you want to use for personalization (First Name, Email, Background URL, etc.).

---

## ⚙️ Step 3: Create an Agentic Workflow

Go to **Agentic Workflows** and create a new automation. https://sendspark.com/agentic-workflows

- Select **Google Sheets** as the trigger.
- Choose the spreadsheet you want to monitor.

---

## 🚀 Step 4: Add Sendspark as the Action

Add a new action and select:

**Sendspark → Create Dynamic Video**

Then choose the Dynamic Video Campaign you created earlier.

## 🔗 Step 5: Map Your Fields

Match the columns from Google Sheets with the corresponding variables in your Dynamic Video Campaign.

## ✅ Step 6: Enable the Workflow

Save the workflow and turn it on.

Any new rows added to the spreadsheet will automatically generate personalized Dynamic Videos.

## 🎉 That's It!

Your Dynamic Videos will now be generated automatically using the data stored in Google Sheets.
