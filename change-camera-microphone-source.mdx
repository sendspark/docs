You can change your camera or microphone source in Sendspark to record with any external device. From the Chrome Extension, open **Settings** and pick the camera or microphone from the selector. From the web app, launch the recorder, click the **Settings gear**, and choose your input. Scroll the selector to see all available devices.

## Key takeaways

- Change your camera or mic to record from any external device.
- In the Chrome Extension: open **Settings**, then choose your camera or microphone from the selector.
- In the web app: launch the recorder, click the **Settings gear**, then choose your input.
- Scroll the selector to see and select all available sources.

It's easy to change your camera or microphone source in Sendspark, so you can record a video from any external camera or microphone.

## Change Camera / Mic from the Chrome Extension

- After launching the Chrome Extension, click into **Settings**
- Click into the Camera or Microphone selector
- Scroll, and select the camera or microphone source you would like to use!

![Change Camera Source](/images/5949263/fcca7cd3-image.png)

## Change Camera / Mic from the Web Application

- After launching the recorder, click the **Settings gear**
- Click into the Camera or Microphone selector
- Scroll, and select the camera or microphone source you would like to use!

![Check Camera Source from Web](/images/5949263/06dec3b7-image.png)
