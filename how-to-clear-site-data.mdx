Is Sendspark not working for you on Google Chrome? Follow these steps to reset Sendspark on Chrome!

1. **Right click** over the Sendspark page and select **Inspect**

![Inspect Elements](/images/6619481/ef7b96ad-image.png)

2. Go to **Application** → **Storage** and click **"Clear site data"**  
​

![Inspector](/images/6619481/d08aaced-image.png)

3. **Refresh the page**and log in again.
