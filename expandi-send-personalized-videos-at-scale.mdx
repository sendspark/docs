## Why Send Auto-Personalized Videos in Expandi

Sending auto-personalized videos in Expandi can help you connect more, at scale!

The Sendspark Expandi integration makes it easy to create one video, and automatically personalized it for each contact you're reaching out to via Expandi LinkedIn automation.

## How to Send Mass Personalized Videos in Expandi

Watch the video or follow the steps below!

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/7yxXmxO1YP0?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. [Record](/record-yourself) or [upload](/upload-a-video-to-sendspark) your video to Sendspark
2. Customize the [video landing page](/customize-your-video-landing-page) and [video thumbnail](/how-to-edit-video-thumbnails) (you can use [personalization tokens](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale) to automatically add your viewer's information to the video)
3. Click**"share"**
4. Select **"Expandi"** (If you don't see Expandi, follow these steps to [modify the share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal))
5. Paste into your message on Expandi
6. Note: you will see a warning about the personalization fields on Expandi. That is expected, no worries!
7. Send!

## Note About Sending Videos on LinkedIn

In order for your recipient to see the preview of your video, and not just a link, they must have either (A) accepted your LinkedIn request, or (B) accepted your InMail request.

For this reason, we do NOT recommend sending videos in connection requests. **Videos are better used as a follow up message, after you are already connected.**
