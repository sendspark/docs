To send a video email in Outlook, record or upload your video, click Share and then Customize, open Edit Preview, and select the **Static** thumbnail option (not Animated). Then paste it into your email draft. Outlook is finicky about images and video, so always use Static and never embed a video or use a GIF.

## Key takeaways

- Outlook is extra finicky about supporting images and videos, so always use the **Static** thumbnail option.
- Do NOT try to embed a video or use a GIF in Outlook.
- Send a video in five steps: record or upload your video, click Share then Customize, click Edit Preview, choose Static, then paste into your email draft.
