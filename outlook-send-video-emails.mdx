---
title: "Outlook — Send Video Emails"
description: "Learn how to send video emails in Outlook using static thumbnails to avoid compatibility issues with embedded videos and GIFs."
---

Outlook is extra finicky about supporting images and videos, soplease use the**Static**thumbnail option. (Do NOT try to embed a video or use a GIF).

---

To share a video in Outlook:

1) Record or upload your video.

2) Click **Share**and then click on**Customize**

![Share modal with Customization option](/images/5569060/14b69f99-image.png)

3) At the bottom, click on the middle option **Edit Preview**

![Edit Thumbnail modal with Edit Preview option](/images/5569060/a7c06121-image.png)

4) And finally, You will see **Animated**& **Static.**For this particular case, only use**Static**

![Thumbnail Type with Animatic and Static options](/images/5781288/234a2474-image.png)

**5)**Paste into your email draft

![Outlook Email Draft](/images/5548709/e8c0e695-image.png)
