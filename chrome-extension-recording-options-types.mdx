Using our Chrome Extension is a great way to record videos anywhere, anytime!

## Recording Options

There are three options to record with the **Chrome Extension**.

![Toolbar options](/images/5152208/1e8f036a-new.png)

**Face (Camera Only)**

With this option, you'll be able to record only your camera.

**Screen (Screen Only)**

With this option selected, you'll be able to record only your screen.

**Both (Cam + Screen)**

With this option selected, you'll be able to record your camera over your screen.

**⚠ Please note:**The face camera feature in Sendspark is designed to work seamlessly on Chrome. However, there are instances where certain apps with a full-screen mode can override Sendspark's bubble recording functionality.

With each recording option, you can choose a recording type as well, to get your recordings just right!

---

## Recording Types

Once you've clicked on "Face", "Screen", or "Both" you are now almost ready to start. Click on "Record" and a new modal will pop up:

![Recording options](/images/5152208/a8774b22-image.png)

**⚠ Please note:**Cam only - You can't change any of the options for **Cam Only** since we're only recording your camera!

**Current Tab**

This option will only record what's below the address bar.

![Tab recording](/images/5152208/c0d0ed8e-recording-types-breakdown-current-tab.png)

**Application window**

This option will record the whole application window of the application you've selected

![Windows recording](/images/5152208/ad114dc2-recording-types-breakdown-application-window.png)

**Entire screen**

This option will record your entire screen/desktop - folders, anything on the screen.

![Entire Screen recording](/images/5152208/118f4ceb-recording-types-breakdown-entire-screen.png)

👋 Happy recording!
