To set or change your logo, go to the **Library** section in Sendspark, open your menu and select **Manage Workspace**, click **Set Global Styles**, find **Upload Your Logo**, and upload your file. Your logo appears in the top-left corner of the video landing page viewers see when they click your shared links. Use a horizontal logo format for the best fit.

## Key takeaways

- Upload or update your logo under **Library → Manage Workspace → Set Global Styles → Upload Your Logo**.
- Your logo appears in the **top-left corner** of the video landing page viewers see when they click your shared video links or thumbnails.
- Use a **horizontal logo format** — the logo sits in a navigation bar whose height can't be changed, so vertical logos appear smaller.
- Consider a simplified, web-friendly version of your logo (as companies like Squarespace and Airbnb do), sometimes dropping the company text and keeping just the symbol.

This guide will walk you through setting up your company logo in Sendspark for the first time or updating an existing logo. Your logo appears in the top-left corner of the video landing page that viewers see when they click on your shared video links.

## Setting or Changing Your Logo

Follow these steps to upload or update your workspace logo:

1. Navigate to the **Library** section in your Sendspark dashboard
2. Click on your menu and select **Manage Workspace**
3. Click on **Set Global Styles**
4. Find the **Upload Your Logo** option
5. Upload your logo file

## Logo Requirements and Best Practices

## Use Horizontal Logo Format

Your logo should be in a **horizontal format** rather than vertical. Here's why:

- The logo is placed within a navigation bar at the top of the page
- Vertical logos appear smaller because you cannot modify the navbar height
- Horizontal logos follow standard web design practices

**Tip:** Consider creating a simplified version of your logo specifically for web use. Many companies like Squarespace and Airbnb use logo variations that work better in horizontal spaces - sometimes removing company text and keeping just the logo symbol.

## What Viewers Will See

Once uploaded, your logo will appear in the top-left corner of the video landing page that customers see when they click on your shared video links or thumbnails.
