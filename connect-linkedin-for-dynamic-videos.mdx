To connect LinkedIn to Sendspark for dynamic video campaigns, start a dynamic video campaign, open the "Integration" section during campaign setup, select LinkedIn, authorize the connection with the required permissions, and complete setup. Once connected, you can pull LinkedIn profiles and content into your videos for richer personalization.

## Key takeaways

- Connecting LinkedIn lets you incorporate LinkedIn profiles directly into your dynamic video content.
- You connect during campaign setup by navigating to the "Integration" section and selecting the LinkedIn option.
- Authorizing the connection includes granting Sendspark the required permissions before setup is complete.
- LinkedIn integration enables enhanced personalization and lets you use profiles and assets to connect with viewers.

## How can I connect my LinkedIn account to Sendspark for dynamic video campaigns?

Using LinkedIn with your Sendspark dynamic video campaigns unlocks advanced features like integrating LinkedIn profiles and content into your videos. Here is a guide to effectively connecting your LinkedIn account to Sendspark.

## Why Connect LinkedIn to Sendspark?

1. **Access LinkedIn Profiles:** Incorporate LinkedIn profiles directly into your video content when developing dynamic video campaigns.
2. **Personalized Video Interactions:** Leverage LinkedIn profile information to create more dynamic and engaging videos in Sendspark campaigns.
3. **Enrich Campaign Content:** Use LinkedIn as a direct content source for your video campaigns.

## What are the steps to connect LinkedIn to Sendspark?

Here's a step-by-step article to show you the steps: [How to Make Dynamic Videos with LinkedIn Profiles](/how-to-make-dynamic-videos-with-linkedin-profiles)

1. **Initiate a Dynamic Video Campaign:** Start creating your campaign in Sendspark.
2. **Proceed to Campaign Setup:** During the campaign setup process, navigate to the "Integration" section.
3. **Select LinkedIn Integration:** At this stage, you will see an option to connect your LinkedIn account. Click on it.
4. **Authorize the Connection:** Follow the on-screen prompts to authorize Sendspark to connect with your LinkedIn account. This process will include granting required permissions.
5. **Complete Setup:** Once authorization is successful, LinkedIn will be successfully integrated with your Sendspark campaign.

## What are the benefits of LinkedIn integration in Sendspark?

- **Simplified Content Creation:** Quickly pull relevant information or media from LinkedIn into your video campaigns.
- **Enhanced Personalization:** Create tailored, professional-looking dynamic videos suitable for your audience on LinkedIn or other channels.
- **Stronger Campaign Impact:** Use LinkedIn profiles and assets to establish a connection with viewers, improving campaign messaging.

By following the above steps, you can seamlessly link your LinkedIn account and begin utilizing it to enhance your Sendspark dynamic video campaigns.
