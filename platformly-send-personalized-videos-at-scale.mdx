To send personalized videos at scale in Platformly, record your video in Sendspark, add merge tags to personalize the name, company, or job title for each recipient, then click Share Video and copy the Platformly snippet. Paste it into Platformly's HTML email builder where you want the video to appear, and send.

## Key takeaways

- The Sendspark + Platformly integration uses [merge tags](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale) to automatically swap in the right first name, company name, or job title for each recipient.
- You share the video by clicking **Share Video**, selecting **Platformly**, and copying the snippet, then pasting it into Platformly's HTML email builder.
- If you don't see Platformly on the share modal, you can [modify your shortcuts](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal).
- Whether the video plays in-line depends on the recipient's email client; clients that don't support video (like Gmail or Outlook) show a clickable fallback image instead.

## Why Send Bulk Personalized Videos in Platformly?

By using the Sendspark + Platformly integration, you can embed videos in Platformly using [merge tags](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale). These merge tags let you automatically change content for each of your recipients — so you can personalize videos with the right first name, company name, or job title for each person.

## How to Send Personalized Videos at Scale in Platformly

Watch the video or follow the steps below:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/j5euv5stuNA?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

## 1. Create your video in Sendspark

You can record, upload, or request a video.

## 2. Use merge tags in the video page or thumbnail text

These merge tags will automatically update for each recipient. If the recipient's information is unknown, they will see a [fallback](/how-merge-tag-fallbacks-work).

## 3. Click "Share Video"

![Share personalized video in Platformly](/images/6503935/7e76927c-image.png)

## 4. From the Sendspark share modal, click "Platformly"

By clicking "Platformly," you will copy your video share snippet to your clipboard.

Note, if you don't see Platformly on the share modal, you can [modify your shortcuts](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal).

![Share personalized videos in Platformly](/images/6497079/a49117a4-image.png)

## 5. Paste in an HTML snippet in Platformly

From any email in Platformly, switch to an HTML email builder.

![Platformly code section](/images/6497079/e02bb106-image.png)

Now, you can **paste your video snippet** where you want your video to go. (Note, you might want to leave yourself a note in the text, like "video goes here," so it is easy to find the spot you want to add your video in the code).

**Click the HTML button again**to see a preview of your video email.

![Preview](/images/6497079/191d9ea6-image.png)

That's it, you're ready to send your video!

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I personalize the video for each recipient?">
    Add [merge tags](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale) to the video page or thumbnail text. They automatically update for each recipient with details like first name, company name, or job title. If a recipient's information is unknown, they'll see a [fallback](/how-merge-tag-fallbacks-work).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I paste the Sendspark snippet in Platformly?">
    From any email in Platformly, switch to the HTML email builder and paste your video snippet where you want the video to appear. Click the HTML button again to preview the video email. It helps to leave yourself a note like "video goes here" so the spot is easy to find in the code.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see Platformly on the share modal?">
    If Platformly isn't listed on the Sendspark share modal, you can [modify your shortcuts](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal) to add it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will the video play inside the email?">
    It might, depending on the recipient's email client. Clients that support video (like Apple Mail, Superhuman, or Samsung) play it in-line; clients that don't (like Gmail or Outlook) show a fallback image the recipient clicks to watch in their browser. See [which email clients support video](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I embed a combined video this way?">
    No. Combined videos cannot be embedded in email through the snippet. Instead, send the video as [an image plus a link](/hubspot-embed-videos-in-emails-as-images).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
