To use a Sendspark dynamic video campaign in ReachInBox, first make sure the campaign is complete and all videos are generated. Click Share campaign, search "reach", and select Reach inbox to copy the snippet. Upload your leads CSV to ReachInBox, paste the snippet into your email, preview to confirm it renders, then launch.

## Key takeaways

- Make sure your dynamic campaign is complete and all videos have been generated before you start.
- In Sendspark, click **Share campaign**, type **"reach"** in the search bar, and select **Reach inbox**; the snippet code is copied to your clipboard automatically.
- Upload your leads CSV in ReachInBox by dragging and dropping the file. At the mapping step, mapping is **not required**, you can continue and finish the upload.
- Paste the snippet with Command + V (Mac) or Control + V (Windows), optionally resize the video element, then click **Preview Email** to confirm it renders before launching.

## 🎬 Integrating Sendspark Dynamic Videos into ReachInBox (Step-by-Step Guide)

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/x3AAMcchgFE?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

This guide walks you through how to take your already-generated dynamic video campaign from Sendspark and use it inside Reachbox to launch personalized outreach at scale.

---

## 🔧 Step 1: Retrieve the Campaign Snippet Code

Before starting, make sure your dynamic campaign is complete and all videos have been generated.

1. Open your ready campaign and click **Share campaign**.
2. In the search bar, type **“reach”**.
3. Select **Reach inbox**.
4. The snippet code is automatically copied to your clipboard.
5. A confirmation message will appear at the top of your screen.

---

## 📤 Step 2: Upload Leads to ReachInBox

1. Go to **ReachInBox**.
2. Click **Drag and Drop** to upload your CSV file of leads.
  - *(Optional: You can download the lead template from the page.)*
3. At the mapping step, mapping is **not required**. You can simply continue and finish the upload.

---

## 🧩 Step 3: Paste and Integrate the Snippet Code

1. Paste the snippet code using:
  - **Command + V** on Mac
  - **Control + V** on Windows
2. *(Optional)* Drag the video element to **resize** it if needed.

---

## 👀 Step 4: Preview Your Email

1. Click **Preview Email**.
2. Confirm that the video renders properly—this is exactly how it will appear to your recipients (e.g., on *dans.com* or *hiDan.com*).

---

## 🚀 Done!

Your dynamic Sendspark video campaign is now fully integrated into ReachInBox and ready to launch.
