Dynamic Video minutes are measured by video length multiplied by the number of leads you generate videos for, at 1 credit per personalized minute — so a 60-second video for 50 leads uses 50 credits. The full video length counts regardless of how much is AI-personalized. Minutes are a monthly allocation that resets with your billing cycle.

## Key takeaways

- Consumption equals video length × number of leads, charged at 1 credit per personalized minute (a 60-second video for 50 leads uses 50 credits).
- The full length of the video counts, regardless of how long the AI-customized portion is.
- Minutes are a monthly allocation that resets with your billing cycle; check your limit bar and renewal date on the Billing page.
- Dynamic Videos are available on the Solo plan and the Plus plan.
- Use [Combined Videos](/use-a-combined-video-in-a-dynamic-video-campaign) to extend length without spending more minutes — only the first, personalized video counts toward your allocation.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How are Dynamic Video minutes measured?">
    The number of minutes used in a campaign is the length of the video multiplied by the number of leads you create videos for, charged at 1 credit per personalized minute. For example, a 60-second video personalized for 50 leads consumes 50 credits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does only the AI-personalized portion count toward my minutes?">
    No. Regardless of the length of the AI customization, the full length of the video is counted as minutes spent. To count only the personalized portion, use the Combined Videos feature — when you combine videos, only the first video is counted, not the full length.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which plans include Dynamic Videos?">
    Dynamic Videos are included on the Solo plan and the Plus plan. Check the [Sendspark Pricing page](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) to see how many minutes each plan includes.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do Dynamic Video minutes affect my regular videos?">
    No. These minutes do not affect the number or length of regular (non-dynamic) videos, which you can create as many of, for as long as you want, on any paid subscription.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
