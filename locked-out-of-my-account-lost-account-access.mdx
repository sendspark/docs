If you're locked out of your Sendspark account — for example after an email domain change or a forgotten password — email [support@sendspark.com](mailto:support@sendspark.com) with verification details: your last payment amount and date, how long you've been a user, whether the account is active, and the name and subject of your last recorded video. The more detail you provide, the faster we can help.

Have you recently lost access to your account due to an email domain change? Perhaps you no longer have access to your inbox and have forgotten your Sendspark password.

Kindly send us an email at [support@sendspark.com](mailto:support@sendspark.com) with the following questions answered:

**-What was the amount of the last payment you made?**

**-What is the date of your last Sendspark charge?**

**-How long have you been a Sendspark user?**

**-Is your account currently active?**

**-What is the name of the video you last recorded?  
-What was the last video you recorded and what was it about?**

The more information you provide, the faster we can help you. :)  
​**​**  
If required, we will ask for more information depending on each circumstance

## Key takeaways

- To recover a locked account, email [support@sendspark.com](mailto:support@sendspark.com) with your verification details.
- Include your last payment amount, the date of your last charge, how long you've been a user, whether the account is active, and the name/subject of your last recorded video.
- The more information you provide, the faster support can help you regain access.
- Depending on the circumstance, support may ask for additional information.
