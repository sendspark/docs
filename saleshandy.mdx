Embedding a Sendspark video in Saleshandy takes about two minutes: copy the email embed code from Sendspark, then paste it into Saleshandy. Recipients whose email clients support video see it play inline; everyone else gets an automatic GIF preview that links to the full video.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark gives you an **email embed code** you add to Saleshandy — no developer required.
- Videos **play inside the email** on supported clients; unsupported clients automatically show a **clickable GIF preview**, so every recipient can watch.
- Embedding video in email can lift click-through rates by **up to 400%** (Sendspark).
- The same Sendspark steps work across every tool, so your setup stays consistent whether you use Saleshandy, HubSpot, or Mailchimp.

## Will an embedded Sendspark video play inside Saleshandy?

Yes. When the recipient's email client supports embedded video, your Sendspark video plays directly inside the email. Whether it plays depends on the recipient's email client, not on Saleshandy. See the current [list of email clients that support embedded video](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos).

## What happens if the recipient's email client doesn't support video?

Nothing breaks. Sendspark automatically generates an animated **GIF preview** of your video as a fallback. The recipient sees the preview and clicks it to open and watch the full video in their browser.

## How to add a Sendspark video to Saleshandy

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. Record or upload your video in Sendspark">Create a recording or upload an existing video from your library.</Step>
  <Step title="2. Click Share Video">Open the share options on the video you want to send.</Step>
  <Step title="3. Click Embed Video">Open the embed options.</Step>
  <Step title="4. Copy the email embed code">This is the snippet you'll paste into Saleshandy.</Step>
  <Step title="5. Open your sequence step in Saleshandy">Edit the email step in the Saleshandy sequence or cadence you want to add video to.</Step>
  <Step title="6. Add the Sendspark video">Paste the email embed code into an HTML/code block, or insert the animated thumbnail linked to your video.</Step>
  <Step title="7. Personalize at scale (optional)">Use a Sendspark Dynamic Video merge tag so each recipient gets a video addressed to them. [Learn about Dynamic Videos](/getting-started-guide-dynamic-videos).</Step>
  <Step title="8. Activate and send">Turn the Saleshandy sequence on and let it send your video to every contact.</Step>
</Steps>
