Yes — you can replace a video in Sendspark while keeping the same share link. Open the video from your library, go to its edit page, click the current video link, and select the new video. The URL stays identical, so anyone who already has the link will now see the updated content instead.

## Key takeaways

- Replacing a video keeps the original share link and landing page URL unchanged.
- This is ideal after a campaign has launched and some recipients have already watched the video.
- Open the video's edit page, click the current video link, and choose the replacement video.
- Refresh the landing page to confirm the video changed while the link stayed the same.

In this guide, I'll show you how to replace a video that has already been shared. This feature is particularly useful if you've launched a campaign, shared the link, and some recipients have already watched the video. Instead of deleting the link, you can simply update the video while keeping the same URL.  
​

**Steps to Replace a Shared Video**

1. **Prepare the New Video**
  - First, record the new video you want to use. If you already have the new video ready, you can proceed to the next step.
2. **Access the Edit Page**
  - Navigate to the edit section of the video you want to replace by simply clicking on the video listed in your library. You will see the shared link and the current video.
3. **Replace the Video**
  - On the edit page, find the link to the current video. Click on this link to search for and select the new video you want to use. ![Replace Video](/images/9565406/a267bdc8-image.png)
4. **Confirm the Replacement**
  - With a single click, the system will replace the old video with the new one. This process is quick and seamless.
5. **Verify the Update**
  - Refresh the landing page and watch the video change while the link remains the same.

Replacing a shared video is simple and efficient, ensuring your campaign link remains unchanged while updating the content. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out.
