To create a folder in Sendspark, open the library or folder where you want the new folder to live, click the **new folder icon** at the top of the page (next to **Start Recording**), give the folder a name, and click **Create**. Your new folder then appears in your dashboard.

## Key takeaways

- Folders help you organize your videos inside your Sendspark library.
- Create one by clicking the **new folder icon** at the top of the page, next to the **Start Recording** button.
- Name the folder, click **Create**, and it appears in your dashboard.
- You can [move a folder](/how-to-move-videos-between-folders) later, so its starting location isn't permanent.

Sendspark lets you create folders to organize your videos.

## How to create a folder

1. Go into the library or folder that you would like your new folder to live in. (Don't worry, you can always [move your folder](/how-to-move-videos-between-folders) later).
2. Click on the new **folder icon** at the top of the page (next to the **Start Recording** button)![Create New Folder](/images/6299776/5d56a145-image.png)
3. Write a **name** for your folder![Name Folder](/images/6299776/ad7b2d2d-image.png)
4. Click **Create**
5. That's it! You'll now see your new folder in your dashboard.![View Folder](/images/6299776/ea413b2e-image.png)
