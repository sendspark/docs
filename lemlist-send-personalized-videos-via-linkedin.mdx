To send personalized videos via LinkedIn with Lemlist, upload your CSV and map each lead's LinkedIn profile URL to the `unique ID` field, export the generated videos, add a `video_link` custom variable to your Lemlist message, then import the video CSV and launch. Send the video as a follow-up after the connection is accepted, because LinkedIn blocks previews in first messages to non-connections.

## Key takeaways

- Map the **LinkedIn Profile URL** to the `unique ID` field when uploading your CSV so each lead gets the right video.
- After generating videos, click **Export Generated Videos** to download a CSV with a `video link` column, then re-import it and map `video_link` to your custom variable.
- Insert the video with a custom variable in your message, e.g. `Hey [First Name], I created a video for you: #{{video_link}}`.
- Only send the video as a **follow-up** after a connection is accepted — LinkedIn blocks previews and images in first messages to people you're not connected with.

## 📹 How to Send Dynamic Videos via LinkedIn Messages Using Lemlist

Sending personalized videos through LinkedIn messages can significantly boost engagement. This guide walks you through the process of using **Lemlist** to send dynamic video messages effectively.

## 🔧 Prerequisites

- A **Lemlist** account
- A list of leads in **CSV format**
- **LinkedIn profiles** for each lead

---

## 🧩 Step 1: Upload and Map Your CSV

1. Go to your Lemlist campaign.
2. Click the button to **upload your CSV file**.
3. During the **mapping step**, make sure to:
  - Map the **LinkedIn Profile URL** to the `unique ID` field.
  - Optionally, set a **custom background** using the LinkedIn profile or a custom image URL.

💡 *Make sure to connect your LinkedIn account within Lemlist first so it can use LinkedIn profile backgrounds.*

---

## 🎥 Step 2: Export Generated Videos

1. After video generation, click **Export Generated Videos**.
2. A new **CSV file** will be downloaded, including a `video link` column for each contact.

---

## 💬 Step 3: Prepare Your LinkedIn Message

1. Go to the campaign editor in Lemlist.
2. Add a **custom variable**:
  - Click on **Add Variables** → **Custom Variable**.
  - Name it something like `video_link`.
  - Click **Save**.
3. Compose your message using the variable:`Hey [First Name], I created a video for you. Be sure to check it out: #{{video_link}}`

🛑 **Important:**  
If you're not already **connected** with the recipient on LinkedIn:

- Do **not** send the video in your initial connection request.
- Instead, send it as a **follow-up message** after the connection is accepted.

LinkedIn **blocks previews and images** in first messages to users you're not connected with.

---

## 📂 Step 4: Import Leads into Lemlist

1. Go to your lead list in Lemlist.
2. Click **Import** → **CSV File Import**.
3. Upload the CSV you downloaded earlier (with the `video link` column).
4. During mapping:
  - Toggle off any unnecessary fields.
  - Map `video_link` to your custom variable.
  - Map `unique ID` to the LinkedIn Profile URL.
  - Adjust other fields (e.g., map `contact name` to `first name`).

---

## 🚀 Step 5: Launch Your Campaign

- Once the mapping is complete and your variables are properly set, launch the campaign.
- Lemlist will automatically insert the video link and LinkedIn will generate a video preview when sent (as long as it's a follow-up message to a connection).

---

## ✅ Summary

By following these steps, you can:

- Personalize your outreach with video
- Ensure LinkedIn displays a video preview
- Send effective, dynamic follow-up messages
