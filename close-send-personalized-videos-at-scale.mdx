This guide walks you through how to send personalized videos at scale using Sendspark's Close integration. You'll learn how to customize your video, set up the Close integration, create email templates, and send personalized videos to your contacts.

## Prerequisites

- A Sendspark account with videos in your library
- A Close account with access to email templates and contacts
- Basic understanding of merge tags (optional - we'll link to additional resources)

## Step 1: Access Your Video Editor

1. Navigate to your Sendspark video library
2. Click on any video you want to personalize
3. This will open the edit section where you can customize your video

## Step 2: Customize Your Video

In the edit section, you can customize three key elements:

- **Header:** The title that appears above your video
- **Message:** Personal text that accompanies your video
- **Call to Action:** The button or link you want viewers to click

**Note:** For detailed information on how merge tags work in these sections, refer to our merge tags documentation.

## Step 3: Set Up Close Integration

1. Click **Share Video**
2. Look for the **Close** option in the sharing menu
3. If you don't see Close listed:
  - Click **Edit** or **Add**
  - Type "Close" in the search field
  - You should see a checkmark appear next to Close
  - Click **Save Changes**
4. The Close option will now appear in your sharing menu

## Step 4: Customize Your Video Banner

1. Click **Customize** to access banner settings
2. Add merge tags to personalize the banner for each recipient
3. You can use any merge tag that Close supports

## Step 5: Copy Your Video Code

1. Select the Close integration option
2. Click to copy the Close code
3. Keep this code ready for the next step

## Step 6: Create an Email Template in Close

1. In your Close account, navigate to **Settings**
2. Select **Template** option
3. Click **New Template**
4. Paste your copied Sendspark code using Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on Mac)
5. Add your subject line and template name
6. Save your template

**Tip:** Creating templates makes it much easier to send personalized videos at scale. You can reuse these templates for multiple campaigns.

## Step 7: Send Your Personalized Video

1. Go to **Contacts** in Close
2. Select the contact you want to email
3. Click **Email**
4. Choose **Template** and select the template you just created
5. Send the email

## Step 8: Verify Your Email

Once sent, check how your email appears to recipients:

- The personalized banner should display correctly
- Merge tags should be replaced with actual contact information
- The video should be embedded and clickable

## Important Tips

**Tip:** Always include a space after merge tags in your customization fields. Without proper spacing, merge tags and surrounding text will run together, creating a poor user experience.

**Tip:** Test your email template by sending it to yourself first to ensure all merge tags are working correctly and the formatting looks professional.
