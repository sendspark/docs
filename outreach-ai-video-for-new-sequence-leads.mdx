You can automatically create and send personalized AI videos to new Outreach.io sequence leads with Sendspark. Create a Dynamic Video Campaign, paste the Dynamic Video Snippet into your Outreach email template, then build an Agentic Workflow that triggers a video whenever a prospect is enrolled in the sequence. This requires Outreach Admin access and the Agentic Workflow feature enabled in Sendspark.

## Key takeaways

- Two prerequisites: Outreach Admin access (to connect the Sendspark Outreach App) and the Agentic Workflow feature enabled in Sendspark.
- Paste the Sendspark "Dynamic Video Snippet" into your Outreach email template exactly where you want the video to appear; you must generate at least one video first to reach the Share Campaign page.
- The Agentic Workflow uses the "Prospect Enrolled in Sequence" trigger from Outreach and a "Create Dynamic Video" action in Sendspark.
- Always map the "email" field, as it is essential for the integration to work properly.
- Leave a brief time delay between enrolling contacts and the email sending so the video has time to generate.

This guide walks through how to automatically create and send personalized videos in Outreach.io sequences using AI.

To set up this automation, you need...

- To have **Outreach Admin access** (to connect the Sendspark Outreach App)
- To have the **Agentic Workflow** feature enabled in Sendspark.

## Step 1: Create Your Video & Email Campaigns

1. Start by [creating your Dynamic Video Campaign](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions) in Sendspark
2. Next, Create your email sequence in Outreach

## Step 2: Add Video Placeholders in Outreach Sequence

Copy and paste the Sendspark "Dynamic Video Snippet" from Sendspark into your Outreach email template, exactly where you want the video to appear in your email.

- In Sendspark, click **"Share Campaign"**(you need to generate at least one video from your dynamic video campaign to get to this page)![Dynamic video campaign page with the Share Campaign button](/images/10705958/47acb1c3-image.png)
- Search for "**Outreach"**in the modal![Share Dynamic Video modal searching for the Outreach option](/images/10705958/ad673253-image.png)
- **Click on "Outreach"** and **paste** directly into your email template ​![Outreach email template editor with Sendspark video snippet pasted in](/images/10705958/d3ca310e-image.png)

Note: we recommend you leave a brief time delay from the time you enroll contacts in this sequence to the time that this email will actually send to give time for the video to generate.

## Step 3: Create Agentic Workflow

Now the fun part! Let's set up an automation so that when prospects get added to the Outreach sequence, AI videos automatically get created!

1. Go to [Sendspark Agentic Workflows](https://sendspark.com/automated-workflows)
2. Create a New Automation
  1. As the Trigger, select **"Outreach"**and then **"Prospect Enrolled in Sequence"** ​![Workflow trigger set to Outreach Prospect Enrolled in a Sequence](/images/10705958/469db8a0-image.png)
  2. A**dd a condition** that the task has a note that contains your dynamic video campaign ​![Condition set to sequence equals the Outreach Tutorial video campaign](/images/10705958/35b810fa-image.png)
  3. Add an action for Sendspark
    1. Select **"Create Dynamic Video"** ​![Workflow action step set to Sendspark Create dynamic video](/images/10705958/bf9857a6-image.png)
    2. Select the **Dynamic Video Campaign**you want to use from Sendspark, and **map in the fields from Outreach**, so you can use them in your videos ​![Map your fields panel mapping Outreach prospect data into the video campaign](/images/10705958/765bc4da-image.png)
    3. **Make sure to map "email,"** as this is essential for the integration to work properly
    4. Save and close!

So, this is how your whole Sendspark automation should look:

![Completed automation with Outreach trigger and Sendspark video action steps](/images/10705958/fd75abad-image.png)

That's it! Hit **"Start"** to start your automation.

You can now add prospects to your sequences in Outreach, and you'll see dynamic videos start getting created for each person in Sendspark.You can now add prospects to your sequences in Outreach, and you'll see dynamic videos start getting created for each person in Sendspark.
