## ⭐ Why Use This Workflow

Customers often show intent to upgrade but never finalize the steps. With this workflow, you can:

- 🏷️ Identify upgrade-ready customers via Intercom tags.
- 🎥 Automatically generate a personalized video in Sendspark.
- ✉️ Deliver the video through HubSpot emails.

This automation saves time, scales your outreach, and increases conversion rates:

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Setup

## Step 1: 🔗 Configure a Webhook in Intercom

1. Go to **⚙️ Settings &gt; Developers &gt; Webhooks** in Intercom.
2. ➕ Create a new webhook that triggers **when a tag is added**.
3. Paste the **webhook URL** from your automation platform (Albato, Zapier, or Make).
4. Ensure the webhook passes customer info (🧑 Name, ✉️ Email, 🏢 Company).

![Intercom Settings page showing Developers menu and Webhooks configuration option](/images/12059896/8b30fbbe-image.png)

---

## Step 2: 🏷️ Tag Customers in Intercom

1. Log in to your **Intercom dashboard**.
2. Open the conversation with a customer who shows interest in upgrading.
3. From the **tag menu**, select or create a tag (e.g., `Upgrade_Interested`).
4. ✅ Apply the tag to the conversation.

![Intercom conversation thread with tag menu open and Upgrade_Interested tag applied](/images/12059896/3dc1d077-image.png)

---

## Step 3: 🎥 Automate Video Creation in Sendspark

1. Connect the Intercom webhook to **Sendspark** in your automation platform.
2. 🔑 Map the fields so Sendspark knows which data to personalize (e.g., customer first name).
3. 📝 Create a Sendspark video template with placeholders (like *Hi #####&#123;&#123;Name&#125;&#125;*).
4. ▶️ Test the setup to confirm a video generates whenever the webhook fires.

![Sendspark template editor displaying personalized video placeholder with customer name field](/images/12059896/d378bd11-image.png)

## 📊 Example Flow in Action

1. 🧑 Customer asks about upgrading.
2. 🏷️ Agent applies the **Upgrade_Interested** tag in Intercom.
3. 🔗 Intercom webhook fires and sends customer data.
4. 🎥 Sendspark generates a personalized video.
5. 📧 HubSpot sends an automated email with the video link.

## 💡 Best Practices

- Use **clear, standardized tags** so agents know exactly when to trigger the workflow.
- 🧪 **Test each step** with a dummy customer before going live.
- 🎯 Keep your video script short, focused, and upgrade-specific.
- 📈 Track HubSpot analytics to measure open rates and conversions.
