Embed your [LemCal](https://www.lemcal.com/) calendar next to your Sendspark videos to help prospects book meetings right after watching.

To connect it, open your meeting type in LemCal, go to the **Embed** section, and copy the two values shown there. In Sendspark, select **LemCal** as the calendar provider, paste the first value (your user ID) and the second value (the meeting), then save. Your calendar appears next to the video.

## Key takeaways

- LemCal's **Embed** tab (inside your meeting type) provides the two values Sendspark needs.
- Set **LemCal** as the provider in Sendspark, then paste the **user ID** and the **meeting** value into their fields.
- When copying each value, do not include the surrounding quotation marks.
- After saving, the LemCal calendar appears beside your video so prospects can book without leaving the page.

## How to embed a LemCal calendar with your video

1. In LemCal, go to **Meetings**, then click the meeting type you created.
2. Open the **Embed** section. You'll see two pieces of data that Sendspark needs.
3. Copy the first value — your **user ID** — making sure not to include the quotation marks.
4. In Sendspark, select **LemCal** as the provider and paste the user ID into its field.
5. Copy the second value (the **meeting**) the same way and paste it into its field.
6. Click **Save**. Your LemCal calendar now appears next to your video.
