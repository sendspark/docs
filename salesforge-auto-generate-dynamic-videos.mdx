title: "Salesforge: Auto-Generate Dynamic Videos"

---

The Sendspark SalesForge integration makes it easy to create and send Dynamic Videos in just a few clicks.

If you haven't connected your account, then please refer to this article first.

**[How to Connect Sendspark and Salesforge](/how-to-connect-sendspark-and-salesforge)**

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/wJNJWv44U38?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

When you create a sequence and you get to the step called "Builder", you will be able to see the Sendspark button at the bottom of the page.

![Salesforge sequence builder page with Sendspark button at bottom](/images/12930662/f7080057-image.png)

Once you click there, you'll be able to select your dynamic video campaign by name:

![Dropdown menu displaying list of dynamic video campaigns by name](/images/12930662/68443504-image.png)

Once you click on add video, you'll get a confirmation that the video has been added and you'll also see a variable automatically applied : #######&#123;&#123;sendspark_video&#125;&#125;

![Confirmation message showing video added with sendspark_video variable](/images/12930662/b13d6d37-image.png)

Once you click on continue, you'll see that this warning message will come up

![Warning message displayed after clicking continue in sequence builder](/images/12930662/a47cfbb3-image.png)

And the moment you click on launch sequence, you'll notice that the videos will start to generate in your Sendspark Dynamic Video Campaign:

![Sendspark dynamic video campaign showing videos in generation process](/images/12930662/15f5d726-image.png)
