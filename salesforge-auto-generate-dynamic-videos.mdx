The Sendspark Salesforge integration lets you generate and send Dynamic Videos in a few clicks. Connect your accounts first, then in your sequence's Builder step click the Sendspark button, select your dynamic video campaign by name, and add the video. Salesforge applies a sendspark_video variable automatically, and launching the sequence starts generating the videos in your Sendspark campaign.

## Key takeaways

- **Connect your accounts first** (see How to Connect Sendspark and Salesforge).
- In the sequence **Builder** step, click the **Sendspark button** and select your dynamic video campaign by name.
- Adding the video **automatically applies a sendspark_video variable**.
- **Launching the sequence** triggers video generation in your Sendspark Dynamic Video Campaign.

The Sendspark SalesForge integration makes it easy to create and send Dynamic Videos in just a few clicks.

If you haven't connected your account, then please refer to this article first.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to connect my accounts first?">
    Yes. Connect your Sendspark and Salesforge accounts before generating videos. See [How to Connect Sendspark and Salesforge](/how-to-connect-sendspark-and-salesforge).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where is the Sendspark button in Salesforge?">
    When you create a sequence and reach the step called "Builder," the Sendspark button appears at the bottom of the page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the sendspark_video variable?">
    It's a variable Salesforge automatically applies when you add your dynamic video, representing the video that will be inserted for each recipient in the sequence.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When do the videos actually generate?">
    The videos start generating in your Sendspark Dynamic Video Campaign the moment you click launch sequence.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
