Are you suddenly changing your email domain or leaving behind your old email to adopt a new one?

These short steps will guide you to easily change your email in Sendspark as well:

**Start by clicking on your menu and heading to My Account:**

![Sendspark menu dropdown with My Account option highlighted](/images/9590392/c6c70298-image.png)

**You'll then jump straight into your profile:**

![Sendspark user profile page displaying account settings](/images/9590392/67118315-image.png)

**Under Account Information, you'll be able to set your new credentials**

![Account Information section with email and password input fields](/images/9590392/752464bb-2.gif)

**Hit on UPDATE to finish the process**

You are all set! You can now use these credentials to log in to your account.

If you have any issues through this process, kindly send an email to: [support@sendspark.com](mailto:support@sendspark.com)
