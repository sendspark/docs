To change your email address and password in Sendspark, click your menu and go to My Account. This opens your profile, where you can set new credentials under the Account Information section. Hit UPDATE to finish, then use your new credentials to log in. If you run into any issues, email support@sendspark.com.

## Key takeaways

- Open the menu and head to My Account to reach your profile.
- Set your new email and password under the Account Information section.
- Hit UPDATE to save your changes, then log in with the new credentials.
- If you have any issues, email support@sendspark.com.
