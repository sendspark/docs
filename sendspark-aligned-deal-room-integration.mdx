You can easily embed Sendspark videos in your deal room with the Sendspark Aligned integration.

## How to Add Sendspark Videos to Aligned Deal Room

1. Go to your deal room in [Aligned](https://alignedup.com/)
2. Click on the plus icon to add new content into your Aligned room ​![Aligned deal room interface with plus icon to add new content](/images/12007211/6ed3b6ab-image.png)
3. Search for "Sendspark" and select to add it into your room ​![Aligned search results showing Sendspark integration option](/images/12007211/8bb322e5-image.png)
4. Paste the link to your Sendspark video, and give your video a name so you can find it later in your Aligned library ​![Aligned form to paste Sendspark video link and enter video name](/images/12007211/0088f3e5-image.png)
5. You'll now see your Sendspark video embedded on your Aligned page. Viewers click to play it right there from the page! ​![Aligned deal room page displaying embedded Sendspark video player](/images/12007211/ee6199b5-image.png)
