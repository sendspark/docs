## 📩 Get Notified When Someone Watches Your Video

## Overview

Learn how to set up an automation to receive a notification whenever someone watches your video from a dynamic video campaign.

## ⚙️ Step-by-Step Setup

## 1. Go to Agentic Workflows

- Navigate to **Agentic Workflows** in your account
- Click **Create New Automation**

---

## 2. Set the Trigger

- Select **Sendspark** as the trigger app
- Choose the event: 👉 **New Video Watched**

You’ll see two options:

- From a specific campaign
- From any campaign

👉 Select **specific campaign** (recommended for better control)

---

## 3. Choose the Campaign

- Select your account
- Click **Update List**
- Choose the dynamic video campaign you want
- Click **Save**

---

## 4. Add an Action

- Click **Add Action**

You can choose different apps like:

- Slack
- Email
- Others

👉 In this example, select **Send Email**

---

## 5. Configure the Email Notification

- **To:** Your email address
- **From:** Your connected account
- **Format:** Text (simple and effective)

## Email Body Example:

```
The video for #{{First Name}} (#{{Contact Email}}) has been played.Campaign: [Your Campaign Name]
```

👉 Use dynamic fields like:

- First Name
- Contact Email

---

## 6. Save & Activate

- Click **Save**
- Turn the automation **ON**

---

## ✅ Result

You’ll receive a notification every time someone watches your video from the selected campaign.

---

## 💡 Tips

- You can connect other apps (like Slack) for real-time alerts
- Use different triggers for other events (e.g., video created)
- Customize the message with dynamic variables for more context
