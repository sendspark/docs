title: "Analytics Update — Percentage Watched"
description: "Learn how to use the new percentage watched analytics feature to track video viewer engagement and understand viewing patterns."
---

On February 2nd, 2022 we released a "% watched" feature to let you see the portion of your video viewers have watched.

The bad news is we don't have "% watched" data from before February 2nd, 2022, so **the** **"% watched" column will be empty for those sessions**.

![Percentage Video Viewed](/images/5949152/c73ef21d-image.png)

**👆 This does NOT mean that viewers did not see your entire video — just that we did not capture the data for this session.**

The good news is that going forward you'll be able to see "% watched" data for all viewer sessions!
