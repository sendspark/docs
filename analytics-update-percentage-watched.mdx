Sendspark released the "% watched" feature on February 2nd, 2022, which shows the portion of your video that each viewer watched. Because the data wasn't captured before that date, the "% watched" column stays empty for older sessions. An empty value doesn't mean viewers skipped your video — only that the data wasn't recorded for that session.

## Key takeaways

- The "% watched" feature launched February 2nd, 2022 and shows how much of a video each viewer watched.
- Sessions from before that date have an empty "% watched" column because the data wasn't captured.
- An empty value doesn't mean viewers didn't watch — just that it wasn't recorded for that session.
- Going forward, "% watched" data is available for all viewer sessions.

On February 2nd, 2022 we released a "% watched" feature to let you see the portion of your video viewers have watched.

The bad news is we don't have "% watched" data from before February 2nd, 2022, so **the** **"% watched" column will be empty for those sessions**.
