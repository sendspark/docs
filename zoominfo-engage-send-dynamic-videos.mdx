To send dynamic personalized videos in ZoomInfo Engage, create a Dynamic Video campaign in Sendspark, import your contacts, then click Share Campaign and select Engage to copy a snippet you paste into a ZoomInfo Engage email template. Each prospect on your Sendspark list receives an email with the personalized video generated for them.

## Key takeaways

- Record one video in Sendspark, then use AI to generate a personalized version for each prospect and send them all through ZoomInfo Engage sequences.
- For AI voice cloning, record your video starting with the phrase **"Hi Watermelon."** — Sendspark replaces "Watermelon" with each viewer's first name.
- Get the snippet from **Share Campaign → Engage**, then paste it into a ZoomInfo Engage email template; a generic preview shows until you preview as a contact from your Sendspark list.
- To be sure you only email people who have a generated video, export the Dynamic Video Contact List as a CSV in Sendspark.

## 1. Create Your Dynamic Video Campaign

Go to the [dynamic video section](https://sendspark.com/dynamic-videos) of Sendspark and click **"Create Dynamic Video"** to create a new campaign.

The first step is to **add your video.**

If you are using AI voice cloning, you need to record your video with the starter phrase **"Hi Watermelon."** (Then, we will replace "Watermelon" with your viewer's name).

Here's an example of a video that would work well:

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/BUOlt3YOVf8?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

After adding your video, you have some options:

![Dynamic video options panel with Simulate Scrolling, Cursor Overlay, and Voice Cloning toggles](/images/9146071/4a84587a-image.png)

- **Simulate Scrolling:**Makes it look like you are recording over your viewer's website, and scrolling through the page
- **Cursor Overlay:** Adds a cursor, to make the website scroll look more convincing
- **Voice Cloning:** Ensures that we replace the word "watermelon" with your viewer's first name

You can also customize the video landing pages to make sure that viewers take the next step in your sales process after watching:

![Video landing page customization settings for viewer actions after watching](/images/9146071/d34154e3-image.png)

## 2. Import Contacts

You can create dynamic videos for contacts in a few ways:

1. Manually type contact data
2. Import a CSV
3. Generate videos via automation (Zapier, API, etc)

![Contact import interface with options for manual entry, CSV upload, and automation](/images/9146071/d2ca0fbb-image.png)

Once dynamic videos have been created, you'll see them populate, and can play them to test out the AI and make sure everything looks great.

![Dynamic video list showing generated personalized videos ready for testing and playback](/images/9146071/6fd00ef2-image.png)

## 3. Share in ZoomInfo Engage

To share videos in ZoomInfo Engage, simply click **"Share Campaign"**

Then, select **"Engage"**

![Share Campaign dialog with Engage option selected for ZoomInfo integration](/images/9146071/3e9aa351-image.png)

Now, you can take that snippet and**paste it in an email template in ZoomInfo Engage.**

When you first paste the video into your email template, you will see a generic video preview.

However, if you preview this video as a contact who was on your Sendspark list, then you should see the video that has been generated for them.

We don't have a ZoomInfo account we can use to demo, but this is how it would look in Apollo, where we have a similar integration:

![Email preview showing personalized dynamic video embedded in Apollo email template](/images/9146071/1a388bc2-image.png)

Now, you should be able to turn on your sequence and ensure that every person who was part of your Sendspark list receives an email with the video made for them.

Note: if you want to be absolutely positive that you are only sending video emails to people whom you've made videos for, you can export the Dynamic Video Contact List as a CSV in Sendspark.

![Export Dynamic Video Contact List as CSV button in Sendspark campaign interface](/images/9146071/be035642-image.png)

## 4. Track Views & Engagement

Once people start to watch your video, you'll be able to see who's watching, what percentage they've watched, and what other they've taken in your video analytics.

Just click **"See Engagement"** at the bottom of your Dynamic Video Builder to view the analytics modal.

![Engagement analytics modal displaying video view rates, watch percentages, and viewer actions](/images/9146071/abd3bad3-image.png)
