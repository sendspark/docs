This guide shows you how to create dynamically personalized videos in Sendspark and integrate them with Apollo emails. With dynamic personalization, you can record one video that automatically adapts to show each recipient's name and company information.

## Prerequisites

- A Sendspark account
- An Apollo account with email templates set up
- A recorded or uploaded video in Sendspark

## Step 1: Set Up Dynamic Personalization in Sendspark

1. Navigate to your video's share page in Sendspark
2. Click **Add Dynamic Personalization**
3. Configure your personalized elements:
  - **Header:** Add "Hi ####&#123;&#123;first_name&#125;&#125;" or similar greeting
  - **Message area:** Include company-specific text like "wanted to show how ####&#123;&#123;company_name&#125;&#125;"
  - **Call to action:** Add your desired CTA text and link

**Tip:** The dynamic fields will automatically populate with each contact's information when they view the video.

## Step 2: Customize Your Video Thumbnail

1. Click **Share Video** from your video page
2. Click **Customize** over the video thumbnail
3. Add your personalized text (like "Hi ####&#123;&#123;first_name&#125;&#125;") to appear on the thumbnail
4. Review how the thumbnail will appear to recipients

## Step 3: Add Video to Apollo Email Template

1. In Sendspark, click the **Apollo snippet** button to copy the video code to your clipboard
2. Open your Apollo email template
3. Paste the video snippet where you want the video to appear
4. Format the placement as needed (you can add spacing or remove the "watch video" text if desired)

## Step 4: Test Your Dynamic Video

1. Preview your Apollo email template with different contacts
2. Verify that names and company information populate correctly
3. Check that the video thumbnail displays the personalized greeting

**Note:** Each recipient will see their own personalized version of the video, including their name in both the thumbnail and video content, creating a more personal connection at scale.
