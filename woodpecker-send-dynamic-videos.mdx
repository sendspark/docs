Sendspark makes it easy to send AI personalized videos in Woodpecker, so each one of your prospects receives a video that says "Hi Name" and shows you recording over their website. Create one dynamic video in Sendspark, turn on voice cloning, import your contacts, then copy the Woodpecker share snippet and paste it into your Woodpecker email.

## Key takeaways

- You record one dynamic video and Sendspark personalizes it for every contact, saying each prospect's name over their own website.
- To personalize the voice, record yourself saying "Hi watermelon" at the start. The word "watermelon" is replaced with each prospect's first name, and you toggle on **voice cloning** to enable this.
- After importing contacts (CSV upload or a few typed in for testing), you click **Share Campaign**, select **Woodpecker**, and copy the share snippet to your clipboard.
- When you first paste the snippet in Woodpecker you'll see only the plain video of yourself with no background. That's the fallback, and it's normal. Preview with real contacts to see each personalized version.

## How to Send AI Personalized Videos in Woodpecker

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/J12kE1p4-sI?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. In Sendspark, create your **Dynamic Video** and select the video you want to use: the camera bubble over the website. This should be a video of you talking as if you're recording over the prospect's website.
2. To optimize the video for AI, record yourself saying **"Hi watermelon"** at the beginning. The "watermelon" variable is replaced with each prospect's first name. Toggle on **voice cloning** to tell Sendspark you've done this.
3. Set up the video page (the page people open when they click the video in the email). Add your logo and branding so viewers can watch, schedule a demo, or take the next step in your process.
4. Import your contacts: upload a **CSV**, or type in a few to test. Sendspark generates the videos, and you can click to play each one.
5. Click **Share Campaign** and select the option for **Woodpecker**, then click the button to copy the share snippet to your clipboard.
6. In Woodpecker, open the email where you want the video, delete any placeholder that's there, and paste the snippet in.
7. Note that on first paste you'll see only the plain video of yourself with no background. That's expected because no contact is selected yet, and it's also the fallback. When you preview with real contacts, you'll see the personalized version with you over each contact's website.
