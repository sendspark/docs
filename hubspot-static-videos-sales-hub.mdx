Sendspark's HubSpot Sales Hub integration lets you send hyper-personalized video emails at scale. Create one video in Sendspark using personalization tokens, share it as a HubSpot Sales Hub snippet, and paste it into a HubSpot template. From there you can use the template in direct emails, Gmail/Outlook, and sequences, and the tokens populate for each viewer automatically.

## Key takeaways

- Adding personalization can increase email engagement by up to 16x, and this integration lets you personalize videos at scale.
- Record one video in Sendspark with personalization tokens like first name, job title, and company name; they populate with each viewer's information when sent through HubSpot.
- If a contact's information is unknown, Sendspark displays [fallback variables](/how-merge-tag-fallbacks-work) so the message still looks complete.
- Use **Share with Personalization → HubSpot Sales Hub** to copy the snippet, paste it into a new HubSpot template, and save. The template then works from direct emails, Gmail/Outlook (with the HubSpot Chrome Extension), and sequences.

With [Sendspark's + Hubspot Sales Hub Integration,](https://www.sendspark.com/integrations/hubspot) you are able to send hyper-personalized video emails in Hubspot.

## How to Send Personalized Videos at Scale in Hubspot Sales Hub

## 1. Create your video in Sendspark

When you create your video message in Sendspark, you can use personalization tokens, like ###&#123;&#123;first name&#125;&#125;, ###&#123;&#123;job title&#125;&#125;, and ###&#123;&#123;company name&#125;&#125;.

![Video with Personalization Tokens](/images/6457173/7c0def7c-image.png)

When you send your videos in Hubspot, these variables will automatically populate with the correct viewer's information.

If the contact information is unknown, Sendspark will display [fallback variables](/how-merge-tag-fallbacks-work).

## 2. Add to a Hubspot template

Under **"Share with Personalization"**click**Hubspot Sales Hub**.

Note, if you don't see Hubspot Sales Hub on the share modal, you can [follow these steps to modify your shortcuts](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal).

![Hubspot Share option](/images/6337900/52e22ec9-image.png)

This will copy the Hubspot video snippet to your clipboard. Now, you can go over to Hubspot and **create a new template**.

**Paste** your Hubspot video snippet right into your template.

![Personalized Video in Hubspot Template](/images/6337900/1a1bef23-image.png)

**Save** your new template!

## 3. Share your video email

Now, you can use your personalized video template from anywhere in Hubspot Sales Hub, including:

- Direct emails to customers in Hubspot
- Direct emails in Gmail / Outlook, as long as you have the Hubspot Chrome Extension installed
- Sequences

When you select your video template, the personalization tokens for your viewers will populate, so you can send one video to many people, and still deliver a personalized experience.
