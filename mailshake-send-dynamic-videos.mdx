To send dynamic videos in Mailshake, copy the Mailshake code snippet from **Share Campaign** in Sendspark, download the campaign CSV, then start a Mailshake campaign, upload those prospects, and paste the snippet into a Mailshake email template. Send to the same contacts you generated videos for so each person gets their own video.

## Key takeaways

- Copy the **Mailshake code snippet** from **Share Campaign** in Sendspark.
- Click **Download CSV** so you send to the same contacts you generated videos for.
- Paste the snippet into a Mailshake email **Template**, save it, then confirm and launch the campaign.
- It's important to send in Mailshake to the same list of contacts you generated videos for in Sendspark.

In this tutorial, we'll show you how to send dynamic videos in Mailshake using the Sendspark + Mailshake integration.

Sendspark's Dynamic Video feature lets you take one video of yourself and instantly put that video over any background, so it looks like you're recording over any website or URL.

Use this to supercharge your sales outreach, product demos, onboarding, customer success, and more!

## How to share a Dynamic Videos Campaign Step by step:

1. Click on Share Campaign:

![Sendspark campaign dashboard with Share Campaign button highlighted](/images/8511040/9152a589-image.png)

2. Look for Mailshake, and click on it to copy the code.

![Sendspark share options menu showing Mailshake integration option](/images/8511040/16443023-image.png)

3. To make sure that you're using the same contacts, click the "Download CSV" button.

![Sendspark campaign interface with Download CSV button visible](/images/8511040/7fb48639-image.png)

4. Go to Mailshake and start a Campaign & upload your prospects to your Campaign.   
​

![Mailshake campaign creation screen with prospect upload section](/images/8511040/295b7d13-image.png)

5. Create an email Template and paste the code "CMD or CTRL + V"

![Mailshake email template editor with paste area for video code](/images/8511040/114a6346-image.png)

6. Save the Template and proceed.

![Mailshake template save confirmation dialog and launch options](/images/8511040/201e9746-image.png)

7. Confirm and launch the campaign

8. Note: it's important that you send the video in Mailshake to the same list of contacts you generated videos for in Sendspark.
