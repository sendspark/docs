Set up an automation that emails prospects who watched your video but haven't replied, using Sendspark and Instantly. Create an automation in Sendspark triggered by Video Played, search the lead in Instantly, use a Router to split repliers from non-repliers, then enroll non-repliers into a casual follow-up campaign in Instantly.

## Key takeaways

- Trigger on **Sendspark: New Video Played - Specific Campaign** to catch prospects who watched a chosen video or campaign.
- Use an Instantly **Search Lead** action with the contact email from the trigger step, then a **Router tool** to separate leads who replied from those who didn't.
- Enroll non-repliers into a follow-up campaign with **Add Lead to Campaign** in Instantly.
- A short, casual follow-up works best, for example: "Hey [First Name], I noticed you watched the video I sent. I'd love to hear your thoughts."

## Step 1: Create an Automation in Sendspark

1. Log into your [Sendspark dashboard](https://sendspark.com).
2. Go to **Agentic Workflows** in the menu.
3. Click **New Automation** and name it something like *Video Viewed — No Reply*.

---

## Step 2: Set the Trigger to “Video Played”

1. In the automation builder, choose **Trigger → Sendspark: New Video Played - Specific Campaign**
2. Select the video or campaign you want to track.

![Sendspark automation trigger settings with Video Played campaign selection option](/images/11997015/f03dfed5-image.png)

---

## Step 3: Check for Replies in Instantly

1. Add an **Action** to **Search Lead** in **Instantly** ​![Instantly Search Lead action panel in Sendspark automation builder workflow](/images/11997015/37f0a00c-image.png)
2. Use the contact email from your first step ​![Automation variable mapping showing contact email field from previous step](/images/11997015/e32b5112-image.png)
3. Then add a **Router tool** to distinguish between leads who have replied or not ​![Router tool configuration distinguishing between replied and non-replied leads](/images/11997015/773d72d5-image.png)

---

## Step 4: Add to a Follow-Up Campaign in Instantly

1. If no reply is found, enroll the contact into a follow-up sequence in Instantly. ​![Instantly Add Lead to Campaign action panel for follow-up sequence enrollment](/images/11997015/8238290d-image.png)
2. Add an additional Action to Add Lead to Campaign in Instantly
3. In that campaign, you can have a a short, casual follow-up email such as: *“Hey [First Name], I noticed you watched the video I sent. I’d love to hear your thoughts.”*

## Step 5: Start Automation

1. When you're ready, hit the Start button to turn on your automation.
2. Your whole automation should look something like this:
