The Sendspark + AWeber integration lets you send personalized videos at scale and can increase engagement by up to 300%. Record or upload your video, customize the landing page and thumbnail with personalization tokens, then click **Share**, select **AWeber**, and paste the snippet into an HTML block in your AWeber email editor. Preview, test, and send.

## Key takeaways

- Video emails can increase engagement by up to 300%, and the AWeber integration personalizes videos at scale.
- Use AWeber contact variables (such as first name) to personalize the video experience.
- Customize the landing page and thumbnail, and use personalization tokens to auto-insert your viewer's information.
- Share → select AWeber → paste into an **HTML snippet** in the AWeber email editor, then preview, test, and send.

## Why Send Mass Personalized Videos in AWeber?

Video emails can increase engagement up to 300%. The Sendspark + AWeber Integration goes above and beyond to let you **personalize videos at scale.**

When sharing a Sendspark video in AWeber, you can use contact variables from AWeber (first name, etc), to personalize the video experience.

This is going to help you drive even more engagement, and build better relationships with your audience.

## How to Send Personalized Videos in AWeber

Watch the video or read the steps below!

1. [Record](/record-yourself) or [upload](/upload-a-video-to-sendspark) your video to Sendspark

3. Click**"share"**
4. Select **"AWeber"**
5. Paste into an **HTML snippet** in your AWeber email editor
6. Preview and test
7. Send!

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I personalize videos in AWeber?">
    You can use AWeber contact variables (such as first name) together with Sendspark [personalization tokens](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale), which automatically add each viewer's information to the video experience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I paste the Sendspark snippet in AWeber?">
    After clicking Share and selecting AWeber, paste the snippet into an HTML snippet block in your AWeber email editor.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I record the video in Sendspark or AWeber?">
    You [record](/record-yourself) or [upload](/upload-a-video-to-sendspark) your video in Sendspark first, then share it into AWeber using the Share → AWeber option.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Should I test before sending?">
    Yes. Preview and test your email before sending so you can confirm the video thumbnail and personalization render correctly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
