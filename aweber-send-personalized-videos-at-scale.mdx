## Why Send Mass Personalized Videos in AWeber?

Video emails can increase engagement up to 300%. The Sendspark + AWeber Integration goes above and beyond to let you **personalize videos at scale.**

When sharing a Sendspark video in AWeber, you can use contact variables from AWeber (first name, etc), to personalize the video experience.

This is going to help you drive even more engagement, and build better relationships with your audience.

## How to Send Personalized Videos in AWeber

Watch the video or read the steps below!

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/jtVKI8b1a_I?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. [Record](/record-yourself) or [upload](/upload-a-video-to-sendspark) your video to Sendspark
2. Customize the [video landing page](/customize-your-video-landing-page) and [video thumbnail](/how-to-edit-video-thumbnails) (you can use [personalization tokens](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale) to automatically add your viewer's information to the video)
3. Click**"share"**
4. Select **"AWeber"**
5. Paste into an **HTML snippet** in your AWeber email editor
6. Preview and test
7. Send!
