To embed your YouCanBookMe calendar on a Sendspark video, open your video page, toggle the **Calendar** option **On**, select **YouCanBookMe** from the bottom of the provider dropdown, and add the link to your calendar. It appears in the preview on the right, so right after watching your video, viewers can book a meeting on your calendar.

## Key takeaways

- Embedding your calendar takes three steps: toggle **Calendar** on, choose **YouCanBookMe**, and paste your calendar link.
- YouCanBookMe sits at the bottom of the provider dropdown list.
- Add the **link** to your calendar. Sendspark builds the embed for you, so no embed code is needed.
- The calendar appears in the preview on the right, letting viewers book a meeting right after watching.

Easily embed your Youcanbook.me calendar next to your Sendspark videos to book more meetings with video prospecting.

## **How to Embed Youcanbookme Calendars Link Next To Videos**

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/szYQc2XKYCc?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Open your Sendspark video page.
2. Toggle **"Calendar"** to **"On"**.
3. Select **"Youcanbookme"** from the provider dropdown list (it's near the bottom of the list).
4. **Add the link** to the Youcanbookme calendar you want to use.

![Embed Youcanbookme Calendar with Sendspark video](/images/9052083/c7d3e6a8-image.png)

Once you add the link, your embedded calendar appears in the preview area on the right. Right after watching your video, viewers can book a meeting on your calendar.
