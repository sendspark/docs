title: "How to Pin a Chrome Extension"
description: "Learn how to pin Chrome extensions to your browser toolbar for easy access and keep your favorite extensions visible at all times."
noindex: true
---

You can pin Chrome Extensions to keep them visible at all times on your Chrome browser. This makes it easy to see and use them in the future.

To pin a Chrome Extension, **click the puzzle piece** on the top right of your browser.

![Click Chrome Extention](/images/4292727/44df6cce-image.png)

This will open a drop-down of all your Chrome Extensions. From here, simply **click the little pin** next to the Chrome Extensions you want to pin to your browser.

![Pin Chrome Extensions](/images/4292727/c7c2ea13-image.png)

Now, your pinned icons will show up at the top of your Chrome browser, next to the puzzle piece. 🎊

![Pinned Chrome Extensions](/images/4292727/b6bfa097-image.png)
