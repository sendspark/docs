Touch-Up Appearance applies real-time touch-up effects to your video feed so you look your best on camera. To use it, start a recording from the Chrome extension, open **Effects** and turn **Camera Effects** on, then click **Appearance** and drag the slider to your preferred setting. You can turn it off anytime if it slows your computer.

## Key takeaways

- Touch-Up Appearance applies **real-time** effects to your video feed, useful for sales pitches, support videos, or team updates.
- Start a recording from the Chrome extension, open **Effects**, and make sure **Camera Effects** is on.
- Click **Appearance** and adjust the slider to find the setting that suits you.
- If the feature slows down your computer, you can turn it back off at any time.

Sendspark's Touch-Up Appearance feature allows you to improve your on-screen presence by applying real-time touch-up effects to your video feed.
