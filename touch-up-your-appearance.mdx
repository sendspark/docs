Sendspark's Touch-Up Appearance feature allows you to improve your on-screen presence by applying real-time touch-up effects to your video feed.

---

Regardless of the purpose of your video, whether it's a sales pitch, a customer support video, or a team update, Touch-Up Appearance helps you present yourself in the best light.

Let's dive into how you can use this feature to enhance your Sendspark videos:

- Simply click on the Chrome extension to start a recording and once you have your App displayed, click on **Effects** and make sure your **Camera Effects** is on.

![Sendspark recorder popup with arrow pointing to the Effects sparkle icon](/images/8114147/c2ec2de2-image.png)

![Enable Camera Effects prompt with arrow pointing to Turn them on button](/images/8114147/019a12e4-image.png)

To access the **Touch-up Appearance** feature, click on "Appearance." From there, you can easily adjust the slider to find the perfect setting that suits your preferences.

![Appearance](/images/8114147/d0c3e88e-image.png)

If you feel like setting this feature on slows down your computer, you can always turn it back off

![Appearance tab with Touch-up slider and Turn off camera effects link](/images/8114147/e8e764f0-image.png)
