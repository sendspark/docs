LinkedIn does not support videos in comments. Use this workaround with Sendspark to share videos as links with GIF thumbnails.

![Share video in LinkedIn comment](/images/8038588/c94d9052-LinkedIn_Comment.png)

---

Please **note**that GIF previews only show when you post the URL from a mobile device (the LinkedIn mobile app, mobile Chrome, mobile Safari, etc).

## How to Post a Video in a LinkedIn Comment with a Thumbnail

To post your video in a LinkedIn comment, launch the **Share moda**l through your [dashboard](https://app.sendspark.com/) or video edit page.

- Click on **Copy Link** to copy the share URL to your clipboard

![Copy share link for video](/images/8038588/52300345-image.png)

- **Paste** the link in the LinkedIn post**from a mobile device** (LinkedIn mobile app, mobile Chrome,, mobile Safari, etc). The link will automatically unfurl to provide a preview of your video. ![LinkedIn comment section](/images/8038588/8b84827c-image.png)
