By default, you always see newest videos on top in your Sendspark dashboard. However, you can easily reorder videos to see the oldest videos on top.

## How to Reorder Videos

1. Under **Order**, select either **Newest** or **Oldest** to select whether you see Newest or Oldest videos on top ![Order Videos by Date](/images/6299981/9c428db9-image.png)
2. Refresh the page to reset to the default
