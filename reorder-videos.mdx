To reorder your videos, use the **Order** control in your Sendspark dashboard and choose **Newest** or **Oldest** to set which videos appear on top. By default the newest videos are shown first, and refreshing the page returns you to that default.

## Key takeaways

- The dashboard shows your newest videos on top by default.
- Use the **Order** control and select **Newest** or **Oldest** to change which videos appear first.
- Refreshing the page resets the order back to the default (Newest first).

By default, you always see newest videos on top in your Sendspark dashboard. However, you can easily reorder videos to see the oldest videos on top.

## How to Reorder Videos

1. Under **Order**, select either **Newest** or **Oldest** to select whether you see Newest or Oldest videos on top ![Order Videos by Date](/images/6299981/9c428db9-image.png)
2. Refresh the page to reset to the default
