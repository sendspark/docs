Sendspark makes it easy to send personalized videos at scale in La Growth Machine. Record one Dynamic Video, download Sendspark's CSV and import it as an LGM audience, then copy the LGM snippet from Share Campaign and paste it straight into your email step's body. Each contact receives their own AI-personalized video.

## Key takeaways

- You create one Dynamic Video that acts as the template, saying "watermelon" where each viewer's first name should go.
- Download the CSV from your Sendspark campaign and import it into LGM to create an audience.
- When mapping columns on import, either skip the VIDEO LINK column or assign it to a field, then import the leads.
- Get the LGM snippet from Share Campaign in Sendspark and paste it directly into the email step body, add a subject, and attach the audience you created.

## Let's break down the steps to share your video

1) Download the CSV file by clicking on the given button:

![Sendspark interface with CSV download button for Dynamic Video campaign](/images/9389226/7bc5eb5b-image.png)

2) Head to LGM platform and create an audience by importing that CSV file

![La Growth Machine platform showing audience creation with imported CSV file](/images/9389226/974146af-image.png)

![LGM audience import dialog with contact data fields displayed](/images/9389226/90b3a18e-image.png)

3) When asked to map the elements be sure to either skip the VIDEO LINK column or assign it to a field and then hit on IMPORT LEADS

![Column mapping interface for CSV import with VIDEO LINK field option](/images/9389226/558d8571-image.png)

4) Name the Audience and save it:

![LGM audience naming and save confirmation dialog](/images/9389226/05b471b9-image.png)

5) Head back to Campaigns:

![LGM campaigns section with campaign list and navigation menu](/images/9389226/cdfd15c4-image.png)

6) Create a new campaign:

![Create new campaign button in LGM platform interface](/images/9389226/200bd1d1-image.png)

7) Select the sequence option that better fits your needs:

![Sequence type selection options for LGM campaign setup](/images/9389226/be1d842f-image.png)

8) Once in the steps page, click on the email option to deploy the side panel:

![LGM campaign steps page with email option to deploy side panel](/images/9389226/e20fd87a-image.png)

9) Head back to your Dynamic Video campaign and click on Share Campaign to find the snippet code for LGM:

![Sendspark Dynamic Video campaign Share Campaign button location](/images/9389226/c3ab87d3-image.png)

10) Copy the snippet code:

![Snippet code copied from Sendspark Dynamic Video campaign](/images/9389226/683112d8-image.png)

11) Add a Subject, Paste the snippet code straight into the body and save it:

![LGM email composition with subject field and pasted snippet code](/images/9389226/4f007dfe-image.png)

12) Finally, add the audience you created:

![LGM campaign audience assignment with previously created audience selected](/images/9389226/b1ba7fb0-image.png)

13) You are good to go!

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I personalize the video for each contact?">
    Record one video in Sendspark and say "watermelon" where you want each viewer's first name to go. Sendspark uses AI to personalize the video with each contact's name and website.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I paste the snippet into a template or the email body?">
    In La Growth Machine you paste the snippet straight into the email body. In your campaign steps, click the email option to open the side panel, add a subject, and paste the LGM snippet code (copied from Share Campaign in Sendspark) directly into the body, then save.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which CSV do I import into LGM?">
    Import the CSV you download from your Sendspark Dynamic Video campaign. Use it to create your LGM audience.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What do I do with the VIDEO LINK column when mapping fields?">
    When LGM asks you to map the imported columns, either skip the VIDEO LINK column or assign it to a field, then click Import Leads.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
