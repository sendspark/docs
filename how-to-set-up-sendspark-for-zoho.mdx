The Sendspark for Zoho integration sends your Sendspark page visits to Zoho SalesIQ, so you can track video engagement against your contacts and visualize it in dashboards. It's available on every Sendspark plan. To connect it, paste your SalesIQ widget code into Sendspark's integrations tab and save. You'll need an active Zoho SalesIQ account.

## Key takeaways

- The integration sends Sendspark page visits to Zoho so you can track engagement with contacts and visualize video data in dashboards.
- Requirements are an active Sendspark account (the integration is available on every plan) and an active Zoho SalesIQ account.
- To connect, go to your Sendspark integrations tab, click **Connect** next to Zoho, enter your SalesIQ **widget code**, and save.
- Find your widget code in Zoho under **SalesIQ → Live View → Brands → Installation → Website**, in the code block labeled **widgetcode**.

## Integrate Sendspark with Zoho

1. Go to your [Sendspark integrations tab](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations)
2. Next to Zoho, click **Connect**![Sendspark integrations tab with Connect button next to Zoho](/images/5970885/17bbb77c-image.png)
3. Enter your**SalesIQ widget code**![Form field for entering SalesIQ widget code in Sendspark](/images/5970885/fd55cf63-image.png)
4. **Save** changes![Save changes button in Sendspark integration settings panel](/images/5970885/0baae9d1-image.png)
5. You should see that Zoho is **enabled** now 🎉

## Where to find your SalesIQ widget code

1. Navigate to *SalesIQ &gt; Live View&gt; Brands*, choose your brand, or create one.
2. In your brand page, choose your brand, click *Installation &gt; Website*
3. In your block of code, look for your **widgetcode**![Zoho SalesIQ installation code block with widgetcode parameter highlighted](/images/5970885/3f303a0c-image.png)
4. In this example, the widgetcode is **d308fa45f4b6ba3edf6841e3467f57f34ee67fd004bd2a02f10d4649d1e7ff6a**
5. That is the value you want to paste into Sendspark
