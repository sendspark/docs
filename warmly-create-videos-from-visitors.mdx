You can automatically send personalized videos to website visitors by connecting Warmly's Orchestrator to a Sendspark Automated Workflow. When Warmly identifies a contact who visited your site, it fires a webhook to Sendspark, which generates a dynamic video and sends it by email. No manual recording is needed for each lead.

## Key takeaways

- The setup links two tools: a **Warmly Orchestrator** built for the "Identified contact visited website" trigger, and a **Sendspark Automated Workflow** triggered by a webhook.
- Warmly's final workflow action sends a **webhook** to the URL provided by your Sendspark automation, which passes the visitor to Sendspark.
- In Sendspark, add an action to create a [dynamic video](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions) linked to the campaign you want to use, then a follow-up action to send that video by email.
- The whole flow is automated, so identified visitors receive a personalized video without you recording one per person.

You can automatically send personalized videos to anonymous website visitors using Warmly and Sendspark.

## How to Send AI Personalized Videos to Warmly Leads

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/_ek5EhymqUY?rel=0" allow="autoplay; fullscreen; picture-in-picture; encrypted-media" allowFullScreen />

1. Start by set up your Warmly Orchestrator for "Identified contact visited website".

![Warmly Orchestrator workflow setup for identified contact visited website trigger](/images/11382818/94ecc380-image.png)

2. Add an action to send a webhook at the end of the workflow.

![Warmly Orchestrator action panel with webhook configuration option selected](/images/11382818/c1b8c268-image.png)

3. Create an [Automated Workflow](/sendspark-automated-workflows-beginner-s-guide)in Sendspark. Use "Webhook" as a trigger, and add this webhook URL to the Warmly Orchestrator.

![Sendspark automated workflow creation interface with webhook trigger selection](/images/11382818/04e2331d-image.png)

4. Add an action to your Sendspark automation to create a [dynamic video](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions). Link to the Dynamic Video Campaign that you would like to use.

![Sendspark workflow action panel linking to dynamic video campaign](/images/11382818/3f906d90-image.png)

5. Add a follow up action to send this video out in email. Note that there are many ways you can send [videos in email](https://help.sendspark.com/en/collections/12626407-automatically-send-videos) after they are generated.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What triggers the video to be sent?">
    In Warmly, the Orchestrator is set up for the "Identified contact visited website" trigger. When an identified contact visits your site, the workflow runs and, at the end, sends a webhook to Sendspark to kick off the video.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does Warmly connect to Sendspark?">
    Warmly connects through a webhook. You create an Automated Workflow in Sendspark that uses "Webhook" as its trigger, then add the webhook URL Sendspark provides to the action at the end of your Warmly Orchestrator.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which video does each visitor receive?">
    In your Sendspark automation you add an action to create a dynamic video and link it to the Dynamic Video Campaign you want to use. Every identified visitor that comes through the webhook gets a personalized video from that campaign.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is the video delivered to the visitor?">
    After the video is generated, you add a follow-up action to send it out by email. There are several ways to send videos in email once they are generated, so you can choose the method that fits your workflow.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
