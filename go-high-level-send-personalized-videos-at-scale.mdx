This guide shows you how to use Sendspark's Go High Level integration to send personalized videos at scale through your email sequences and marketing campaigns. You can create one video and automatically personalize it for each recipient using dynamic variables.

## Creating Your Personalized Video

1. Create your video in Sendspark by either:
  - Recording a new video using our web-based recorder or Chrome extension
  - Uploading an existing video file
2. Navigate to the **video page editor**
3. Add personalization elements:
  - Add text above the video (e.g., "Hi &#123;first name&#125;" or "Video for &#123;first name&#125;")
  - Add additional text below the video using dynamic variables
  - Include a clear call-to-action telling viewers what to do next

**Note:** The personalization will automatically populate with each viewer's actual first name when they receive the video.

## Sharing Your Video in Go High Level

1. Click the **Share Video** button on your video page
2. Select the appropriate Lead Connector option:
  - **Lead Connector:** Use for sales communications, one-to-one emails, and automated sequences
  - **Lead Connector Marketing:** Use for HTML marketing emails and templated campaigns
3. Copy the generated embed code
4. Paste the code into your Go High Level email or sequence

**Tip:** Each share option generates code optimized for its specific use case, so choose the right one for better email appearance.

## Customizing Your Video Display

Once you've pasted the video into Go High Level:

- The video will automatically pull in the viewer's first name
- You can resize the video if needed to fit your email template
- No additional editing is required

Your personalized videos will now automatically customize for each recipient when sent through your Go High Level campaigns, helping you connect with leads and customers at scale.
