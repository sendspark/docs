To rename a folder in Sendspark, hover over the folder, click the ellipses that appears, and select **Rename**. Type the new name, then click anywhere outside the folder or press **return** to save.

## Key takeaways

- Hover over a folder, click the ellipses, and select **Rename** to edit its name.
- Save the new name by clicking outside the folder or pressing **return**.

Sendspark makes it easy to rename folders. Here's how you can do it:

## How to Rename a Folder

1. **Hover** over the folder you want to rename
2. Click on the **ellipses** that appears
3. Select **Rename**![Rename Folders](/images/6299790/8077c380-image.png)
4. Write your new name![New Folder Name](/images/6299790/138fc9b7-image.png)
5. Click anywhere outside of the folder or type **return** to save
