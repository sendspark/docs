## Recording System Audio in Sendspark

Normally, Sendspark recordings capture:

- **Microphone audio** (your voice).
- **Speaker audio** (if your mic picks it up from the room).

However, this does **not** include the actual **system audio stream** from your computer.

The only way to record system audio in Sendspark is by using the **Chrome Extension** with the **Record Tab** option and enabling **“Share Tab Audio.”**

The **desktop app** and other recording modes cannot capture system audio.

---

## How to Do It

1. [Click here to install the Chrome Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph)
2. Open the Chrome tab that has the audio you want to record (e.g. YouTube, webinar, music).
3. Click the **Sendspark Chrome Extension** icon.
4. Choose **Record Tab** as your source.
5. When prompted, check the box for **Share Tab Audio**.
6. Start recording — Sendspark will capture both the video of the tab and its system audio.

![Sendspark Chrome Extension Record Tab dialog with Share Tab Audio checkbox option](/images/12268069/28c4097a-image.png)

---

## Notes & Limitations

- Works only in **Google Chrome**.
- The **desktop app** does not support system audio recording.
- You can also enable both **microphone + tab audio** if you want to record your voice *and* the system sound together.
