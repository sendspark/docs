To send personalized videos in Marketo, select or record a video in Sendspark, add personalization variables like first name and company, customize your thumbnail, then click the Marketo button to copy the HTML snippet. Paste that snippet into your Marketo email template, where Sendspark automatically maps variables to your Marketo contact fields.

## Key takeaways

- You can create dynamic personalized videos in Sendspark and embed them in Marketo email campaigns that automatically pull contact data.
- Available personalization variables include first name, company name, job title, and virtual backgrounds (depending on your account); always set fallback text so videos look professional when contact data is incomplete.
- Sendspark generates an HTML snippet from the Marketo sharing option that you paste into your Marketo email template.
- Sendspark automatically maps its variables to Marketo fields (for example, Sendspark "first name" → Marketo first name field); send test emails to verify personalization and fallbacks display correctly.

This guide walks you through creating dynamic personalized videos in Sendspark and integrating them with your Marketo email campaigns. You'll learn how to add personalization variables, customize thumbnails, and embed videos that automatically pull contact data from Marketo.

## Prerequisites

- Active Sendspark account
- Access to Marketo email campaigns
- Video content ready to upload or record

## Step 1: Select and Prepare Your Video

1. Navigate to your Sendspark dashboard
2. Choose your video by either:
  - Recording directly from the platform
  - Uploading an existing video file
  - Selecting from your video library
3. Once selected, go to the video page to begin adding personalization

## Step 2: Add Dynamic Personalization Variables

1. Click the **Personalize** button on your video page
2. Select from available variables including:
  - First name
  - Company name
  - Job title
  - Virtual backgrounds (may be available depending on your account)
3. Preview your personalization above the video player
4. Set fallback text (like "Hi there") for contacts missing specific data in Marketo

**Tip:** Always include fallback text to ensure your videos look professional even when contact data is incomplete.

## Step 3: Customize Additional Elements

1. Add personalized text to the video header
2. Include a custom message below your video
3. Create a call-to-action button for next steps such as:
  - Schedule a demo
  - Sign up for a trial
  - Book a consultation

## Step 4: Personalize Your Video Thumbnail

1. Click **Share Video** to see the Marketo preview
2. Click the **Customize** button on the thumbnail
3. Add dynamic personalization to the thumbnail text
4. Use additional editing options to polish your thumbnail design

**Note:** The thumbnail is crucial for email engagement since it's the first thing recipients see. Personalized thumbnails significantly improve click-through rates.

## Step 5: Generate Marketo Integration Code

1. Click the **Marketo** button in the sharing options
2. Copy the provided HTML snippet
3. If you don't see Marketo as an option:
  - Click **Edit**
  - Select Marketo from the integrations list
  - Deselect unused integrations for a cleaner interface

## Step 6: Add Video to Your Marketo Email

1. Open your Marketo email campaign
2. Paste the HTML snippet into your email template
3. Sendspark will automatically map variables between platforms:
  - Sendspark "first name" → Marketo first name field
  - Sendspark "company name" → Marketo company field
  - Additional variables as configured
4. Test your email to ensure personalization displays correctly

**Tip:** Send test emails to contacts with different data completeness levels to verify your fallback text works properly.
