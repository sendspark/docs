Sendspark lets you embed videos directly in HubSpot Marketing Hub emails. Record, request, or upload a video, click Share, then Copy Code Snippet under Email Platforms. In HubSpot, add an HTML block to your email and paste the snippet in. The video plays inside the inbox where supported, and shows a clickable fallback image where it isn't.

## Key takeaways

- The video plays directly in the inbox for viewers whose email client supports embedded video; where it isn't supported, viewers see a fallback image they can click to watch on a landing page.
- Copy the embed code from **Share → Email Platforms → Copy Code Snippet**, then paste it into an **HTML block** in your HubSpot email draft.
- You will see an error about using style tags when you paste. This is expected and fine, the style tags tell Sendspark when to show the embedded video versus the fallback image and link.
- See an up-to-date list of [which email providers support embedded videos in email](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos).

Sendspark has a neat integration to allow you to embed videos directly in Hubspot emails. When you send a video this way in Hubspot, the video will play directly in the inbox for your viewers, as long as it's supported by that email client.

If the video is not supported by the email client, your viewer will see a fallback image that can be clicked to view the video on a landing page. So either way, they have a great experience.

See an up-to-date list of [which email providers support embedded videos in email](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos).

Okay, let's get to it!

## How to embed your video in a Hubspot email 📹

1. Record, request, or upload a video to Sendspark

2. Click "Share"

![Sendspark video share dialog with sharing options and buttons](/images/4408138/10b753ca-image.png)

3. Under Email Platforms, click on "Copy Code Snippet"

![Email Platforms section highlighted with Copy Code Snippet button visible](/images/4408138/91220124-image.png)

4. Go over to Hubspot, and make your email

![Hubspot email editor interface with compose area and formatting options](/images/4408138/1185c977-image.png)

5. Add an HTML block to your Hubspot email draft

![Hubspot email draft showing HTML block insertion menu or option](/images/4408138/9cd7944e-Screen_Recording_2020-09-03_at_09.52_AM.gif)

6. Paste your email embed code into your Hubspot email draft

![Hubspot email editor with embed code pasted into HTML block section](/images/4408138/8d19347e-Screen_Recording_2020-09-03_at_09.54_AM.gif)

Note: you will see an error about using style tags. That's perfectly okay! Those style tags are important for Sendspark to determine when to show the embedded video and when to show the fallback image + link.

## Now send your video email 💌

That's it! Good luck, and let us know how your engagement is with your new video emails!
