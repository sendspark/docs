Sendspark gives agencies a white-label way to create and send personalized video at scale for their clients. You can spin up a separate sub-account per client, remove Sendspark branding, serve videos from your own custom domain, and run AI-personalized video prospecting campaigns across every client from one place.

## Key takeaways

- **Client separation:** create a **sub-account** (or a separate workspace) per client so videos, branding, and analytics stay isolated.
- **White-label:** remove Sendspark branding, set each client's logo and colors, and host videos on a **custom domain** so the experience looks like your agency's (or your client's).
- **Prospecting at scale:** use dynamic, AI-personalized videos to run outbound for clients across email, LinkedIn, and CRM tools.
- **Where to start:** most agencies set up sub-accounts first, then white-label, then wire up their sending/prospecting workflow.
