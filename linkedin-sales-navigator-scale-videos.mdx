## Why Use Videos in LinkedIn Sales Navigator?

Sending a video in LinkedIn Sales Navigator helps you stand out and add a face to the name. Whether you're doing sales outreach or following up with a connection, a short video builds trust faster than text alone.

## 1. Create a Video in Sendspark

Start in Sendspark. You can:

- [Record yourself on camera](/record-yourself)
- [Record your screen](/record-your-screen)
- [Record both camera & screen together](/record-both-your-face-and-screen-together)
- [Create a Dynamic Video Campaign](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions) to create personalized videos in bulk with AI.

Once you've created videos, you'll be able to share videos easily in LinkedIn Sales Nav.

---

**Bonus:** if you have the [Sendspark Chrome Extension](/instant-integration-linkedin)installed, you'll see a Sendspark icon in LinkedIn that makes it easy to create and send videos from right LinkedIn!

![Sendspark Chrome Extension icon visible in LinkedIn interface](/images/11420366/53d31d4a-image.png)

---

## 2. Copy the LinkedIn Video Link

Next to each video, you’ll see a LinkedIn icon. Click it to copy the link.

![LinkedIn icon button next to video in Sendspark dashboard to copy link](/images/11420366/318cc277-image.png)

This version of the link is designed to work well inside LinkedIn messages. It’s short, trackable, and fast to load.

---

## 3. Paste the Link into LinkedIn Sales Navigator

Open a conversation in LinkedIn Sales Navigator and paste the link directly into the message field.

On your end, it’ll just look like a plain link:

![Plain text video link pasted in LinkedIn Sales Navigator message field](/images/11420366/e8f8a565-Screenshot_2025-05-19_at_12_23_32_PM.png)

This is because Sales Navigator keeps a simple UI for you.

But if your prospect accepts your message or accepts your connection request, **they’ll see a clickable preview of the video with a moving thumbnail**.

![Video preview with moving thumbnail displayed in LinkedIn inbox message](/images/11420366/0a87f6bc-image.png)

---

## 4. Track Who’s Watching

When someone opens and watches your video, Sendspark sends you a quick email. You’ll also see more details in your dashboard, like:

- Who watched it
- How long they watched
- Whether they clicked your call to action

![Sendspark dashboard analytics showing video views and engagement metrics](/images/11420366/8a2f05d4-image.png)

This makes follow-ups easier. You know who’s interested and when to respond.

## Pros and Cons of Sending Video in LinkedIn Sales Navigator

### Pros

- Gets more attention than plain text
- Adds a personal touch
- Preview shows in inbox once your message is accepted
- You can track views in real time

### Cons

- Thumbnails only show for accepted messages or connections

---

## When to Use Video in LinkedIn Sales Navigator

- **Outbound sales**: Send quick custom intros to leads
- **Partnerships**: Start conversations with something more personal than text
- **Customer follow-ups**: Use video if email replies are slowing down

---

## Tips to Get More Views

- Mention the person’s name early in the video
- Keep it short—under 60 seconds works best
- Add a quick sentence above the video link, like:“Made this for you—wanted to walk through a quick idea”
- Check Sendspark for view alerts and follow up while the lead is warm
