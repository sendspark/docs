If your test emails show a missing thumbnail or land in spam, the usual cause is your domain's reputation. Domain reputation measures how trustworthy your domain looks to email providers, and it directly affects deliverability. Use a free tool like MXToolbox, EasyDMARC, or MultiRBL to check whether your SPF, DMARC, and DKIM records are set up correctly.

## Key takeaways

- A missing email thumbnail or emails going to spam is usually a signal to review your **domain's reputation**.
- Domain reputation reflects how trustworthy your domain appears to email providers and impacts email deliverability, SEO, and overall credibility.
- Three free tools can check it: [MXToolbox](https://mxtoolbox.com/), [EasyDMARC](https://easydmarc.com/), and [MultiRBL](https://multirbl.valli.org/).
- These tools show whether **SPF, DMARC, or DKIM** records are missing, confirm your SSL certificate status, and reveal whether your domain is on any anti-spam blacklist.
