Did you test your email and it looks like this?

![](/images/10323375/2c5824c5-image.png)

If that is the case, it's time to review your Domain's reputation.

A domain's reputation is a measure of how trustworthy and credible domains appear to email providers, search engines, or other platforms. It impacts email deliverability, SEO rankings, and overall digital credibility.

How do we test this?

There are several free tools online that can provide such information.

1) [https://mxtoolbox.com/](https://mxtoolbox.com/)

![](/images/10323375/1eb5b320-image.png)

2) [https://easydmarc.com/](https://easydmarc.com/)

![](/images/10323375/6b63545c-image.png)

3) [https://multirbl.valli.org/](https://multirbl.valli.org/)

![](/images/10323375/e2f03f5a-image.png)

With these 3 tools, you'll be able to get valuable information about your Domain reputation. Understand if any of the SPF, DMARC, and DKIM keys are missing. Confirm the status of the SSL certificate and even check if your domain has been added to any blacklist Anty Spam archives.
