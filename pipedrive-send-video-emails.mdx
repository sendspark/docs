Sendspark makes it easy to send video emails in Pipedrive, which can boost email engagement by up to 300%. Record your video in Sendspark, click **Share**, then click **Copy Animated Preview** and paste it into a Pipedrive email. Recipients see an animated preview they can click to watch the full video.

## Key takeaways

- Sending videos in Pipedrive can increase email engagement by up to 300%.
- Record your video with Sendspark first.
- Click **Share**, then click **Copy Animated Preview**.
- Paste the animated preview into a Pipedrive email and send.

## How to Send Videos in Pipedrive

Follow these steps to send videos in your Pipedrive emails:

1. [Record your video](/record-yourself) with Sendspark
2. Click **Share** ![Share your video](/images/5697812/f75bd45e-image.png)
3. Click **Copy Animated Preview** ![Copy an image preview to share in email](/images/5697812/e567711f-image.png)
4. **Paste** into an email on Pipedrive ![Paste your video preview in Pipedrive](/images/5697812/4b3d6c5e-image.png)
