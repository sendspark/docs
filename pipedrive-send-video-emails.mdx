## Why Send Videos in Pipedrive

Sending videos in Pipedrive can help you increase email engagement up to 300%. Sendspark makes it easy to record and share videos in Pipedrive. Just [watch the video](https://sendspark.com/share/adr7dvgccz5bgrrw) or follow the steps below!

## How to Send Videos in Pipedrive

Follow these steps to send videos in your Pipedrive emails:

1. [Record your video](/record-yourself) with Sendspark
2. Click **Share** ![Share your video](/images/5697812/f75bd45e-image.png)
3. Click **Copy Animated Preview** ![Copy an image preview to share in email](/images/5697812/e567711f-image.png)
4. **Paste** into an email on Pipedrive ![Paste your video preview in Pipedrive](/images/5697812/4b3d6c5e-image.png)
