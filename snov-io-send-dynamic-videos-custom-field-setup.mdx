To send dynamic videos through Snov.io, first create a custom text field called "email2" in Snov.io, since Snov.io needs a unique email field to connect with Sendspark. Then create your campaign in Sendspark, share it to Snov.io as "Email Only," paste the snippet into your Snov.io email template, and upload a prospect list that includes the matching "email2" column.

## Key takeaways

- Snov.io requires a unique email field to connect with Sendspark, so add a custom text field titled "email2" in the Leads app under Manage Custom Fields.
- In Sendspark, go to Share Campaign, select Snov.io, choose "Email Only," and copy the snippet code into your Snov.io email template.
- Your uploaded prospect list must include an additional email column titled "email2" that exactly matches the custom field.
- Map both the "email" and "email2" columns correctly when uploading in Snov.io.
- Use Preview and Test under the email step to review each contact's dynamic elements like personalized backgrounds and voice cloning before sending.

## In this article, I'll guide you through sharing a dynamic video campaign using Snov.io. Let’s jump right into it.

## Step 1: Set Up a Custom Email Field in Snov.io

1. **Open Snov.io** and navigate to the Leads app.

2. **Access Custom Fields** by clicking on the three dots and selecting "Manage Custom Fields."

3. **Create a Unique Email Field:**

- Since Snov.io requires a unique email field to connect with Sendspark, add a new text field titled **"email2"**. This field is essential for linking the correct video with the right recipient.

4. **Save** this custom field configuration.

## Step 2: Create a Campaign in Sendspark

1. Go to Sendspark and **Create Your Campaign**. If this is your first time, check the link below for guidance on creating a campaign from scratch.

2. In Sendspark, go to **Share Campaign** and select Snov.io.

3. **Choose "Email Only"** and copy the provided snippet code.

## Step 3: Set Up Your Campaign in Snov.io

1. **Start a New Campaign** in Snov.io.

2. **Paste the Snippet Code:** Paste the copied snippet into the email template.

3. **Add Subject Line** and any required information before saving.

## Step 4: Upload Your Prospect List

1. In the Prospects section, **Create a New List** (e.g., “Test”).

2. **Upload Your Prospect List:**

- Format the file with an additional email column titled **"email2"** (exactly matching your custom field).

3. **Map Your Fields:** Ensure both "email" and "email2" columns are correctly mapped in Snov.io.

## Step 5: Preview and Test

1. After uploading, go to **Sequences** in Snov.io and select **Preview and Test** under the email step.

2. **Review Each Contact Preview** with dynamic elements like personalized backgrounds and voice cloning.

**That's it! Let us know if you have any questions.**
