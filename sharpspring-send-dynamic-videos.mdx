## How to Send AI Personalized Videos in Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (SharpSpring)

## 1. Create AI Personalized Videos in Sendspark

If you have not done that yet, please start with [this article](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions)!

## 2. Click "Share Campaign"

![Purple Share Campaign button in Sendspark](/images/11139044/5991c308-image.png)

## 3. Copy the Snippet for Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (SharpSpring)

![Share Dynamic Video search matching Constant Contact Lead Gen and CRM Sharpspring](/images/11139044/d909e745-image.png)

## 4. Paste in an HTML block in your email

![Sendspark video snippet in an HTML block within SharpSpring email editor](/images/11139044/493fd932-image.png)

## 5. Preview & Send!

You can test your email by clicking "Send a Preview Mail," and sending a sample email with someone who is on your Sendspark video list.

![Check and Preview dropdown showing Send a Preview Mail option](/images/11139044/7b9177a5-image.png)

If you don't see the Sendspark AI video, it might because you're testing a video for someone who you have not made a video for. Please make sure their email address is also on your dynamic video list!

![Sendspark generated videos list showing prospect emails for the campaign](/images/11139044/c8bcc2e8-image.png)
