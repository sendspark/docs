To send AI personalized videos in Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (SharpSpring), first create your dynamic video campaign in Sendspark. Then click **Share Campaign**, copy the SharpSpring snippet, and paste it into an HTML block in your email. Preview it with someone on your video list, then send.

## Key takeaways

- Start by creating your AI personalized videos in Sendspark before sharing — see [how to make dynamic videos](/dynamic-videos-make-videos-with-ai-introductions) if you haven't yet.
- Share by clicking **Share Campaign**, copying the snippet for Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (SharpSpring), and pasting it into an HTML block in your email.
- Test with **Send a Preview Mail** to someone who is on your Sendspark video list.
- If the video doesn't appear in the preview, the recipient's email address is probably not on your dynamic video list — only contacts you generated a video for will see one.
