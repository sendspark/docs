To send personalized videos at scale in Mailshake, record or upload your video in Sendspark, customize the header, message, and thumbnail with placeholders, then click **Share Video** and select **Mailshake**. Paste the snippet into a Mailshake email draft, preview, and send. Each recipient's video personalizes automatically with their first name, company name, and job title.

## Key takeaways

- Personalize the **video header**, **message**, and **thumbnail** with placeholders that pull contact info from Mailshake.
- Share via **Share Video → Mailshake**; if Mailshake isn't listed, [modify your share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal).
- Paste the snippet into a Mailshake email draft, then preview and send.
- Videos auto-personalize per recipient with first name, company name, and job title.

6. Preview and send!

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What personalizes automatically in Mailshake?">
    Sendspark automatically pulls in your viewer's first name, company name, and job title using placeholders you add to the video header, message, and thumbnail.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I don't see Mailshake in the Share Video options — what do I do?">
    Follow the steps to [modify your share modal](/how-to-modify-shortcuts-on-share-modal) to add Mailshake as a share shortcut.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where do I paste the video share snippet?">
    Paste it directly into a Mailshake email draft, then preview and send. Your video personalizes for each recipient automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is this different from sending dynamic videos in Mailshake?">
    This flow uses placeholders to personalize the text and thumbnail around one recording. If you want a uniquely generated video per contact, see [Mailshake — Send Dynamic Videos](/mailshake-send-dynamic-videos).
  </Accordion>
