Sendspark lets you record your camera, your screen, or both at once using the Chrome Extension or Desktop App, then share the video by email as a linked GIF. Install the recorder, set your preferences, hit record, then edit and share, all while tracking who watches from your video library.

## Key takeaways

- The Chrome Extension is the easiest way to record yourself, your screen, or both, and it comes with pre-built integrations for Gmail, LinkedIn, Outreach, HubSpot, and more.
- The Desktop App adds the ability to record over any app on your computer, better audio/video sync, and higher quality videos.
- You can record three types of video: Camera Only, Screen Only, or Cam + Screen.
- The Linked GIF option (a link plus a GIF thumbnail) is the best way to share a video via email, while Copy Link is best for LinkedIn, Slack, and almost anywhere except email.

## How do I install Sendspark?

To start recording videos, please install our Chrome Extension. This is the easiest way to record yourself, your screen, or both at the same time.

**👉 [Install the Sendspark Chrome Extension here](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph) 👈**

The Sendspark Desktop App offers several advantages over the Chrome Extension, such as the ability to record over any app on your computer, better audio/video sync, and higher quality videos.

However, the Chrome Extension can be faster to use, and comes with pre-built integrations with Gmail, LinkedIn, Outreach, Hubspot and more. So, if you don't mind having two applications, we recommend installing both!

**👉 [Install the Sendspark Desktop App here](https://www.sendspark.com/download-desktop-application) 👈**

## Record & Share Videos

This tutorial will cover how to record and share videos using the Sendspark Chrome Extension. If you are using the Desktop app, please refer to this article: [How to Use the Sendspark Desktop App](/how-to-use-the-sendspark-desktop-application)

## 1. Launch the Recorder

You can launch Sendspark anytime from your Chrome browser. Simply click on the purple Sendspark icon on the top right of your browser to open the recorder.

![Chrome browser toolbar with purple Sendspark extension icon highlighted](/images/8458228/6d2a88b4-image.png)

If you don't see the Sendspark icon on the top of your Chrome browser, you might not have it pinned!

Pin the Sendspark extension by **clicking on the Chrome puzzle piece** icon on the top right of your browser, and then **select the pin icon next to Sendspark**. This will pin Sendspark so it's easy to launch in the future.

![Chrome extensions menu with puzzle piece icon and Sendspark pin option](/images/8458228/7cf4778a-image.png)

(If you don't see Sendspark there at all, you might not have the Chrome Extension installed. You can install the Sendspark Chrome Extension [install the Sendspark Chrome extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/sendspark-video-and-scree/blimjkpadkhcpmkeboeknjcmiaogbkph)).

## 2. Set Preferences

You can record three types of videos on Sendspark:

![](/images/8458228/91b498b9-image.png)

- **Camera Only** — Records just your face (your webcam). When this recording option is selected, you will not capture anything on your screen.
- **Screen Only**— Records just what is on your screen (not your webcam). When this option is selected, you will only record your screen, and not yourself.
- **Cam + Screen** — Records both yourself and your screen. When this option is selected, you will record yourself in a small bubble over your screen.

When you have **"Both"**selected, you will also see a small menu over your camera bubble that lets you customize more options:

![Camera bubble customization menu with size, shape, and position controls](/images/8458228/26ef2c73-image.png)

- **Change Size** — Lets you change the size of your camera bubble.
- **Change Shape** — Lets you change the shape of your camera bubble. Circle, Square, and Rectangle are all supported.
- **Flip Camera**— Lets you mirror your camera, so you can record either the regular or mirrored version of yourself.
- **Spotlight**— Enlarges your camera so it takes center space. This can be useful when you want to start your video with you as the main focus, and then switch to the content of your screen.

You can also use the **"Settings"** gear to adjust more preferences.

![](/images/8458228/b33c5c87-extension.gif)

- **Change Camera Source** — Lets you change the webcam to capture the video.
- **Change Microphone Source**— Lets you change the microphone to record your voice.
- **Countdown (on/off)**— Lets you enable or disable a 3-second countdown before the recording starts.
- **Microphone (on/off)**— Lets you enable/disable sound, so you can record a silent video.
- **Toolbar (on/off)**— Lets you enable/disable the toolbar, so you can record a video without showing the toolbar on screen. (If you disable the toolbar, you can click on the Sendspark extension on the top right of your browser to stop recording).
- **Toolbar Visibility (50% / 100%)** — Lets you decide to show the full toolbar, or keep it at 50% opacity so it doesn't stand out as much.
- **Camera Spotlight (circle / fullscreen)**— Lets you decide if your camera spotlight will show up full screen (over your entire video) or simply in a circle over your screen.
- **Camera Effects (on/off)** — Lets you enable / disable camera effects, to use virtual backgrounds or touch up your appearance.
- **Reload Extension**— Lets you force a reload to fix a bug or get an updated version.

## 3. Start Recording

Once you are all set with your recording preferences, simply click "Record" to get started making your video!

If you are recording your screen, you will see a popup to choose what you want to record from your screen:

![Screen recording selection dialog with Chrome Tab, Window, Entire Screen options](/images/8458228/b8726b09-image.png)

- **Chrome Tab** — Captures just what is on a single tab. This can be the "cleanest" recording option, because you are recording just the content of your screen, and not anything else on your screen. The limit of this option is if you switch between multiple tabs or applications while recording, only the original Chrome tab will be recorded.
- **Window** — Will let you record everything within your Chrome instance or another application. This is the best option if you plan to record multiple chrome tabs.
- **Entire Screen** — Will record everything on your computer. This is the best option if you want to record multiple applications in one video.

Remember, the only way to record your camera bubble over non-Chrome applications (PowerPoint, PDFs, etc.) is to use the Sendspark Desktop App. [Install the desktop app here](https://sendspark.com/download-desktop-application).

## 4. Use the Recording Toolbar

Once you are recording, you will be able to use the Sendspark toolbar to control your video. You have several options.

![](/images/8458228/82fe69d0-image.png)

- **Delete Video** — Lets you trash your video if you don't like it
- **Restart Recording**— Lets you restart from the beginning
- **Pause / Resume**— Lets you pause your video until you're ready to resume recording
- **Stop Recording**— Lets you complete recording so you can edit and share the video.

Note, if you're NOT using the recording toolbar, you can end the recording by **clicking on the Sendspark icon** in the top right of your browser.

## 5. Edit & Share Video

Once you've completed recording your video, you will see it pop up in a preview window over your screen. Here, you have several options to quickly edit and share your video.

![Video preview window with Download, Delete, Restart, and Edit options](/images/8458228/e1c6ace7-image.png)

- **Download**— Will download your video to your desktop.
- **Delete**— Will delete your video immediately.
- **Restart**— Will delete this video and set you up to record a new one
- **Select a Template**— Will let you select from your [template library](https://sendspark.com/templates) to instantly add page text and a call-to-action to your video
- **Add Video** — Will let you append an existing video to the one you just recorded. This is a great option if you want to record a lot of similar videos quickly... You can make one "core video" first, and then record multiple custom intros. Then, just click "add video" to add your core video to the custom introduction.
- **Edit Page / Thumbnail** — Will open your video in the edit page in Sendspark, where you can add more customization to the video before sharing.
- **Trim**— Lets you trim the beginning or end of your video.
- **Copy Link** — Copies the video share link to your clipboard so you can easily share it with anyone. This is the best way to share videos in LinkedIn messages, Slack, or pretty much anywhere *except* email

There are also more share options:

![Share options menu showing Linked GIF, Download, and Insert buttons](/images/8458228/f76de98c-image.png)

- **Linked GIF**— Copies a link + a GIF thumbnail to your clipboard. Then, you can just paste it in an email draft. This is the best way to share your video via email.
- **Download Video**— Will download the MP4 version of your video to your desktop
- **Insert (for Instant Integrations)** — If you are recording over a page where we have an instant integration, you will see an option to insert your video directly into your email or LinkedIn message.

## How do I manage videos and view analytics?

After you record your videos, you can manage them in your Sendspark library.

Here, you have a private library, [my library](https://sendspark.com/my-library), for videos that you make. These are private to you until shared with others.

You also have a [team library](https://sendspark.com/team-library) that you can use to share videos with other people in your workspace. Everyone in the workspace has access to all videos in the team library.

Feel free to organize both with folders so it's easy to find and re-use videos!

You can click on a video anytime to edit it or share it. You can also [view analytics](/how-to-understand-video-analytics) for each video.

Here, if the information is known, you will see who has watched your video, what percentage of the videos they've watched, and what other actions they've taken"

![Video analytics dashboard displaying viewer engagement and watch statistics](/images/8458228/a369d9a9-image.png)

## What advanced skills can help me scale?

These are some advanced tools that Sendspark has, to make it faster and easier for you to scale yourself with video.

## 1. Instant Integrations

As long as you have the Sendspark Chrome Extension installed, you will see shortcuts to record videos right from common messaging platforms: LinkedIn, Gmail, Outreach, Hubspot, and more. [Learn more about Instant Integrations](/sendspark-instant-integrations-how-they-work).

![Instant Integration shortcuts visible in Gmail, LinkedIn, or Outreach interface](/images/8458228/3d143575-image.png)

## 2. Templates

Templates make it easy to add text and calls-to-action to videos after recording, without any typing. You can create and [manage templates](https://sendspark.com/templates) from the templates section of Sendspark. [Learn more about Templates](/how-to-use-templates)

![Templates library showing pre-built video page designs and customization options](/images/8458228/a3262925-image.png)

## 3. Combined Videos

Combined videos are a great way to personalize your outreach at scale. Record personalized introductions for each viewer, and combine the personalized video with an existing video to move fast. [Learn more about combined videos.](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale)

![Combined videos feature demonstrating personalized intro joined with core content](/images/8458228/c682d35c-image.png)

## 4. Personalized Variables

Use personalization variables to automatically add your viewer's name, company, or job title to your video page or video thumbnail. This way, you can just record one video, and auto-personalize it for each viewer. [Learn more about personalization at scale](/use-merge-tags-to-personalize-videos-at-scale).

![Personalized variables panel with name, company, and job title fields](/images/8458228/55571f0c-image.png)

## 5. Dynamic Videos

Dynamic videos let you do next-level personalization. In addition to using personalized variables, you can also use [AI Intros](/ai-voice-cloning-how-to-get-the-best-results) to automatically add your viewer's first name to the video, and [dynamic backgrounds](/how-to-make-videos-with-dynamic-backgrounds) to automatically put their website behind you, like you're recording over their website or [LinkedIn profile](/how-to-make-dynamic-videos-with-linkedin-profiles).

This is the best way to make thousands of personalized videos in minutes for sales prospecting.

![Dynamic video settings showing AI Intros and dynamic background features](/images/8458228/8c8a185c-image.png)
