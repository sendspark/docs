## 📤 How to Know if Your LinkedIn Outreach Platform Works with Sendspark Video Campaigns

A common question we get is:

**“Do you integrate with [X platform] that lets me share my dynamic video campaigns via LinkedIn messages?”**

## ✅ The Short Answer:

Yes, we integrate with several platforms that support LinkedIn messaging — including **native integration with Expandi** — but the real key is this:

**If the platform lets you upload a CSV file and map a “video link” to a dynamic placeholder in a message, then it’s compatible with Sendspark.**

## 🔄 Platforms That Work

There are plenty of outreach tools that support video messages in LinkedIn, such as:

- **Expandi** (native integration)
- **Lemlist**
- **The Growth Machine**

These platforms let you upload CSVs and dynamically insert personalized video links into LinkedIn messages.

---

## 🛠️ How to Test if Your Platform is Compatible

Here’s a simple way to verify if your tool works with Sendspark:

1. **Generate a Dynamic Video Campaign** in Sendspark.
2. **Download the CSV file** from your campaign.
3. Open your outreach platform (e.g., Expandi, Lemlist, etc.).
4. **Upload the CSV** and look for the ability to:
  - Map fields like `first_name`
  - **Map the `video_link` field to a dynamic placeholder**

If you can do that — you're good to go!

⚠️ Note: Some tools like **Dripify** (as of now) **do not support** CSV uploads with dynamic placeholders. That means you can’t use dynamic video campaigns with those tools unless that feature is added.

---

## 🔗 Using Video Links in LinkedIn Messages

Even without full platform integration, you can **manually copy-paste** the video link into a LinkedIn message. LinkedIn will **automatically generate a thumbnail preview**, just like with any regular link.

## Pro Tips for LinkedIn:

- Don’t send video links in your **first message** to a cold lead — it may get flagged or ignored.
- Always test how the link appears before launching your full campaign.

- Sendspark works with **any outreach tool** that allows CSV upload + dynamic field mapping.
- Look for the ability to map a `video_link` in your platform.
- For manual use, just **copy the link** and paste it into LinkedIn — a thumbnail will appear.
