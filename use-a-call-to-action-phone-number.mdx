---
title: "Use a Call-to-Action (Phone Number)"
description: "Add a phone number call-to-action button to your Sendspark videos. Configure click-to-call CTAs so viewers can call you directly from the video page."
---

**Note**: you must be on the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or a higher plan to use video CTAs (Call-To-Action)

---

Follow these steps to add a "Call Me" button to your videos

- **Toggle** on the Call-to-Action field

![Call to Action](/images/5566693/6f97cc32-image.png)

- Write your desired **button text**(ex: Call Me)
- Write**tel:** and **your phone number** where it says **URL**(ex: tel:1234567890)

![Call to Action display](/images/5566692/3a376c77-image.png)

You'll be able to check that everything looks good in the preview area on the right of your page 👍
