To add a click-to-call button to your video, toggle on the Call-to-Action field, set the button text (such as "Call Me"), and in the URL field enter **tel:** followed by your phone number (for example, `tel:1234567890`). Viewers can then tap the button to call you directly. This requires the Starter Plan or higher.

## Key takeaways

- Video CTAs require the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or higher.
- Toggle on the Call-to-Action field, then set your button text (for example, "Call Me").
- In the URL field, enter **tel:** followed by your phone number, like `tel:1234567890`.
- Check the preview area on the right of the page to confirm the button looks correct before sharing.

**Note**: you must be on the [Starter Plan](https://www.sendspark.com/pricing) or a higher plan to use video CTAs (Call-To-Action)

## How do I format the phone number for a click-to-call button?

Enter the number in the URL field as **tel:** immediately followed by the digits, with no spaces, like `tel:1234567890`. The `tel:` prefix is what tells the viewer's device to place a call when they tap the button.
