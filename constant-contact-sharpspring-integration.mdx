Sendspark integrates with Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (formerly SharpSpring) in three ways: send AI-personalized videos in email by pasting a Share Campaign snippet into your email body, embed your SharpSpring calendar next to your video, and add your LG&CRM website tracking snippet so video-page visitors are tracked like any other page.

## Key takeaways

- There are three integration paths: sending AI-personalized videos in email, embedding your SharpSpring calendar on video pages, and adding LG&CRM website tracking.
- To send personalized videos, generate dynamic videos in Sendspark first, then use **Share Campaign → Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (SharpSpring)** and paste the snippet into your email body.
- Always test with real contacts before sending personalized videos out at scale.
- Website tracking is connected from your [Integrations Settings page](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations) by pasting your LG&CRM tracking ID under "LG&CRM Website Tracking."

Sendspark integrates with Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (formerly SharpSpring) in a few ways:

- Send AI-Personalized Videos in Email
- Embed SharpSpring Calendar on Video Pages
- Use LG&CRM Website Tracking

## I. Send AI-Personalized Videos in Email

To send AI-personalized videos in Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (SharpSpring) emails, first generate dynamic videos in Sendspark.

If you do not know how to create dynamic videos, please read this article first.

After your videos generate, follow these steps to share them in Constant Contact LG & CRM.

1. Click **"Share Campaign" ​**![Purple Share Campaign button beside the generated prospect list](/images/10254493/4f60dcd2-image.png)
2. Select **"Constant Contact Lead Gen & CRM (SharpSpring) Integration"**
3. Then, head to Constant Contact, and paste the snippet in the body of your email where you would like the video to go.
4. Test this with real contacts before sending it out at scale.

## II. Embed SharpSpring Calendar

To embed your SharpSpring calendar next to your video, simply follow these steps:

1. In the calendar section on your video page, select "**SharpSpring**"
2. Paste the link to your calendar
3. You'll know it's working when you see your calendar appear in the preview area underneath your video!

![Share page editor with SharpSpring calendar selected and embedded in the preview](/images/10254493/6c498732-image.png)

## III. LG&CRM Website Tracking

Want to track folks who visit your Sendspark video pages the same way you track people who visit other website pages?

You can add your LG&CRM website tracking snippet to Sendspark.

1. Just head to your [Integrations Settings page](https://sendspark.com/settings/integrations)
2. Under**"LG&CRM Website Tracking,"**click **"Connect"** ​![LG&CRM Website Tracking row showing Disabled status and a Connect button](/images/10254493/b4eefc6c-image.png)
3. Paste your tracking ID and click **"Save"** ​![Tracking ID input field with Cancel and Save buttons](/images/10254493/4aa42507-image.png)
4. And you'll see that the integration is enabled! ​![LG&CRM Website Tracking row now showing Enabled status and an Edit button](/images/10254493/451a1329-image.png) ​
