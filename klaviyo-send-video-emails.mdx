With Sendspark, you can embed a video in a Klaviyo email so it plays right in the recipient's inbox (where their email client supports video). In Klaviyo, create an email template, add a text block, and edit its **Source**. Then in Sendspark click **Share Video → Embed Video → Copy Code**, paste the code into the Klaviyo source, and save.

## Key takeaways

- Sendspark lets you embed videos in Klaviyo so they play in-email, as long as the recipient's [email client supports video](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos).
- Where video isn't supported (like Gmail and Outlook), viewers see a preview image and can click to watch in their browser, so the experience stays intact across clients.
- The workflow: create a Klaviyo email template, add a text block, edit its **Source**, then paste Sendspark's embed code and save.
- You get the embed code in Sendspark via **Share Video → Embed Video → Copy Code**.

With Sendspark, you can easily embed videos in Klaviyo so they play for viewers right in their email, as long as their [email client supports videos.](https://blog.sendspark.com/which-email-clients-support-embedded-videos)

## What happens if video is not supported in an email client?

Sendspark provides a great fallback option for when the video is not able to play in email. In email clients such as Gmail and Outlook, where video tags are not supported, your viewers will see a preview image of the video, and be able to click to watch it in their browser.

By following the steps below, you can easily embed videos in Klaviyo emails, and feel comfortable that your recipients will have great viewing experiences across all email clients and devices.

## How to embed videos in Klaviyo with Sendspark:

1. Make a **new email template** in Klaviyo

![Klaviyo email template creation screen with new template option](/images/4430976/86afe7b3-image.png)

2. Add a**text block** to your email

![Klaviyo email editor showing text block insertion into template](/images/4430976/d0f21820-Screen_Recording_2020-09-10_at_10.42_AM.gif)

3. Click to edit the **Source**

![Klaviyo text block source code editing panel open](/images/4430976/9ab1da0d-image.png)

4. **Make your video** in Sendspark. Either record, upload, or request a video in the platform to send out.

![Sendspark video creation interface with record upload and request options](/images/4430976/de32b6b7-image.png)

5. Click **Share Video**, then under Embed Video, **Copy Code**

![Sendspark share video dialog with embed code copy button highlighted](/images/4430976/a5c9695f-image.png)

6. **Paste** your code into the Source section in Klaviyo and click **save**

![Klaviyo source editor with pasted embed code and save button](/images/4430976/7a403571-Screen_Recording_2020-09-10_at_10.44_AM.gif)

7. You're all ready to send your video email in Klaviyo!
