Dynamic video campaign limits depend on your plan. On the Solo plan, each campaign allows up to 4,000 leads or videos, with a maximum of 2,000 videos per batch. On the Growth and Team plans, each campaign allows up to 10,000 leads or videos. Limits apply per campaign and per batch.

This article will let you understand how many leads you can upload per batch and per campaign.

Solo plan.

Each dynamic campaign allows you to generate a maximum of 4,000 Leads or videos. And you can generate a maximum of 2,000 videos per batch, which means that you can do two batches of 2,000 videos per campaign.

Growth Plan:

Each dynamic campaign allows you to generate a maximum of 10,000 Leads or videos. And you can generate a maximum of 100,000 videos per batch, which means that you can do two batches of 2,000 videos per campaign.

Team Plan:

Each dynamic campaign allows you to generate a maximum of 10,000 Leads or videos. And you can generate a maximum of 100,000 videos per batch, which means that you can do two batches of 2,000 videos per campaign.

## Key takeaways

- Limits apply two ways: a per-campaign cap on total leads/videos, and a per-batch cap on how many videos you can generate at once.
- **Solo plan:** up to 4,000 leads or videos per campaign, with a maximum of 2,000 videos per batch (two batches of 2,000 per campaign).
- **Growth plan:** up to 10,000 leads or videos per campaign.
- **Team plan:** up to 10,000 leads or videos per campaign.
