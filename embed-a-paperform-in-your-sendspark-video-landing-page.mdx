You can easily embed a **Paperform** directly inside your **Sendspark dynamic video landing page**.

This allows viewers to **fill out a form while watching your video**, which is great for collecting leads, booking demos, or qualifying prospects.

---

## ✨ How It Works

When someone opens your Sendspark video page, the **Paperform appears right below the video** so viewers can interact with it immediately.

This is perfect for:

✨ Demo request forms  
✨ Lead qualification  
✨ Customer onboarding  
✨ Surveys or feedback

---

## ⚙️ How to Embed a Paperform

Follow these steps:

## 1️⃣ Open Your Dynamic Video Campaign

Go to your **Dynamic Video Campaign** and navigate to the **Landing Page step**.

---

## 2️⃣ Select the Embed Option

Look for the **Calendar Embed option**.

Click it to open the available embed integrations.

---

## 3️⃣ Choose Paperform

From the dropdown menu, select **Paperform**.

---

## 4️⃣ Copy Your Paperform Link

Open **Paperform** and either create a new form or open an existing one.

Click **Share** and copy the **shareable form link**.

💡 Tip: Keep your form **short and simple** so it displays nicely inside the Sendspark page.

---

## 5️⃣ Paste the Link in Sendspark

Paste the Paperform link into the **embed field inside Sendspark**.

---

## 6️⃣ Generate Your Videos

Once your campaign generates videos, the **Paperform will automatically appear on the landing page** under the video.

Your viewers can now fill out the form directly while watching.

---

## 💡 Best Practices

✔ Keep the form short (3–5 questions works best)  
✔ Ask for essential information only  
✔ Use it for demo requests or lead capture  
✔ Test your landing page before sending campaigns
