Connecting takes about a minute: add Sendspark as a custom (remote) MCP connector in your assistant, then sign in to approve access.

## Before you start

- A Sendspark account on the **Growth, Business, or Scale** plan.
- The Sendspark MCP URL:

```text
https://apiv2.sendspark.com/api/mcp
```

## Add the connector

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your assistant's connector settings">
    In your AI assistant, go to where custom or remote MCP connectors are added (see the per-client guides below).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a custom remote connector">
    Create a new connector and paste the Sendspark MCP URL above. Give it a name like "Sendspark".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to Sendspark">
    Your assistant opens a Sendspark sign-in page. Log in and approve access. There are no keys or tokens to copy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify the connection">
    Ask the assistant a read-only question such as "Which Sendspark workspaces can I access?" — it should call `list_workspaces` and return your workspaces.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Warning>

## Remove or re-authenticate

The connection stays linked to your account until you remove it in your assistant's connector settings. Remove and re-add the connector to sign in again.

## Support and privacy

- **Trouble connecting or a question about access?** Email [support@sendspark.com](mailto:support@sendspark.com).
- **How your data is handled:** the connection is read-only and scoped to your workspaces. See the Sendspark [Privacy Policy](https://www.sendspark.com/privacy) for details.
