The Sendspark MCP server lets AI assistants that support the Model Context Protocol — Claude, ChatGPT, Mistral Le Chat, Perplexity, Grok — read your Dynamic Videos, prospects, and analytics in plain language, directly from your assistant.

## Key takeaways

- Add one remote connector URL and sign in — no API keys to copy.
- The connection is **read-only**: assistants can look things up but cannot create, edit, or delete.
- Requires a Sendspark account on the **Growth, Business, or Scale** plan.

## Connection URL

```
https://apiv2.sendspark.com/api/mcp
```

Add this as a custom (remote) MCP connector in your AI assistant. Your assistant opens a Sendspark sign-in page — log in and approve access, and the connection stays linked to your account until you remove it.

## Requirements

- A Sendspark account on the **Growth, Business, or Scale** plan (Free and trial plans don't include MCP access).
- An AI assistant that supports custom remote MCP connectors.

## What your assistant can access

The connection only sees data in workspaces you belong to that are active:

- **Workspaces** — the workspaces you're a member of and your role in each.
- **Dynamic Video campaigns** — campaign name, status, settings, and share-page setup.
- **Prospects** — name, email, company, job title, status, and (when complete) share/embed links and engagement stats.
- **Analytics** — visits, plays, CTA clicks, thumbs-up, and a per-visitor breakdown (location, device, watch %).
