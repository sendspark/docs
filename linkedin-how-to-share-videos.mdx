To share a Sendspark video on LinkedIn, open the Share modal from your video edit page or dashboard, click Copy Link, then paste the link into your LinkedIn message. LinkedIn automatically unfurls the link into a video preview. If you'd rather record and share without leaving LinkedIn, use the LinkedIn Video Integration.

## Key takeaways

- Launch the Share modal from your video edit page or dashboard and click Copy Link.
- Paste the link into a LinkedIn message and it automatically unfurls into a video preview.
- Sendspark also offers a LinkedIn Video Integration to record and share videos entirely on LinkedIn.

This article shows how to copy & paste videos on LinkedIn from Sendspark.

Please note, we have a [LinkedIn Video Integration](/instant-integration-linkedin) to record and share videos entirely on LinkedIn, without ever leaving the page.

---

## To copy and paste your video in LinkedIn...

- From your video edit page or [dashboard](https://app.sendspark.com/), launch the **Share modal**
- Click **Copy Link**

![Share Link button](/images/5548720/deb60371-image.png)

- **Paste** the link in your LinkedIn message. The link will automatically unfurl to provide a preview of your video.

![Linkedin example](/images/5548720/dadcb20e-image.png)
