This article shows how to copy & paste videos on LinkedIn from Sendspark.

Please note, we have a [LinkedIn Video Integration](/instant-integration-linkedin) to record and share videos entirely on LinkedIn, without ever leaving the page.

---

## To copy and paste your video in LinkedIn...

- From your video edit page or [dashboard](https://app.sendspark.com/), launch the **Share modal**
- Click **Copy Link**

![Share Link button](/images/5548720/deb60371-image.png)

- **Paste** the link in your LinkedIn message. The link will automatically unfurl to provide a preview of your video.

![Linkedin example](/images/5548720/dadcb20e-image.png)
